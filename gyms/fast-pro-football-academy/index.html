Overview

FAST PRO Football Academy is one of Pattaya's most credentialed youth football academies — a UEFA-A licensed European football coach-led program offering professional European-style football training to children ages 4-15. The academy distinguishes itself from local Thai-led football programs by importing European coaching methodology directly through a UEFA-A qualified head coach — the second-highest UEFA coaching qualification, the same level required to coach top European professional youth academies.

The academy operates from two Pattaya locations: Horseshoe Point Pattaya (the 1,500-acre equestrian and adventure resort east of Pattaya near Mabprachan Reservoir) and IP Soccer Club Pattaya. The dual-location model gives families schedule flexibility — sessions can be booked at the location more convenient to home / school.

The academy's stated mission goes beyond pure football: "to develop young players not only as footballers but as confident, disciplined and motivated individuals through professional European-style football training in a safe, positive and structured environment." This character-and-values approach alongside technical football skill development appeals to parents seeking holistic athletic development for their children.

For Pattaya expat families and Thai families seeking the most credentialed European-style youth football education available in the city — and especially for parents whose children show football promise warranting investment — FAST PRO is among the top-tier options alongside the newer Liverpool FC International Academy Pattaya (announced 2026).

UEFA-A Licensed Coaching — What It Means

UEFA Coaching License Hierarchy

The Union of European Football Associations (UEFA) operates a globally recognized coaching qualification ladder:

  • UEFA C — entry-level, grassroots youth coaching
  • UEFA B — competent youth + adult amateur coaching
  • UEFA A — professional youth and senior amateur, top-tier youth academies
  • UEFA Pro — top-flight professional teams (Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga first teams)

Most football academies globally — even premium-positioned ones — operate with UEFA B or below coaching. UEFA-A licensed coaching is genuinely premium tier — the same level taught at top European youth academies (Manchester United Academy, Ajax Youth Academy, Barcelona La Masia youth structure, etc.).

What UEFA-A Coaches Bring

UEFA-A licensed coaches have completed:

  • 150+ hours of coursework across football technique, tactics, sports science, psychology
  • Field coaching practical assessments
  • Pedagogical training for age-appropriate instruction
  • Sports medicine awareness
  • Long-term athlete development frameworks
  • Talent identification training

For young Pattaya footballers, training under a UEFA-A coach means:

  • European technical curriculum — first touch, passing under pressure, position-specific skills
  • Tactical concepts age-appropriate — small-sided games progressing to full-pitch tactics
  • Long-term development pathway — not short-cut wins-now coaching
  • Sports psychology integration — confidence, focus, dealing with mistakes

This is dramatically higher caliber instruction than amateur-coached programs that dominate Thai youth football.

Two Training Locations

1. Horseshoe Point Pattaya

Horseshoe Point Resort & Country Club is the partnership venue for FAST PRO sessions:

  • 1,500-acre property with multi-sport facilities
  • East Pattaya near Mabprachan Reservoir
  • Multi-sport field suitable for football training
  • Quality grass pitch maintained
  • Established equestrian + adventure resort — additional family activities possible
  • Drive ~30-40 minutes from central Pattaya

2. IP Soccer Club Pattaya

IP Soccer Club — separate dedicated soccer/football venue in Pattaya. Specifics include:

  • Dedicated football pitches (typically AstroTurf or grass)
  • Convenient Pattaya location
  • Smaller-scale than Horseshoe Point but football-purposeful
  • Drive time depends on which Pattaya area you're in

The dual-venue model lets parents choose the location closer to home, school, or daily routine — meaningful for the time-pressed family demographic FAST PRO targets.

Age-Group Structure

Ages 4-15 Programmed

  • Players divided into groups by age and skill level

  • Skill checkpoints mark progression
  • Multi-year commitment encouraged for proper development

Typical Age Groups

  • U9 (7-8 years): Foundation skills, simple games
  • U11 (9-10 years): Tactical introduction, position concepts
  • U13 (11-12 years): Position specialization, fitness components
  • U15 (13-15 years): Pre-professional preparation, advanced tactics

Why Age Grouping Matters

  • Cognitive development matches instruction complexity
  • Physical maturity prevents injury and frustration
  • Peer-learning with same-age teammates
  • Coach can specialise in age-group pedagogy

Training Curriculum

UEFA-A informed European-style football training typically includes:

Technical Skills

  • First touch — receiving pass cleanly under pressure
  • Passing — short, medium, long with both feet
  • Dribbling — close ball control, change of direction
  • Shooting — power, accuracy, technique variations
  • Heading — age-appropriate at older groups
  • 1v1 attacking and defending

Tactical Concepts

  • Position roles — what each position does
  • Spatial awareness — finding space, denying space
  • Pressing principles — when to press, when to hold
  • Build-up play — passing patterns from defense
  • Attacking patterns — third-man runs, overloads
  • Set pieces — corners, free kicks

Physical Conditioning

  • Age-appropriate fitness — no overtraining young athletes
  • Speed and agility — football-specific movement
  • Coordination drills
  • Recovery and rest integrated

Psychological Development

  • Confidence building through successful skill execution
  • Discipline in training and match conduct
  • Motivation — internal drive vs. external pressure
  • Sportsmanship — handling wins and losses gracefully
