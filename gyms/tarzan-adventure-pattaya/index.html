Overview

Tarzan Adventure is Pattaya's premier outdoor jungle adventure park — a zipline-and-rope-challenge facility nestled between Nong Nooch Tropical Garden and the Golden Cliff Temple in Sattahip's hill country, offering a 25-platform full-course zipline experience plus alternative half-course and kids' course options. Beyond pure ziplining, the park integrates bridges, ladders, wire walks, abseiling stations, rope challenges, and jumping rope elements into a multi-element circuit that's part adventure, part fitness, part team-building.

The location is genuinely scenic — mountain jungle with views of the Golden Cliff Temple (Khao Chi Chan, the famous gold-leafed Buddha image) during the zipline ride. Where Bira Circuit delivers motor racing thrill, EasyKart delivers go-karting, Pattaya Park Tower Jump delivers the 56th-floor zip line, and Thai Sky Adventures delivers tandem skydiving, Tarzan Adventure delivers the jungle ropes-course adrenaline experience — a meaningful complement to the city's adventure tourism stack.

The park is partnered with Ramayana Water Park (also Sattahip), allowing combined-ticket adventure-and-water-park days. Multilingual staff serve English, Thai, Russian, Chinese, and Korean visitors, reflecting the park's broad tourist appeal.

The 25-Platform Zipline Course

How It Works

A multi-platform zipline course routes participants through:

  • 25 different platforms elevated in jungle canopy
  • Cable wires between platforms
  • Trained instructor at each transfer point
  • Safety harness + helmet mandatory
  • Step off platform, slide along cable, land on next platform
  • Multi-hour total experience — typically 1.5-3 hours

Course Variants

Full Course (25 platforms)

  • Premium experience
  • Adults and committed teens
  • Multi-hour duration
  • Most adrenaline

Half Course

  • Shorter version for time-constrained or less-confident participants
  • Adult and older teens still applicable
  • ~1-1.5 hour duration

Kids' Course

  • Lower platforms for younger participants
  • Shorter cable lengths
  • Adult-supervised depending on age
  • Family-friendly entry-level adventure

Course Elements Beyond Pure Zipline

Tarzan Adventure adds variety:

  • Bridges — narrow walking platforms between trees
  • Ladders — vertical climbs to next level
  • Wire walks — cable balance crossings
  • Abseiling — rope descent stations
  • Rope challenges — knot, swing, and obstacle work
  • Jumping rope — final-platform exit

This mixed-element circuit keeps the experience varied vs. pure zipline-after-zipline monotony.

Other On-Site Activities

Elephant Riding

A controversial offering — international animal welfare standards (WAZA, BIAZA) increasingly discourage commercial elephant riding due to welfare concerns. Visitors should research the specific operation's elephant care practices before participating.

Shooting Range

On-site small-scale shooting range option — combinable with zipline for adventure-day visitors.

Korean Restaurant

Reflects the substantial Korean tourist population in Pattaya — Korean BBQ and Korean cuisine on-site.

Coffee Shop

Standard adventure-park amenity for waiting family members and post-adventure refreshment.

Location & Setting

Geographic Setting

  • 108/14 Moo 6, Na Chom Thian, Sattahip District
  • Between Nong Nooch Tropical Garden and Golden Cliff Temple
  • Mountain / jungle terrain providing course infrastructure
  • Views of Khao Chi Chan (the famous gold-leafed Buddha cliff face)
  • 20-30 minute drive south of central Pattaya
  • Combinable with Ramayana Water Park, Cartoon Network Amazone (also Sattahip area)

Why The Setting Matters

The mountain jungle setting gives Tarzan Adventure several advantages over urban competitors:

  • Genuine elevation — platforms are high in actual canopy
  • Mountain breezes — cooler than coastal Pattaya
  • Scenic vistas — Buddha mountain visible during runs
  • Less commercial / urban atmosphere — natural environment
  • Combinable day with nearby Ramayana / Cartoon Network water parks

Pricing (Indicative)

Tarzan Adventure pricing:

  • Full course (25 platforms): ~฿1,500-2,500
  • Half course: ~฿900-1,500
  • Kids course: ~฿500-1,000
  • Group discounts available
  • Combined tickets with Ramayana Water Park
  • Hotel transfer sometimes available as add-on
  • Online platforms (Klook, KKday, Trip.com) often have 20-35% discounts

Safety

Standard Zipline Safety

  • Mandatory harness + helmet
  • Safety briefing before course start
  • Trained instructors at each transfer
  • Equipment inspections routine
  • Weight limits (typically 30-130 kg)
  • Closed-toe shoes required
  • No alcohol before participation

Risk Awareness

Zipline / ropes courses have statistically rare but real injury risk:

  • Equipment failures are very rare with maintained kit
  • User error at platform transfers most common cause of incidents
  • Travel insurance with adventure sports coverage strongly recommended
  • Pre-existing injuries (back, shoulder) should consult medical advice
  • Pregnancy disqualifies
