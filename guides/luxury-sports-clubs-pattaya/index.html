Luxury sports clubs in Pattaya

Pattaya is not just budget tourism — at the top tier, the city hosts genuinely world-class sports facilities. FITZ Club at Royal Cliff (triple-TripAdvisor Travelers' Choice winner), Greta Sport Club's 6 covered ITF Plexipave tennis courts, championship Pete Dye and Jack Nicklaus golf, and Asia's largest polo + equestrian operation are all here.
