# Rambaa Somdet M16 Muay Thai & MMA

## Overview

Rambaa Somdet M16 is the personal training camp of **Thailand's first MMA world champion**, the legendary Muay Thai fighter and Shooto Flyweight champion **Rambaa "M16" Somdet**. Founded in **2016** in Nong Prue, Pattaya — built directly adjacent to the **Sor Klinmee gym** where Rambaa learned the sport as a child — the camp blends **authentic Muay Thai** (Rambaa's roots) with a **serious MMA program** (his world-championship discipline). It is one of the very few gyms in Pattaya that genuinely cross-trains both sports under elite-level guidance, in a no-frills, old-school environment.

The camp is small, family-connected, and built around Rambaa himself — students get direct access to a fighter who held the **Shooto Flyweight World Championship** and competed at the highest level globally.

## Rambaa "M16" Somdet — Background

Born **August 30, 1974** in Pattaya as **Silajak Klinmee**, Rambaa started boxing at age 7 at the famous **Sit Or camp** — the camp founded by his uncle that would later become Sor Klinmee. He fought first under the name "**Somdet Sit Or**", then as "**Somdet M16**" — the M16 being his sponsor (the M16 assault rifle).

### Muay Thai Career

- Approximately **100 fights** with **70+ wins**, including over **30 by KO**
- Known throughout Thailand as **"The Crazy Dancer"** — famous for his whimsical pre- and post-fight routines featuring acrobatics and Hip-Hop dance
- Multi-time stadium champion in Thailand

### MMA Career

- Transitioned to MMA in his thirties
- Became **Thailand's first ever MMA world champion** when he won the **Shooto Flyweight (115 lb) World Championship on November 23, 2009** in Tokyo, defeating Noboru Tahara in a rematch
- Considered the **world's best 115 lb / Flyweight MMA fighter from approximately 2007-2012**
- Final MMA record: 16 fights, **13 wins / 3 losses**

His combined Muay Thai + MMA résumé is virtually unique in Thai combat sports — very few fighters reached world-class level in both.

## Disciplines

- **Muay Thai** — full traditional program
- **MMA** — cage work, ground game, integration of striking and grappling
- **Cross-training between the two** — key differentiator vs. pure Muay Thai camps

## Facilities

- Traditional **Muay Thai ring**
- Dedicated **MMA cage** (rare in Pattaya outside the resort camps)
- Heavy bags, pad-holding stations
- Mat area for grappling work
- Open-air pavilion
- Small free-weight area
- Adjacent to Sor Klinmee — students can move between the two

The vibe is described as "no-nonsense, old-school" — same Thai-camp aesthetic as Sor Klinmee, with the addition of dedicated MMA infrastructure.

## Trainers

Rambaa himself is regularly present at the gym and works directly with students — both group and private. Additional trainers include former Thai pros and grapplers. English communication is workable thanks to Rambaa's international career and exposure.

## Daily Schedule

Standard two-a-day rhythm:

- **Morning session:** run, shadow, padwork, bagwork
- **Afternoon session:** technical work, sparring, MMA cross-training
- **Mon-Sat**, Sundays rest

Specific schedule may vary — confirm with gym.

## Family / Camp Cluster

Rambaa Somdet M16 sits **100 meters from Sor Klinmee Gym** (his uncle's camp where he started). Together with surrounding family camps in Nong Prue, this creates a **small concentrated cluster of authentic Thai gyms** in East Pattaya — students sometimes train at multiple in the cluster during longer stays.

## Accommodation

Limited on-site / near-site basic accommodation for students. Most visitors arrange housing in the Nong Prue / East Pattaya area or commute from central Pattaya.

- Authentic family-camp culture
- Workable English (Rambaa's international career exposure)

## Reputation Summary

Held in **high esteem among MMA fighters specifically** — there are very few gyms in all of Thailand where you can train MMA under a former world champion in an authentic Thai-camp environment. Particularly recommended by visitors who've trained widely in Thailand and want something **different from the standard Muay Thai-only experience**.

MMA fighters and amateurs • Muay Thai students wanting MMA cross-training • Long-stay expats in Pattaya • Visitors wanting access to a world-champion-level coach in an authentic camp • Anyone planning to also visit Sor Klinmee next door

Pure tourists looking for resort polish • Visitors wanting one-week luxury training holidays • Anyone uncomfortable with small, traditional facilities
