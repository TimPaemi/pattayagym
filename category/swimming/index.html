03 Common questions

Swimming in Pattaya — FAQ.

Where can I swim in Pattaya besides the sea?

Pattaya has public and hotel swimming pools, lap pools, swim academies and waterparks. Several hotel pools admit non-guests for a day fee, and there are dedicated swim-coaching venues for adults and children. Browse this category for the options and their locations.

Are there proper lap pools for training?

Yes. A few venues offer proper lap pools, including 25-metre pools, suitable for training rather than just leisure. Some sit inside hotels or sports clubs. Check each venue page for pool details before planning a training session.

Can children learn to swim in Pattaya?

Yes. There are swim academies with certified coaches running lessons for children and beginners, plus family-friendly pools and waterparks. Look for venues tagged swim-academy or kids, and contact them about lesson schedules.

How much does pool access cost?

Public pools and hotel day passes are inexpensive; waterparks charge an entry fee; coaching is priced per session or course. See each venue page for its price tier and contact it for current rates.
