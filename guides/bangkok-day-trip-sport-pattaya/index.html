Common questions

How long does it take to drive from Pattaya to Bangkok stadiums?

Bangkok is 1.5-2 hours from Pattaya by car or bus depending on traffic. A day-trip with afternoon arrival, evening fight night, and post-fight bus back to Pattaya is realistic and popular.

Should I visit Lumpinee or Rajadamnern Stadium?

Both are essential. Rajadamnern (founded 1945) is the world's first Muay Thai stadium and runs fights every Mon-Sun. Lumpinee (Ramintra Road since 2014) is the modern flagship — Friday and Saturday cards. Many Pattaya visitors do both on different trips.

Can I combine a Bangkok stadium night with a Pattaya stay?

Yes — most Pattaya hotels can book the round-trip transfer + tickets package. Tourist mini-buses run Pattaya → Bangkok stadium → Pattaya same-day for ~฿1,500-2,500 inclusive of tickets.
