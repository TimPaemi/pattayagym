Common questions about tennis / padel / squash in Pattaya

Where can I play tennis in Pattaya?

Pattaya Sports Club (founded 1992), Fitz Club at Royal Cliff (premier multi-court), Greta Sport Club (6 covered ITF Plexipave courts), Inter Club, Tennis Pattaya, and most 5-star resort courts.

Is there pickleball in Pattaya?

Yes — Pickleball Pattaya on Pratumnak Soi 6 is the dedicated facility. Multi-sport venues like Inter Club and Fitz Club also have pickleball courts. Active 50+ expat community with leagues and tournaments.

Where can I play badminton in Pattaya?

Euro Badminton (dedicated), Pattaya Tennis & Badminton Inter Club, and several condo/community courts. Indoor court hire ฿200–฿400/hour.

Can I rent racquets at Pattaya courts?

Most venues provide loaner racquets and balls/shuttles. Confirm at booking, especially for tennis and squash where personal racquet preference matters.
