# Real Divers Pattaya

## Overview

Real Divers is **Pattaya's only British family-run PADI 5-Star Instructor Development Center (IDC)** — operating since **2005** by **brothers Neil and Peter Chandler**, sons of founder Dave Chandler (a retired British journalist who started diving 30 years ago, sold his publishing company, and emigrated to Thailand to pursue diving full-time). The family-business character is genuinely meaningful in Pattaya's dive market — most dive shops are corporate-owned or independently owned by single instructors, while Real Divers' **multi-generational family operation** delivers a different cultural and operational profile.

The boat is a **12-metre speedboat** — meaningfully larger than typical Pattaya dive boats — making it stable for full classes plus crew with comfortable kit-up zones, dry storage, and freshwater. **Trips depart at 09:00 and return by 14:00**, fitting a 5-hour window perfect for tourists who want to combine diving with afternoon Pattaya activities. **Daily trips run year-round to either the Pattaya near islands or the Samae San islands** (south of Sattahip), both delivering coral and wreck diving at recreational depths.

The PADI 5-Star IDC tier puts Real Divers alongside **No Limit Divers (PADI 5-Star IDC, Gary Phillips Platinum Course Director)** and **Aquanauts (PADI 5-Star CDC + Nat Geo)** as **the top three credentialed dive operations in Pattaya**. For divers wanting **the most credentialed instruction available in the city plus the family-business operational character**, Real Divers is genuinely the British family choice.

## The 12-Metre Speedboat

### Why Boat Size Matters

A **12-metre speedboat** is **larger than typical Pattaya dive boats** (most operators use 8-10m boats):

- **More stable** — better for newer divers or rough conditions
- **Comfortable kit-up zones** — dry tank racks, equipment storage
- **Freshwater on board** — equipment rinse during surface intervals
- **Toilet facility**
- **Shaded deck** for sun protection during transit
- **Space for full class** plus crew comfortably
- **Faster transit** to dive sites — speedboat hull design

The speedboat capability matters for **far-island trips to the Samae San islands** (south of Sattahip) — these require longer transit time than Pattaya near islands, and a comfortable speedboat makes the experience meaningfully better.

## PADI 5-Star IDC Capability

### What IDC Status Means

The **5-Star IDC tier** requires:

- **Course Director on staff** (PADI rank empowered to train new instructors)
- **Multi-classroom training facilities**
- **Sufficient boat capacity** for daily IE-readiness diving
- **Long operating history** under PADI auditing
- **Multiple instructor staff** (not just Course Director)
- **Continuous IDC offerings** rather than occasional

Real Divers' IDC status means **the full pipeline from beginner Discover Scuba through PADI Open Water Scuba Instructor certification is available in-house** — students don't need to relocate to a different operator for advanced training.

### Course Catalog

Beyond standard recreational courses:

- **PADI Divemaster Internship**
- **PADI Open Water Scuba Instructor (OWSI)**
- **PADI IDC Staff Instructor** — qualified to assist with IDC programs
- **PADI Specialty Instructor** courses
- **Continuing education** for instructors

This **professional-track depth** is meaningful for serious dive career students.

## Daily Trip Format

### Schedule

- **09:00** — boat departs Bali Hai Pier
- **~10:00** — arrival at first dive site (Pattaya near islands or Samae San)
- **First dive** — typically 30-45 minutes
- **Surface interval** — refreshments, debrief, equipment rinse
- **Second dive** — typically 30-45 minutes (same or different site)
- **Return transit** — back to Bali Hai
- **14:00** — return to pier

### What's Included

- **Boat transport**
- **Tanks + weights**
- **Dive guide / instructor**
- **Marine park fees** (where applicable)
- **Light snacks + water**
- **Equipment rental** included in courses; available for fun divers

## Dive Site Coverage

### Pattaya Near Islands (30-min boat ride)

The standard recreational menu:

- **Koh Larn (Coral Island)** — soft + hard coral gardens, bamboo sharks, hawksbill turtles
- **Koh Sak** — anemone gardens, smaller scale
- **Koh Krok** — sloping reef, beginner-friendly currents

### Samae San Islands (1-1.5 hour boat ride)

A meaningful Real Divers specialty — most operators don't run regular Samae San trips:

- **Located south of Sattahip** — quieter, less commercial than near islands
- **Better visibility** typically (10-20m vs. 5-15m near Pattaya)
- **Larger reef structures**
- **Multiple wreck sites** — Royal Thai Navy artificial reefs
- **Less dive traffic** — fewer divers per site
- **Half-day commitment** required

Visiting the Samae San islands sets Real Divers apart from purely near-island operators.
