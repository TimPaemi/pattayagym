Overview

Pattaya Country Club is one of the closest 18-hole golf courses to central Pattaya — a 7,054-yard par-72 layout opened in 1993 and spread across more than 2,000 rai (320 hectares / 790 acres) of gently rolling hills in Khao Mai Kaeo. The location advantage is meaningful: where most Pattaya-area golf requires a 40-50 minute drive to Bang Phra (Khao Kheow, Bangpra), Sattahip (Chee Chan, Phoenix Gold), or Rayong (St Andrews 2000), Pattaya Country Club sits closer to central Pattaya than nearly any other championship-length course.

The course was built before the 1990s mega-course construction boom that produced Khao Kheow (1992), Treasure Hill (1994), Phoenix Gold (1997), and Laem Chabang (1995) — making it part of the first wave of Pattaya golf-tourism investment triggered by the 1988 Bangpra renovation. The 7,054-yard length, par 72, and rolling-hills setting make it a solid mid-tier round without the architectural drama of newer courses, but with convenience and value that more remote courses can't match.

For golfers who want a quality 18-hole round without committing 50+ minutes of drive each way, Pattaya Country Club is the pragmatic choice in the Pattaya golf circuit.

The 1993 Opening

The course opened in 1993 — early in Pattaya's golf-tourism construction cycle. This places it:

  • Five years after Bangpra's 1988 tourism-renovation flagship
  • One year after Khao Kheow's 1992 Pete Dye opening
  • Pre-Treasure Hill (1994), pre-Laem Chabang (1995), pre-Phoenix Gold (1997)
  • Among the first generation of purpose-built Pattaya golf-tourism courses

The 1993 era's design language is mature parkland — gentler than the dramatic strategic courses of the 2000s+ — making Pattaya Country Club's playing character friendly and approachable rather than punishingly strategic.

Course Architecture

Layout

  • 18 holes
  • Par 72
  • 7,054 yards from championship tees
  • Multiple tee boxes for varied skill levels (typically 4-5 tees per hole)

Setting

  • 2,000+ rai of rolling hills — gently undulating natural terrain
  • Khao Mai Kaeo location — east of central Pattaya
  • Tree-lined fairways — established mature trees define the corridors
  • Water hazards strategically placed but not dominant
  • Views of surrounding hills

Style

The course design is mid-tier mature parkland — characteristic of mid-1990s Asian golf development:

  • Generous fairway widths on most holes
  • Classic green complexes without extreme tier severity
  • Strategic but not punishing hazard placement
  • Rolling terrain providing natural elevation variation
  • Friendly to mid-handicap players

This makes Pattaya Country Club more accessible than the strategic-school designs at Khao Kheow, Mountain Shadow, or St Andrews 2000.

Conditions

The course is generally mid-tier maintained:

  • Bermuda fairways — adequate to good
  • Bent / Tifeagle greens depending on era
  • Bunkers raked and groomed
  • Tee surfaces functional

Conditions are solid for the price tier — not premium-resort pristine, but reliable for the golf experience.

Common questions about Pattaya Country Club

Where is Pattaya Country Club?

Pattaya Country Club is located in Khao Mai Kaeo (one of the closest courses to Pattaya proper), Pattaya, Thailand. Full address: Khao Mai Kaeo, Bang Lamung, Tambon Khao Mai Kaeo, Chonburi 20150.

What are the opening hours of Pattaya Country Club?

Pattaya Country Club operates Daily — first tee from sunrise; last tee typically 14:30. Hours can change on Thai public holidays — call ahead or check their website to confirm.

How much does Pattaya Country Club cost?

Pattaya Country Club is in the ฿฿ tier — mid-tier pricing (typically ฿2,000–฿5,000/month or moderate drop-in). For exact current rates, contact the venue directly.

Do I need to book in advance at Pattaya Country Club?

Tee-time booking in advance is strongly recommended at all Pattaya golf courses, especially on weekends and during peak tourist season (Nov–Feb). Most courses also rent clubs and arrange caddies on the day.

Do staff at Pattaya Country Club speak English?

Pattaya is an international tourist city, and most venues have at least one English-speaking staff member. Contact Pattaya Country Club ahead of your visit if you need language confirmation.
