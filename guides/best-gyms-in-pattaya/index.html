If you type "best gyms in Pattaya" into Google, you usually want one of three things: a commercial fitness gym for weights, a Muay Thai camp for a training holiday, or a resort-style club with tennis and a pool. Pattaya has all three — spread across six neighborhoods, from ฿ budget day passes on Soi Buakhao to Fitz Club at Royal Cliff.

Quick answer — best gym in Pattaya by trip type

  • Best overall commercial gym (weights + machines): Muscle Factory Pattaya (Pratamnak) for serious lifters; Anytime Fitness (Central) for 24-hour chain convenience.
  • Best budget gym: Tony's Gym (Central) — cheapest weights room in the city. See cheapest gyms + price table.
  • Best Muay Thai camp (overall): Fairtex Training Center (resort stay-and-train) and WKO Muay Thai (budget authenticity). Full ranking: best Muay Thai in Pattaya.
  • Best gym for tourists (3–7 nights): Jetts Fitness or Anytime near Beach Road — walkable, English-friendly, day passes. Gym day pass guide.
  • Best luxury sports club: Fitz Club at Royal Cliff — tennis, squash, pool, weights. Luxury clubs guide.
  • Best for digital nomads / long-stay: Jomtien condo belt — Coco Fitness, Jomtien area guide, nomad fitness guide.

Best gyms by Pattaya area

Pattaya is not one neighbourhood — picking the wrong area costs you 30 minutes in traffic twice a day.

Central Pattaya (Beach Road, Soi Buakhao)

Highest density of commercial gyms and budget Muay Thai. Best if your hotel is on Beach Road or you want Walking Street walkability.

FAQ — best gyms in Pattaya

  • What is the best gym in Pattaya for tourists? For a short stay without a car, Central Pattaya chains (Anytime, Jetts, Fitness 7) or a walkable Muay Thai camp (WKO, Battle Conquer) are the practical default. Use our day pass guide for drop-in pricing tiers.
  • How much does a gym membership cost in Pattaya? Day passes and weekly rates are tourist-friendly; monthly memberships are far cheaper than Western gyms. Budget venues start around a few hundred baht per visit — see cheapest gyms for tier comparison. Always confirm current rates at the venue.
  • Is Pattaya good for Muay Thai training? Yes — one of Thailand's highest densities of foreigner-friendly camps. Beginners guide · Best camps ranking.
  • Are there 24-hour gyms in Pattaya? Yes — Anytime Fitness and Jetts both run extended or 24-hour access in Central and Jomtien. 24-hour gyms guide.
  • Can I use a hotel gym without staying there? Some resort gyms sell day passes to non-guests. Hotel gym day pass guide.
