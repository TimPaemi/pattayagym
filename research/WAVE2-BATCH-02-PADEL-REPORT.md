### What Thai sources added

- **Prime:** recent Thai reviewers repeatedly supplied practical details absent
  from the booking app: high roof, useful airflow in direct sun,
  air-conditioned bathrooms, a child play/waiting area, spacious parking and
  easy motorway access. Thai beginners also describe staff teaching them.
- **Chilli:** Banmuang supplied the exact official-launch context and date. The
  small Thai review subset was positive but too thin to establish a service or
  facility problem. Translated recent international reviews instead revealed
  that some non-court facilities were unfinished during opening and that
  tournaments can occupy convenient slots.

### Methods that produced no upgrade

- Prime's visible recent Thai set produced no repeated complaint. That absence
  is reported as a negative finding, not converted into a claim that the venue
  has no drawbacks.
- Chilli's Thai reviews did not independently establish staff language. Its
  `confirmed` result comes from the direct multilingual coach listing, not from
  name, tourist location or an English hunch.
- Padium and Vamos have no completed-session reviews, so neither received an
  English-confidence outcome or a full record.
