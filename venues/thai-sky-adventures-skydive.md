# Thai Sky Adventures — Tandem Skydiving Pattaya

## Overview

Thai Sky Adventures is **the only full-time skydiving drop zone in Southeast Asia** — a uniquely positioned operator with **over 25 years of skydiving experience** that delivers tandem jumps from **13,000 feet (4,000 metres)** with **60 seconds of freefall at 200 km/h** followed by a **5-minute parachute ride** featuring **stunning ocean views over Rayong's beaches**. The dropzone is located **25 km north of Pattaya** (and 96 km south of Bangkok) with **free hotel pickup from Pattaya** included for both **07:00 AM and 10:00 AM jump slots**.

The skydiving experience is **Pattaya's apex adrenaline activity** — for travellers who've already done water parks (Cartoon Network Amazone, Ramayana), beach activities (parasailing with Wave Pattaya, kitesurfing with KBA Pattaya), karting (EasyKart), and other thrills, **a tandem skydive is the bucket-list capstone**. The 60 seconds of freefall at 200 km/h is genuinely **a transformative experience** — most first-time skydivers describe it as **one of the most intense moments of their lives**.

The "only full-time SE Asia drop zone" positioning is meaningful: most Asian regions have **occasional skydiving operations** that run when conditions allow, but Thai Sky Adventures operates **with year-round drop zone infrastructure, certified tandem instructors, professional aircraft, and continuous safety standards** — putting it in a category usually reserved for the major skydiving destinations globally (Florida, California, Spain, Australia, New Zealand).

## The Tandem Skydive Experience

### The Day of Your Jump

#### Pre-Jump (Pattaya Hotel Pickup)

- **07:00 OR 10:00 AM pickup** from Pattaya hotels
- **Free transfer included** in price
- **Drive 25 km north** to drop zone (~30-40 minutes)

#### Arrival at Drop Zone

- **Briefing** with tandem instructor
- **Safety video** mandatory viewing
- **Equipment fitting** — harness, jumpsuit
- **Liability waiver** signing
- **Health screening** — basic questions

#### Boarding the Plane

- **Skydive aircraft** seats 8-15 jumpers + crew
- **Climb to 13,000 feet** — typically 15-20 minutes
- **Anticipation builds** during climb

#### The Jump

- **At altitude:** door opens, instructor moves you to edge
- **Step off / pushed off:** you exit the plane
- **Freefall begins:** 60 seconds at 200 km/h
- **Body position:** instructor controls, you experience
- **Wind sound deafening** during freefall
- **Vision: Earth approaching** at incredible speed

#### Parachute Deployment

- **At ~5,000 feet:** instructor deploys parachute
- **Sudden deceleration** as canopy opens
- **5 minutes of canopy ride** to landing
- **Calm and serene** vs. the freefall chaos
- **Possible to talk** with instructor during canopy
- **Steering** — instructor or experienced jumpers can let tandem rider steer briefly

#### Landing

- **Sliding feet-first landing** on prepared landing zone
- **Instructor handles** the technical aspects
- **Emotional debrief** — most first-timers are euphoric

#### Post-Jump

- **Photos and video review** with photographer
- **Skydive certificate** presentation
- **Souvenirs** included
- **Return to Pattaya** by hotel transfer

## Requirements & Restrictions

### Age and Weight

- **Minimum age:** 12 years with parental consent
- **Minimum weight:** 25 kg
- **Basic weight limit:** 95 kg
- **Maximum weight:** 115 kg (with surcharge possibly)
- **Pregnancy:** not allowed
- **Recent surgery:** require physician clearance

### Health Conditions

Disqualifying or restricting conditions:

- **Severe heart disease**
- **Recent surgery within 6-12 months** (depending on type)
- **Pregnancy**
- **Severe asthma** uncontrolled
- **Recent eye surgery**
- **Severe back conditions**

For most healthy adults aged 12-65, **the skydive is accessible**.

### Equipment

All equipment provided by Thai Sky Adventures:

- **Tandem harness** — connects you to instructor
- **Jumpsuit** — wind-resistant
- **Goggles** — eye protection during freefall
- **Helmet** for canopy descent (optional during freefall sometimes)
- **Camera operator's** equipment
