Tennis & badminton.

By Tim Paemi · 9 min read · Updated 2026-05-31 · How we rank →

Pattaya runs floodlit tennis on Pratamnak, covered courts south at Greta, budget badminton halls on the Darkside, and seven courts at Fitz Club. Padel and pickleball have their own guide — this page is tennis and badminton only.

Pattaya · 158 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

Pattaya has more tennis and badminton than most visitors expect — floodlit hard courts on Pratamnak, covered courts south at Greta, budget badminton halls on the Darkside, and the luxury tier at Fitz Club. Padel and pickleball have their own guide; this page covers tennis, badminton, and multi-racquet clubs in our directory.

Hub: Tennis / Padel / Squash · Padel & pickleball guide · compare · plan my trip.

Tennis in Pattaya — how venues differ

Fitz Club — luxury tier (Royal Cliff)

Fitz Club at Royal Cliff runs seven tennis courts, two AC squash courts, table tennis, swimming, and gym — Pattaya's headline racquet address. ฿฿฿฿. Day-pass policies vary; call ahead. Compare: luxury sports clubs.

Central & social tennis

  • Pattaya Tennis Club — floodlit hard courts, coaching, social leagues, central location.
  • Tara Tennis Club — three hard courts including one covered roof (resurfaced 2022).
  • TOS Tennis Academy — Jomtien-side courts at Baan Suan Lalana; closest tennis to Jomtien Beach.
  • Pattaya Sports Club — non-profit multi-sport club, ~4,700 members, tennis among other sports.

Hotel & resort courts

  • Siam Bayshore Resort — three outdoor hard courts near Walking Street; hotel guests and day visitors.
  • Centara Grand Mirage — two tennis courts plus water park and family facilities.
  • InterContinental Pattaya — hilltop Pratamnak courts adjacent to Play Padel cluster.

South — covered courts

Greta Sport Club (Na Jomtien / Sattahip) — six covered tennis courts with rain protection. Pair with Na Jomtien & Sattahip guide if you base south.

Badminton in Pattaya

Badminton suits humid evenings when outdoor tennis is brutal — wood-floor halls, hourly court hire, casual community nights.

  • Euro Badminton — East Pattaya (Soi Chaiyaphruek 2); courts, pool, Thai-food restaurant. Budget tier.
  • Diamond Badminton — South Pattaya 3rd Road; multiple wood-floor courts, equipment rental.
  • Pattaya Tennis & Badminton Inter Club — tennis, badminton, pickleball, table tennis under one roof.

Padel & pickleball — separate guide

Dedicated padel and pickleball facilities live on Pratamnak and Mabprachan — not repeated here. Full court map: padel & pickleball in Pattaya.

Where to stay for racquet sport

  • Pratamnak Hill — Fitz Club, Play Padel, Pickleball Pattaya, InterContinental. Naklua & Pratamnak guide.
  • Jomtien — TOS Tennis; 20–30 min taxi to Pratamnak leagues. Jomtien guide.
  • Central — Pattaya Tennis Club, Diamond Badminton, Pattaya Sports Club. Central guide.

Sample weekly rhythm (tennis + gym)

  • Mon/Wed: evening tennis at Pattaya Tennis Club or Tara
  • Tue/Thu: strength at Muscle Factory or hotel gym — day pass guide
  • Fri: badminton social at Euro or Inter Club
  • Weekend: padel or pickleball — padel guide
