03 Common questions

Tennis / Padel / Squash in Pattaya — FAQ.

Can I play tennis or padel as a visitor in Pattaya?

Yes. Pattaya has public and club courts for tennis, padel, squash, pickleball, badminton and table tennis, many available to visitors by the hour. Some venues offer coaching and social leagues that are easy to join for a short stay. Browse this category for courts near you.

How much does court hire cost?

Hourly court rates are generally good value, and floodlit evening play may cost a little more. Coaching and ball-machine sessions are charged separately. Check each venue page for its price tier and contact it for hourly rates and to reserve a court.

Is padel available in Pattaya?

Yes. Padel has grown quickly and Pattaya now has dedicated padel courts, listed in this category. Most welcome beginners and can help arrange games or coaching, which is one of the easiest ways into the sport on a short visit.

Do I need to bring my own racquet?

Not necessarily. Many venues rent racquets and sell balls, and coaching venues provide equipment. If you have a preferred racquet, bring it; otherwise contact the venue to confirm rental before you arrive.
