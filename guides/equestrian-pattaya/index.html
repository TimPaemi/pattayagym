Equestrian & polo.

By Tim Paemi · 7 min read · Updated 2026-05-31 · How we rank →

Pattaya equestrian means two world-scale venues — Horseshoe Point's 1,500-acre riding school at Mabprachan and Thai Polo Club's 250-hectare polo fields north of the city. This guide maps both, booking rules, and how to pair a horse day with golf or Muay Thai.

Pattaya · 158 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

Pattaya has two serious equestrian venues — Horseshoe Point Resort at Mabprachan and Thai Polo & Equestrian Club north of the city. Both run riding schools, polo, and multi-sport days on large estates. This guide covers who each suits, booking expectations, and how to pair a horse day with Muay Thai or golf nearby.

Hub: Equestrian category · Luxury sports clubs · East Pattaya guide · compare · plan my trip.

Horseshoe Point Resort — Mabprachan mega-facility

Horseshoe Point Resort & Country Club (Soi Siam Country Club, near Mabprachan Reservoir) is a 1,500-acre estate — International Riding School since 1999, five indoor/outdoor arenas, 100+ horses across Lusitano, Appaloosa, Lipizzaner, Arab, Warmblood, Falabella, and Thai pony breeds.

Same campus runs polo, paintball, zipline, mountain biking, and multi-sport fields — useful if your group splits interests. On-site accommodation for stay-and-ride weekends. Phone +66 38 253 599.

Best for: families wanting a full-day estate visit; beginner trail rides; FAST PRO Football Academy uses Horseshoe grounds — kids sport guide. Pair with Darkside training at Kombat Group or Jungle Gym CrossFit.

Thai Polo & Equestrian Club — Asia-scale polo

Thai Polo & Equestrian Club (Banglamung / Mabprachan Lake, ~25 min north of Pattaya) claims largest multi-functional polo and equestrian facility in Asia — 250 hectares, three polo fields plus two practice pitches, 250+ stables, endurance and Derby programmes, Argentinian vet on staff.

Best for: serious polo players and international equestrian visitors; premium tier (฿฿฿฿). Book ahead — walk-ins rare. Website: thai-polo-club.com.

Horseshoe vs Thai Polo — quick pick

  • First trail ride / family day? → Horseshoe Point — riding school + adventure activities on one ticket.
  • Polo or competitive equestrian? → Thai Polo & Equestrian Club — tournament-scale fields.
  • Stay overnight? → Horseshoe accommodation on-site; Thai Polo by appointment.
  • Budget day? → Neither is cheap — compare resort tier: luxury sports clubs.

Booking and safety

Both venues require advance booking for riding slots and helmet fitting. Confirm weight limits and age policies for children. Transport: Pattaya Vehicle Rentals — estates sit east/north of central Pattaya, not walkable from Beach Road.

Injury backup: Pattaya Medical. Gentle alternatives: seniors low-impact sport.

Sample day trip (equestrian + sport)

Morning trail ride Horseshoe → lunch on estate → afternoon paintball or MT pads at east camp → evening pool — swimming & pools guide.
