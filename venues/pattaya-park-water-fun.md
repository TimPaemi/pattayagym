# Pattaya Park Water & Fun Park (with Tower Jump)

## Overview

Pattaya Park is **the original Pattaya beachfront tower-and-water-park complex** — opened in **1989 as the first and tallest beachfront tower in Pattaya**, combining **a water park with slides and pools, a private beach, an amusement park with roller coaster and rides, AND a 170-metre zip-line tower jump from the 56th floor of Pattaya Park Tower**. Located on **Soi 12 Thappraya Road at the South Pattaya / Jomtien border**, the complex predates Cartoon Network Amazone (2014) and Ramayana Water Park (2016) by decades — making it **Pattaya's foundational water-park-and-thrill destination** that established the model these newer parks built on.

The complex's **most iconic attraction is the Tower Jump** — a **170-metre zip line slide from the 56th floor** where riders are **strapped into a harness and slide down to ground level**. Despite the name "Tower Jump", riders aren't truly free-falling — they're attached to the zip line throughout — but the **adrenaline experience of stepping off the 56th-floor platform** delivers a **bucket-list-tier thrill** for those who want to challenge their fear of heights.

For Pattaya families and tourists who want **a single-venue full-day experience combining water slides, beach time, amusement rides, AND a unique tower zip line** — and especially for **adrenaline seekers who want to slide off a 56th-floor tower** — Pattaya Park is genuinely unique in the city. The 1989 vintage means it's older and less polished than the newer water parks, but the **tower zip line cannot be replicated** by competitors.

## The 1989 Heritage

### Why Pattaya Park Matters Historically

Opened in **1989** as **the first and tallest beachfront tower in Pattaya**:

- **Pre-dates the modern Pattaya water-park boom** by 25+ years
- **Pioneer of beach-resort-tower architecture** in Pattaya
- **Established the multi-attraction complex model** that Cartoon Network Amazone, Ramayana, and others followed
- **Generations of Pattaya families** have memories from the park
- **Tower Jump zip line** has been a Pattaya bucket-list attraction since the 1990s

### What 1989 Vintage Means

The park is **older than its newer competitors**:

- **Less polished infrastructure** than Cartoon Network Amazone (2014) or Ramayana (2016)
- **Vintage attractions** with retro charm
- **Smaller scale** than Ramayana's 184,000 sqm
- **Established maintenance routines** but periodic renovations needed
- **Mixed reviews** — some find it dated, others appreciate the heritage character

For visitors comparing, **newer parks deliver more modern hardware**; **Pattaya Park delivers history + the unique Tower Jump**.

## The Tower Jump — The Defining Attraction

### What the Tower Jump Is

The Tower Jump is **the centerpiece thrill experience**:

- **Located at Pattaya Park Tower** — the original 1989 building
- **Slide from the 56th floor** — approximately **170 metres elevation**
- **Zip line system** — riders strapped into harness, attached to cable
- **Slide path** descends from tower to ground
- **NOT free-falling** despite the "Jump" name
- **Approximately 30-60 second descent** from start to landing

### Three Ride Variants

The Tower complex offers three different "jump" experiences:

#### 1. Tower Jump (Original)

- **Standard zip line** from 56th floor
- **Most popular** of the three rides
- **Suitable for** adults and older teens with parental supervision

#### 2. Sky Shutter

- **Variant zip line** with different speed/profile
- **Adrenaline level** comparable to original
- **Some report different angle** of descent

#### 3. Speed Shutter

- **Fastest of the three**
- **For thrill-seekers** wanting maximum adrenaline
- **Higher speed throughout descent**

### Safety

- **Professional safety harness** systems
- **Certified guides** at top platform
- **Cable inspections** routine
- **Weight and health restrictions** apply (typically 35-130kg, no severe heart conditions)
- **Closed in extreme weather** (high winds especially)

### Pricing

Tower Jump is typically **separate ticket from water park**:

- **Tower Jump:** ~฿700-1,200 typical
- **Tower Jump + Water Park combo:** ~฿1,200-1,800
- **Tower Jump + Beach access combo:** discounts available
- **Group / family rates** sometimes negotiable

## The Water Park

### Water Park Attractions

The water park has been **operational since 1989** with periodic updates:

- **Tornado slide** — high-thrust spiral descent
- **Speed slide** — straight-shot drop
- **Family slide** — for kids and groups
- **Lazy river** — relaxing tube circuit
- **Whirlpools** — circulating relaxation pools
- **Swimming pools** — varied depths for different ages
- **Children's play area** — splash zones for young kids

The slide hardware is **older than the slides at Cartoon Network Amazone or Ramayana** — vintage 1990s-2000s era. They're **functional and safe** but lack the modern engineering of newer parks.

### Hours

- **Water park: 10:00-19:00 daily**
- **Tower Jump: separate hours, may extend later**
