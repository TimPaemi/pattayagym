Best gym on the Darkside.

By TimPaemi · 6 min read · Updated 2026-06-02 · How we rank →

East Pattaya is where serious camps live away from Beach Road — Huai Yai all-inclusive resorts, authentic gyms, and Mabprachan lake sport with honest transport reality.

Pattaya · 157 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

East Pattaya — locals call it the Darkside — is Pattaya's rural training belt: Huai Yai lake country, Mabprachan Reservoir, Nong Prue back sois, and Soi Khao Talo. No Beach Road noise. No walking to pads from your hotel. Instead you get Kombat Group's all-inclusive campus, Sor Klinmee's authentic family camp, Sanit Sport Club's 1,000-member Mabprachan floor, and wakeboarding beside the reservoir.

Start with the East Pattaya area hub, slices like east-side Muay Thai and east fitness, then compare and plan my trip. You need a scooter or Grab for most venues — Pattaya Vehicle Rentals · central alternative: Central Pattaya guide.

East Pattaya geography — where am I actually training?

Huai Yai / Chak Nok Lake — Kombat Group's rural all-inclusive camp; 25–35 minutes from Beach Road.

Nong Prue / Khao Talo — Sor Klinmee, Rambaa M16, Jungle Gym CrossFit; authentic Thai gym belt, budget tier.

Mabprachan Reservoir — Sanit Sport Club, Thai Wake Park, Pattaya Padel Club lakeside, Horseshoe Point equestrian mega-campus.

These are not one neighbourhood — plan transport before you sign a monthly contract. Many long-stayers live east specifically to avoid central Pattaya discipline traps.

Why fighters choose East over Beach Road

  • Camp bubble focus — Kombat Group and Sor Klinmee run real two-a-day rhythm without Soi 6 distractions.
  • Authentic Thai gym culture — less resort polish, lower monthly burn than Fairtex tier.
  • Mabprachan lifestyle — lake runs, wakeboard recovery, padel social leagues on the reservoir.
  • Multi-discipline depth — MMA cage (Rambaa M16), CrossFit affiliate (Jungle Gym), equestrian (Horseshoe Point).

Trade-off: you are not walking to 7-Eleven at midnight from every camp. East suits committed training blocks, not 3-night tourist dabbling — unless you book Kombat's airport package.

Best Muay Thai camps on the Darkside

Kombat Group Thailand — all-inclusive training holiday

Kombat Group in Huai Yai is Pattaya's flagship stay-and-train resort camp: six rings, Muay Thai + boxing + MMA + BJJ + Krav, meals, rooms, laundry, airport transfer. Christian Daghio competitive legacy. ฿฿฿. The default east-side answer for foreigners who want one invoice and zero logistics.

Pick this if: 2–8 week focused camp holiday. Pair with camps with accommodation and visa guide. Skip if: you need beach walks between sessions — see Jomtien.

Sor Klinmee Muay Thai — family-run authentic camp

Sor Klinmee in Nong Prue is budget authentic Thailand: Mit Klinmee's family gym since 2009, home of Sudsakorn Sor Klinmee, drop-ins ~฿200–300, two-a-days Mon–Sat. Real Thai gym floor — not English-resort culture.

Pick this if: monthly budget MT with lineage credibility. Read safety guide and beginners guide first.

Sanit Sport Club — Mabprachan two-zone mega-club

Sanit Sport Club combines a commercial fitness centre with a dedicated Muay Thai gym on Mabprachan — 1,000+ members, Kru Rung (40 years Lumpinee/Rajadamnern), four daily MT sessions. ฿฿. Best if you want iron + pads on one membership east of Sukhumvit.

Rambaa Somdet M16 — MMA world champion's camp

Rambaa M16 sits 100 metres from Sor Klinmee — Thailand's first MMA world champion's personal camp, cage on site, Muay Thai + MMA two-a-days. ฿฿. Pair both gyms in one Nong Prue trip if you are comparing striking vs cage culture.

Fitness, CrossFit & budget iron

CrossFit Pattaya @ The Jungle Gym

Jungle Gym in Nong Prue is Pattaya's only CrossFit affiliate — also archery, dodgeball, slacklining, MMA. English coaches. ฿฿. The serious functional fitness address on the east side.

Castra Gym — relaxed Darkside bodybuilding

Castra Gym on Soi Khao Talo — budget ฿ tier, bodybuilder-friendly, less tourist traffic than central iron palaces. Compare pricing: cheapest gyms.

Mabprachan sport — wake, padel, horses

  • Thai Wake Park — Pattaya's only cable wakeboarding; beginner and advanced lakes.
  • Pattaya Padel Club — lakeside courts; see also padel & pickleball guide.
  • Horseshoe Point Resort — 1,500-acre equestrian + polo + paintball + zipline campus.
  • Mabprachan running loop — 4 km reservoir path; dawn heat management essential.

Getting to East Pattaya

From central Pattaya: Grab/Bolt ฿150–300 depending on Huai Yai vs Mabprachan. Baht buses do not serve most camp sois — budget scooter if training daily (experience required). From U-Tapao airport, Huai Yai camps are closer than Beach Road hotels.

Huai Yai vs Nong Prue vs Mabprachan — three east belts

Huai Yai is resort-camp country — Kombat Group, large compounds, airport-transfer culture. Nong Prue / Darkside holds authentic Thai gyms (Sor Klinmee) and local fitness without beach marketing. Mabprachan lake adds padel, wake park, and CrossFit — expat lake lifestyle, not walking distance to Beach Road. Pick one belt and rent nearby; daily cross-belt taxis erode training time.

All-inclusive camp checklist

  1. Confirm meals included — dietary restrictions (halal, vegan) need advance notice at Kombat-tier camps.
  2. Ask BJJ/MMA mat schedule if you are not pure Muay Thai — timetables rotate.
  3. Clarify partner training for couples — some rooms are fighter-only.
  4. Verify airport pickup window — UTP vs BKK arrival times differ by hours.
  5. Read camps with accommodation for Fairtex vs east-side comparison.

When east Pattaya is the wrong choice

Weekend party tourists who want Walking Street stumble distance should stay central. Pure weightlifting tourists without MT may find equipment sparse outside Jungle Gym and camp gyms — add central iron day passes. Digital nomads needing café density every afternoon often migrate to Jomtien after the first camp month.

Mabprachan lake week

Wake park mornings, padel afternoons, CrossFit evenings — east lake belt suits hybrid athletes. Grocery and café options improved but still thinner than Jomtien — plan meal prep or scooter to Thepprasit.

Sanit + guesthouse long-stay pattern

Athletes train at Sanit Sport Club, rent monthly room near Mabprachan, cook breakfast, taxi to camp — total cost often beats Kombat weekly rate if you do not need resort meals. Trade-off: you manage laundry, meals, and motivation without camp bubble accountability.

Rural east roads flood in heavy rain — have a backup indoor gym day (central day pass) when Huai Yai sois are underwater.

Grab sometimes declines remote east pickups at 05:00 — pre-book camp shuttle or keep camp driver contact for morning sessions.
