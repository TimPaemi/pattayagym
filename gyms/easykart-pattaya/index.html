★ About this venue

EasyKart Pattaya Thepprasit

Overview

EasyKart Pattaya Thepprasit is the newest go-kart racing facility in Pattaya — an outdoor multi-track venue offering a 350-metre beginner track plus an 800-metre racing track with multi-tier kart classes (Kids Kart, Regular Kart, Fast Kart, 2-Seater Kart) and a broad operating window from 10:00 to 01:00 daily. The 15-hour operating schedule is substantially longer than competitor Pattaya Kart Speedway (the older first-karting-in-Pattaya facility on Thepprasit Soi 9), making EasyKart the most accessible karting venue for evening and late-night enthusiasts.

The facility sits on Thepprasit Road — Pattaya's adventure-and-action corridor that also hosts Pattaya Kart Speedway, Pattaya Floating Market, and various tourist activities. EasyKart's specific position: modern infrastructure, multi-tier track system, accessible pricing (from ~฿480-598 per session), and family-friendly age range from 7-year-olds (Kids Kart) to adults (Fast Kart and 2-Seater for kids+adults together).

For Pattaya families, group bookings (bachelor parties, corporate events, birthday celebrations), thrill-seekers, and motorsport-curious visitors, EasyKart provides a structured outdoor karting experience with serious safety standards and modern equipment — a significant step up from the older Pattaya Kart Speedway in both infrastructure and operational professionalism.

EasyKart's specific advantage: dual-track configuration + modern karts + extended hours.

Track Configuration

Beginner Track (350 metres)

  • 350-metre outdoor track
  • Wider, gentler turns for skill-building
  • Suitable for Kids Kart and Regular Kart at slower speeds
  • Max speed for Regular Kart: 35 km/h
  • First-time karters start here
  • Adult-supervised kids comfortable on this track

Racing Track (800 metres)

  • 800-metre outdoor racing track
  • Tighter turns + faster straights
  • Suitable for Regular Kart and Fast Kart at higher speeds
  • Max speed for Regular Kart: 55 km/h
  • Fast Kart speeds: 70 km/h+
  • Competition-grade racing experience
  • Skilled driver progression track

The dual-track design lets mixed-skill groups split — beginners on the 350m, faster drivers on the 800m, all under same booking.

Kart Classes

Kids Kart

  • Age: 7-13 years
  • Height requirement: 130 cm+
  • Speed: governed for safety
  • Track: beginner 350m only
  • Adult supervision required

Regular Kart

  • Age: 12+
  • Height: 140 cm+
  • Max speed: 35 km/h on beginner track, 55 km/h on racing track
  • Most popular class for general visitors
  • Both tracks accessible

Fast Kart

  • Age: 15+
  • Height: 150 cm+
  • Higher engine output than Regular Kart
  • Speed: 70 km/h+ on racing track
  • Experienced drivers preferred
  • Racing track only

2-Seater Kart

  • Adult driver (20+) + child passenger
  • Child requirement: 110 cm+ height
  • OR 2 adults
  • Tandem riding for shared experience
  • Suitable for: family bonding, parent-child first-time karting, dating couples

Pricing

Multi-tier pricing structure:

  • Special prices from ~฿480 for promotional bookings
  • Standard pricing from ~฿598 typical
  • Discount via online platforms (Klook, KKday, Tiqets, Expedia)
  • Multi-session bundles discount per-session
  • Group bookings negotiable
  • Birthday party packages available

Per-session pricing typically covers:

  • One race / time slot (~10-15 minutes typical)
  • Equipment use (kart, helmet, balaclava optional)
  • Track briefing for first-timers
  • Insurance typically included

For a typical Pattaya kart day:

  • Single session: ฿480-700
  • Multi-session: ฿1,500-3,000 across 3-5 races
  • Endurance / racing event: premium rate

Hours — 10:00 to 01:00 Daily

The 15-hour operating window is meaningful:

Why It Matters

  • Day-time karting for tourists with morning availability
  • Afternoon family sessions for school-age kids
  • Evening entertainment for couples / friends
  • Late-night thrill for nightlife adjacent (Pattaya Walking Street ~5km away)
  • Birthday parties can run dinner-and-karting evenings
  • Corporate events can extend into post-dinner hours

The 01:00 close is distinctive in Pattaya — most active recreational venues close 22:00-23:00. EasyKart's late-night operation accommodates the night-out demographic that Pattaya naturally attracts.

Safety Equipment & Procedures

Standard karting safety:

  • Helmet mandatory and provided
  • Balaclava (head sock) optional for hygiene
  • Closed-toe shoes required (no flip-flops, sandals)
  • Long pants recommended for skin protection
  • Track marshals monitoring laps
  • Yellow flags for caution, red flags for stop
  • First aid on premises
  • Insurance on all bookings

The Thepprasit Road Setting

Geographic Context

Thepprasit Road is a major Pattaya arterial connecting central Pattaya to Sukhumvit Road and beyond:

  • Multiple Pattaya tourist attractions
  • Pattaya Kart Speedway (sister/competitor) on Thepprasit Soi 9
  • Pattaya Floating Market nearby
  • Multiple restaurants along Thepprasit
  • Songthaew accessible along the road
  • 15-25 minutes from Walking Street central Pattaya
  • 10-15 minutes from Jomtien Beach

Pros

  • Newest karting infrastructure in Pattaya
  • Dual-track system — 350m beginner + 800m racing
  • Multi-tier kart classes — Kids, Regular, Fast, 2-Seater
  • 15-hour daily operating — 10:00-01:00
  • Late-night sessions unique in Pattaya recreational scene
  • Thepprasit Road location — accessible
  • Multilingual service — English, Thai, Russian, Chinese
  • Online booking via multiple platforms
  • Safety equipment provided
  • Family-friendly — Kids Kart from age 7
  • Corporate / birthday packages available

Cons

  • Outdoor only — weather dependent (rain stops play)
  • Tropical heat challenging in dry season
  • Premium pricing for premium-equipment experience
  • Crowded peak hours — book ahead
  • Single venue — no multi-location flexibility
  • Less established reputation than Pattaya Kart Speedway

Best For

  • First-time karters (Kids Kart + 2-Seater entry)
  • Family bookings with mixed-age members
  • Birthday parties / group celebrations
  • Corporate team-building events
  • Bachelor / hen parties
  • Thrill-seeking tourists
  • Late-night adventure seekers
  • Couples wanting active date activity

Not Best For

  • Pure budget seekers — kids' arcade indoor karts cheaper
  • Walk-in only travellers — booking advantage
  • Heat-sensitive participants in dry season midday
  • Rain-season visitors with outdoor closure risk
  • Small-child supervision issues — minimum age 7
