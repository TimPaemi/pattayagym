BJJ & MMA.

By Tim Paemi · 6 min read · Updated 2026-06-01 · How we rank →

Pattaya is Muay Thai country first — but dedicated BJJ academies, cage gyms, and multi-discipline camps now cover Gi, No-Gi, MMA, and strength work from Jomtien to the Darkside. This guide maps venues where grappling is a real programme, not a single evening class on a boxing schedule.

Pattaya is a Muay Thai city first — but the grappling and MMA scene has grown fast. Dedicated BJJ academies, cage gyms, and multi-discipline camps now cover Gi, No-Gi, MMA, and S&C from Jomtien to the Darkside. This guide maps where to train — venues where BJJ/MMA is a real programme, not a single evening class on a boxing timetable.

Hub: BJJ / Grappling · MMA · Muay Thai · compare venues · plan my trip.

BJJ-only vs MMA gym vs multi-discipline camp

  • BJJ-only gyms — Gi and No-Gi classes, belt progression, open mat. Example: ALFA BJJ.
  • MMA gyms — striking plus grappling, often a competition cage. Venum, Rage, Rambaa M16.
  • Multi-discipline camps — MT mornings + BJJ afternoons. Kombat Group, Fairtex BJJ blocks, Venum passes.

Beginner context: Muay Thai beginners · best for beginners (all sports) · Safety: is Muay Thai safe?

1. ALFA BJJ Pattaya — dedicated academy

ALFA BJJ Pattaya (Soi Ko Pai 12, Muang Pattaya) is Pattaya's first standalone Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu academy in venue files — Gi and No-Gi, all levels, mat-focused environment without pad-round noise in the same room.

Why standalone matters: more rolls per hour, instructors focused on jiu-jitsu, partners who came to grapple — not tourists squeezing one class between Muay Thai sessions. Phone +66 96 794 1992 · verify class times on Facebook AlfaBJJPattaya before you travel.

Best for: purple belts and above on holiday wanting pure mat time; beginners who want BJJ identity without learning nine other martial arts on day one.

2. Venum Training Camp — Jomtien corridor

Venum Training Camp near Jomtien runs Muay Thai, MMA, BJJ, combat fitness, and kids programmes. Two rings plus cage/mat zone. Strong for visitors who want beach proximity and modern facilities — Jomtien area guide.

Best for: holiday fighters stacking morning MT + evening No-Gi; families with kids MT and parent BJJ on one campus brand.

3. Rage Fight Academy — south Pattaya / Dongtan

Rage Fight Academy stacks Muay Thai, boxing, BJJ, MMA, and cross-training. Three rings plus dedicated BJJ/MMA mats. Education visa sponsor for long-stays — training & visa guide.

Best for: long-stay expats wanting Dongtan/Jomtien south belt without Darkside commute; visa paperwork as part of training plan.

4. Kombat Group Thailand — East Pattaya all-inclusive

Kombat Group Thailand is the premium rural camp: Muay Thai, boxing, MMA, BJJ, Krav, six rings, on-site accommodation. Christian Daghio legacy. Best for 4–12 week fight camps — East Pattaya guide · camps with accommodation.

Best for: fighters who want meals, lodging, and twice-daily sessions without taxi logistics; not ideal for casual one-off drop-ins compared to ALFA or central gyms.

5. Rambaa Somdet M16 — MMA pedigree

Rambaa Somdet M16 in Nong Prue is the personal camp of Thailand's first MMA world champion (Shooto 2009) and Muay Thai legend Rambaa. Cage on site, 100m from Sor Klinmee. Serious strikers who want MMA transitions — not a tourist drop-in factory.

6. Fairtex Pattaya — brand HQ with BJJ block

Fairtex Pattaya remains the global Muay Thai brand headquarters — BJJ classes listed evenings in venue research. Choose Fairtex when MT is primary and BJJ is secondary; choose ALFA when BJJ is primary.

Strength & conditioning for fighters

CrossFit Pattaya @ Jungle Gym (Nong Prue) is Pattaya's only CrossFit affiliate — useful S&C between camp sessions. Also MMA and archery on same site — CrossFit guide.

  • Mon/Wed/Fri AM: Muay Thai pads at camp
  • Tue/Thu PM: Gi or No-Gi at ALFA or Venum
  • Sat: open mat or beach run — running & clubs
  • Sun: pool recovery — swimming pools

What to verify before you enroll

  • Drop-in day pass vs weekly unlimited — multi-discipline camps price differently
  • Gi rental or bring your own kimono (humid climate — wash daily)
  • Open mat etiquette and required mouthguard / rashguard rules
  • Kids class times if family training
  • Education visa minimum months at Rage — contract terms
  • Sparring intensity — ask if "fundamentals" class is no-contact or positional only

Combining BJJ with Muay Thai — realistic weekly load

Holiday fighters often overbook: morning pads five days plus evening rolls four days. Pattaya humidity amplifies fatigue — most sustainable split is three MT sessions + two BJJ sessions for a two-week trip, or alternate weeks if you stay a month. Use commercial gym days for legs-only S&C instead of extra sparring.

Gi laundry: wash kimono daily; skin infections spread in shared academies — shower immediately after class. Open mat etiquette: ask higher belts before repping hard; tap early in heat. Competition visitors should confirm if gym runs IBJJF-style rules or local submission-only rounds before you assume points game.

If visa is the goal, Rage education sponsorship and visa guide matter more than mat prestige — read contract hours required per week before you sign.

FAQ

Where is the only dedicated BJJ gym in Pattaya?

ALFA BJJ Pattaya — first standalone BJJ academy in our files. Multi-discipline gyms also run strong BJJ blocks.

Can I train BJJ without Muay Thai?

Yes at ALFA. At Venum, Rage, or Kombat you can usually buy grappling-focused attendance — confirm at reception.

Best gym for a 4-week fight camp?

Kombat Group for all-inclusive lodging + multi-discipline. Rage for south-side city access + visa sponsor.

Is there a cage for MMA sparring?

Venum, Rage, Kombat, and Rambaa M16 list cage/MMA zones — confirm sparring rules before you assume full contact.

CrossFit with fight training?

Jungle Gym CrossFit on Darkside — schedule around leg-heavy WODs before fight week.

What gear should I bring?

Rashguard, mouthguard, Gi (or buy locally), flip-flops for mat hygiene, and nail clippers — gyms enforce grooming rules.
