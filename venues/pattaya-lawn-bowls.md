# Pattaya Lawn Bowls Clubs

## Overview

Pattaya hosts **the most active lawn bowls scene in all of Thailand** — three dedicated lawn bowls facilities operating across **Banchang, Jomtien, and additional locations**, supporting a thriving expat-driven lawn bowls community with weekly **open roll-up sessions, ladder leagues, charity tournaments, and international competitions**. The combination of **substantial British / Australian / South African retiree population** (lawn bowls is huge in those countries) plus **year-round outdoor playing weather** plus **affordable artificial-rink infrastructure** makes Pattaya genuinely **a top global destination for retiree lawn bowls**.

The three primary facilities differentiate clearly:

- **Banchang Indoor Lawn Bowls** — 6 covered artificial rinks, weather-immune, ฿250/day all-inclusive
- **Paradise Lawn Bowls Jomtien** — 8 outdoor floodlit artificial rinks, spacious clubhouse, advance bookings required
- **The Retreat Lawn Bowls Club** — uniquely offers BOTH grass AND carpet rinks at the same location

Together these clubs run **the Pattaya Sports Club Lawn Bowls League** — a year-round competitive structure that attracts **international visiting bowlers from Australia, UK, New Zealand, and South Africa** for major tournaments.

## What Lawn Bowls Is

### Origins (England, 13th century+)

**Lawn bowls** is one of the **oldest organised sports in the English-speaking world**:

- **Formalized rules** in England by 13th-14th century
- **Sir Francis Drake famously played** at Plymouth Hoe in 1588 before sailing to defeat the Spanish Armada
- **Spread globally** with British colonial expansion to Australia, New Zealand, South Africa, Canada, India
- **Commonwealth Games sport** since 1930

### How It Differs from Petanque

| Feature | Lawn Bowls | Pétanque |
|---|---|---|
| Surface | Grass / artificial green | Hard packed dirt / gravel |
| Bowls | Larger (~1.4 kg each), biased | Smaller metal balls (~700g) |
| Delivery | Rolled along ground | Thrown / lobbed |
| Distance | 30-40m | 6-10m |
| Origin | England | France (1907) |

Both are **endurance-friendly outdoor games** suitable for older players, with similar tactical depth.

### Format

- **2v2, 3v3, 4v4 (rinks)** typical
- **Each player has 2-4 bowls**
- **Goal:** roll bowls closest to the small white "jack"
- **Bowls have biased weighting** — they curve along the ground
- **Game continues until** target score reached (typically 21)

## The Three Pattaya Facilities

### Banchang Indoor Lawn Bowls

**Brand-new covered facility in central Banchang:**

- **6 covered artificial rinks**
- **Probably the fastest greens in Thailand**
- **Weather-immune** — covered structure
- **฿250/day** all-inclusive (rinks, equipment, facilities)
- **Open roll-up sessions:** Monday, Wednesday, Friday at 10:00
- **Various competitions** throughout the year
- **All bowlers welcome**

The ฿250/day pricing is **exceptionally accessible** — for committed bowlers, this is dramatically cheaper than equivalent UK / Australia bowls clubs ($30-60 USD per session typical).

### Paradise Lawn Bowls Jomtien

**Brand-new outdoor facility just off Jomtien 2nd Road:**

- **8 outdoor artificial rinks**
- **Floodlights** for evening play
- **Spacious clubhouse** with full amenities
- **Advance bookings required** — popular venue
- **Ideal for** bowls and bowls-related functions / events
- **Tournament hosting** capability

The 8-rink configuration is **substantial** — larger than most British provincial clubs.

### The Retreat Lawn Bowls Club

**Unique grass + carpet combination at single location:**

- **Both grass rinks AND carpet (artificial) rinks**
- **Choice of surface** for different play styles
- **Established club** with multi-decade operating history
- **Member-driven** community

The grass + carpet option is genuinely rare — most facilities choose one surface.

## Pattaya Sports Club Lawn Bowls League

### How It Works

The **Pattaya Sports Club** (the foundational expat sports organization, founded 1992) runs **a year-round Lawn Bowls League** spanning multiple Pattaya clubs:

- **Round-robin team competition**
- **Multiple divisions** based on skill
- **Weekly fixtures** during league season
- **Trophies / awards** for division winners
- **Charity fundraising** integration

### Competition Calendar

- **High season (Nov-Feb):** peak competition, international visiting bowlers
- **Year-round leagues** with seasonal pace adjustment
- **International tournaments** — visitors from Australia, UK, NZ, South Africa
- **Charity events** raising funds for local Pattaya causes

## Pricing

Pattaya lawn bowls is **dramatically affordable**:

- **Banchang facility:** **฿250/day all-inclusive**
- **Paradise facility:** ~฿200-400 per session (booking-dependent)
- **The Retreat:** member rates lower; visitor rates moderate
- **Annual membership:** ~฿2,000-5,000 for committed bowlers
- **Tournament entry:** modest fees with prizes
- **Equipment:** included or borrowable; own bowls cost ~฿3,000-15,000 for serious players

## Demographics

### Who Plays Lawn Bowls in Pattaya

Strongly **British / Australian / New Zealand / South African expat retirees** — these countries have **substantial lawn bowls traditions** and many retirees relocate to Pattaya specifically because facilities exist to continue playing. Plus:

- **Some Thai players** — the sport has Thai community uptake
- **Mixed-age** — primarily 50+ but not exclusive
- **Both genders** — typical lawn bowls scene
- **Long-stay** — most players are residents, not tourists

### Cultural Significance

For British and Commonwealth retirees in Pattaya, lawn bowls is **deeply cultural** — childhood memories of bowls greens, Sunday afternoons, pub-and-bowls combinations. The Pattaya scene **replicates this lifestyle** in tropical Thailand at a fraction of UK / Australian costs.
