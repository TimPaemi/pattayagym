03 Common questions

MMA in Pattaya — FAQ.

Is there an MMA gym in Pattaya?

Yes. Pattaya has a dedicated mixed martial arts camp, listed in this category, training striking, grappling and full MMA. Many of the city Muay Thai camps and the BJJ academy also cover parts of the MMA skill set.

Can beginners train MMA in Pattaya?

Yes. The MMA camp welcomes beginners as well as experienced and competitive fighters, with coaching scaled to your level. You do not need a martial-arts background to start. Contact the camp about beginner classes and what to bring.

How does MMA training in Pattaya work for visitors?

Like the Muay Thai camps, the MMA camp caters to training tourists with drop-in sessions and longer packages. If you want to combine MMA with Muay Thai or BJJ, the camps and academy are all within the Pattaya area; see also the Muay Thai and BJJ categories.
