★ About this venue

Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort — Lost World Water Park & Fitness

Overview

Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort is Pattaya's flagship Lost World-themed family resort — a 5-star property on 230 metres of private Wong Amat Beach (North Pattaya) featuring 555 sea-facing rooms, suites, and family residences, 8 dining venues, the Lost World on-site water park with multiple slides and pools, the multi-award Spa Cenvaree with 24 treatment rooms, and an active fitness program including a fitness center, 2 tennis courts, and an unusual rock climbing wall. The Lost World theming — prehistoric / jungle / waterfall aesthetic — gives the resort a distinctive identity vs. generic 5-star hotels and makes it one of Pattaya's most family-friendly luxury options.

The Lost World on-site water park is the resort's defining feature — most premium hotel pools cap at 1-2 pools while Centara delivers a sprawling jungle-themed water park with freeform pool, lazy river, multiple slides, an adult pool, jacuzzis, cliff jumping platforms, and the Monsoon Island children's water playground. While not as massive as Ramayana Water Park (184,000 sqm Southeast Asia largest), Centara's on-site water park is genuinely substantial and lets resort guests have water park experiences without leaving the property.

The rock climbing wall is uniquely rare at a Pattaya resort — most family-resort fitness offerings stop at gym + tennis. Centara adds proper rock climbing to the active recreation menu, alongside Pilates, Latin aqua aerobics, and kayaking activity programs.

Lost World — The Themed Water Park

Theme & Aesthetic

The Lost World theme gives the entire resort a jungle / prehistoric / waterfall aesthetic:

  • Volcano-themed pool features
  • Cave bars and dining
  • Tropical-foliage landscaping
  • Themed signage throughout
  • Photo opportunities at distinctive spots

Water Park Components

Freeform Pool

  • Centerpiece swimming area
  • Multiple-depth zones
  • Volcano feature as visual anchor
  • Adult + family mixed use

Lazy River

  • Meandering circuit
  • Tube-based floating
  • Relaxation alternative to active swimming

Waterslides

  • Multiple slides for varied thrill levels
  • Family-friendly intermediate
  • Premium thrill slides for older children / adults

Adult Pool

  • Adults-only zone
  • Quieter ambiance
  • Pool bar service typical

Jacuzzis

  • Recovery / relaxation post-swim

Cliff Jumping Platforms

  • Multi-height jumping platforms
  • Adventurous water park feature
  • Supervised by lifeguards
  • Adult-required for higher platforms

Monsoon Island

  • Children's water playground
  • Shallow splash zones
  • Tipping bucket features
  • Smaller slides for younger kids

The Lost World water park scale is genuinely substantial — guests can spend a full day in the water park alone before exploring the broader resort.

Rock Climbing Wall

The uniquely rare amenity for a Pattaya resort:

  • Indoor / outdoor wall with various routes
  • Beginner-friendly with auto-belay typical at hotels
  • Instructor-led sessions for first-timers
  • Family-friendly with age guidelines
  • Combinable with kayaking + Pilates programs

This is legitimately distinctive — Pattaya climbing scene is otherwise concentrated at Harbor Pattaya Mall (Deep Climbing + Bean Cow). Having a hotel-integrated climbing wall is unusual.

Activity Programs

Daily / weekly scheduled programs:

  • Pilates — group classes
  • Latin aqua aerobics — pool-based fitness
  • Rock climbing — instructor-led
  • Kayaking — beach launches
  • Yoga in the meditation pavilion (likely)

Spa Cenvaree

Cenvaree Brand

Cenvaree is Centara's in-house premium spa brand rolled out across Centara properties globally:

  • Thai-traditional treatment focus
  • Multi-treatment packages
  • Couples-treatment options
  • Yoga / meditation integration

24 Treatment Rooms

The 24 treatment rooms at Centara is dramatically larger than typical hotel spas (most have 6-12). The scale enables:

  • High concurrent guest demand during peak seasons
  • Multiple simultaneous group bookings (wedding parties, corporate retreats)
  • Different treatment specialties in different rooms
  • Couples + singles + groups mixed accommodation

Yoga & Meditation Pavilion

A separate yoga and meditation pavilion beyond the spa proper:

  • Quiet outdoor / semi-outdoor space
  • Group yoga sessions
  • Private meditation
  • Sunrise / sunset programs
  • Visiting yoga teacher workshops

The 230-Metre Private Beach

Wong Amat Beach Setting

Centara's beach is genuinely meaningful:

  • 230 metres of private sandy beach
  • North Pattaya / Wong Amat location (quieter than central Pattaya)
  • Direct hotel access — no public-beach navigation
  • Beach service — loungers, towels, drinks
  • Water sports can launch from beach
  • Sunset views westward
  • Calmer water than central Pattaya bay typically

Pricing (Indicative)

Centara pricing — premium family-resort tier:

  • Standard rooms: ~฿6,000-12,000/night
  • Family residences: ~฿12,000-25,000+/night
  • Suites: ~฿15,000-35,000+/night
  • Water park day pass: ~฿1,500-2,500 (when offered to non-guests)
  • Spa treatments: ~฿2,500-7,000 typical
  • Tennis court rental: ~฿400-800/hour for non-guests
  • Activity classes: typically included for resort guests
