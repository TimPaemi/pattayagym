JumpZ Trampoline Park

Overview

JumpZ Trampoline Park is Pattaya's primary indoor trampoline park — located on the 6th floor of Harbor Pattaya Shopping Mall on Central Pattaya Road, adjacent to Foodland Supermarket. The facility offers high-flying fun with trampolines, games, and family-friendly activities for all ages in a vibrant indoor environment — providing Pattaya's main weather-independent active-play option for families with children alongside the Deep Climbing Gym (also at Harbor Pattaya), Bean Cow Climbing Gym, and Manta Kids' multi-sport activities.

The Harbor Pattaya Shopping Mall location is meaningful: Harbor Pattaya is one of Pattaya's family-friendly mall destinations (alongside Central Festival Pattaya and Royal Garden Plaza), and the 6th floor placement provides indoor air-conditioned, weather-immune family entertainment that's particularly valuable during monsoon-season afternoons (May-October) and hot-season midday heat (March-May). The hours of 10:30 to 19:30 daily accommodate post-school weekday play, weekend morning sessions, and post-shopping evening visits.

For Pattaya families with children — and especially for families staying in central Pattaya hotels who want convenient mall-attached active recreation — JumpZ provides a safe, supervised, indoor trampoline experience that's now globally established as a youth-fitness category.

The Trampoline Park Concept

Modern Trampoline Park Format

Indoor trampoline parks emerged globally in the 2010s — modern facilities typically include:

  • Open jumping area with multiple connected trampolines
  • Wall-to-wall trampolines for safe falling
  • Foam pits — soft-landing pools of cubic foam
  • Dodgeball trampoline courts — combined sport-and-bounce
  • Slam dunk basketball — trampoline-assisted dunking
  • Slacklines / balance beams above trampolines
  • Toddler sections — soft-play for younger kids
  • Aerial silks / circus-style equipment at premium parks

Why Trampoline Parks Work

  • Cardiovascular workout disguised as fun
  • Coordination and balance development
  • Safe environment with padded zones
  • Mixed-age suitability with appropriate sections
  • Weather-immune indoor
  • Birthday party suitable
  • Group activity appeal

JumpZ's specific feature inventory may vary, but the standard trampoline park format applies.

The Harbor Pattaya Shopping Mall Setting

Mall Context

Harbor Pattaya Shopping Mall is one of Pattaya's larger family malls:

  • 6 floors of retail, dining, and entertainment
  • Central Pattaya Road location — accessible from beach hotels
  • Foodland Supermarket as anchor tenant
  • Multiple entertainment venues — climbing, trampoline, cinema, etc.

Why Mall-Attached Matters

  • Convenient parking integrated with mall
  • Multiple activities in single visit (shopping + climbing + trampoline + dining)
  • Air-conditioned throughout — comfortable family day
  • Food courts for meals
  • Restrooms / facilities at mall standards
  • Safety / security of mall environment

For traveling families, mall-attached attractions reduce logistics complexity vs. stand-alone facilities.

Pricing (Indicative)

Pattaya trampoline park pricing typical:

  • 1-hour session: ~฿300-500 per person
  • 2-hour session: ~฿500-800
  • All-day pass: ~฿700-1,200
  • Birthday party packages: ~฿5,000-15,000+ for groups
  • Special events (Friday night Bounce, etc.): premium pricing
  • Trampoline socks: typically required and provided/sold ~฿100-200
  • Group bookings: sometimes negotiable

Safety

Trampoline Park Safety Standards

  • Padded landing zones
  • Spotters / supervisors at busy zones
  • Single-jumper rules for some trampolines
  • Age and weight restrictions for advanced sections
  • Trampoline socks mandatory
  • Liability waivers required
  • First aid on premises

Risk Awareness

Trampoline parks have known injury risk — sprains, fractures, dislocations occur. Modern parks mitigate through:

  • Strict supervision rules
  • Insurance requirements
  • Equipment inspection routines
  • Medical response capability
