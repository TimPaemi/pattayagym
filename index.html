About

Pattaya is one of the world's great training destinations. From legendary Muay Thai camps producing world champions, to a thriving CrossFit and functional fitness scene, to expansive golf, tennis, and watersports — this site catalogs it all in one place. Listings are researched from gym websites, Google Maps, TripAdvisor, expat communities, and on-the-ground sources. Every entry includes a "last verified" date so you know the data is current.

Found an error or want to add your gym? Email hello@pattaya-gym.com and we'll get it updated.
