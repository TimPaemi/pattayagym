BJJ & MMA.

By Tim Paemi · 9 min read · Updated 2026-05-31 · How we rank →

Pattaya is Muay Thai country — but dedicated BJJ academies, cage gyms, and multi-discipline fight camps now cover Gi, No-Gi, MMA, and strength work from Jomtien to the Darkside. This guide maps the real grappling scene.

Pattaya · 158 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

Pattaya is a Muay Thai city first — but the grappling and MMA scene has grown fast. Dedicated BJJ academies, cage gyms, and multi-discipline camps now cover Gi, No-Gi, MMA, and S&C from Jomtien to the Darkside. This guide maps where to train — not every gym with a mat, but the venues where BJJ/MMA is a real programme.

Hub pages: BJJ / Grappling · MMA · Muay Thai · compare venues · plan my trip.

Pattaya BJJ vs MMA — know what you are booking

BJJ-only gyms focus on Gi and No-Gi classes, belt progression, and open mat. MMA gyms combine striking (Muay Thai or boxing) with grappling and often run a competition cage. Multi-discipline camps (Kombat Group, Venum, Rage) let you stack MT mornings with BJJ afternoons — ideal for fight prep or holiday cross-training.

Beginner context: Muay Thai beginners · best for beginners (all sports) · Safety: is Muay Thai safe?

Dedicated BJJ — ALFA BJJ Pattaya

ALFA BJJ Pattaya is Pattaya's first standalone Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu academy — Gi and No-Gi, all levels, pure-BJJ environment without the noise of a multi-sport fight factory. Best pick if you want belt-focused rolling and a community that lives on the mat, not pad rounds.

Multi-discipline fight gyms

Venum Training Camp — Jomtien corridor

Venum Training Camp near Jomtien runs Muay Thai, MMA, BJJ, combat fitness, and kids programmes. Two rings plus cage/mat zone. Strong for visitors who want beach proximity and modern facilities — see Jomtien area guide.

Rage Fight Academy — south Pattaya / Dongtan

Rage Fight Academy stacks Muay Thai, boxing, BJJ, MMA, and cross-training. Three rings plus dedicated BJJ/MMA mats. Education visa sponsor for long-stays — pair with training & visa guide.

Kombat Group — East Pattaya all-inclusive

Kombat Group Thailand is the premium rural camp: Muay Thai, boxing, MMA, BJJ, Krav, six rings, on-site accommodation. Christian Daghio legacy. Best for 4–12 week fight camps — full context in East Pattaya guide and camps with accommodation.

MMA pedigree — Rambaa Somdet M16

Rambaa Somdet M16 in Nong Prue is the personal camp of Thailand's first MMA world champion (Shooto 2009) and Muay Thai legend Rambaa. Cage on site, 100m from Sor Klinmee. Serious strikers who want MMA transitions — not a tourist drop-in factory.

Strength & conditioning for fighters

CrossFit Pattaya @ Jungle Gym (Nong Prue) is Pattaya's only CrossFit affiliate — useful S&C block between camp sessions. Also runs MMA and archery on the same site. East-side base: East Pattaya guide.

Commercial iron for off-camp days: cheapest gyms · 24-hour gyms · Muscle Factory (Pratamnak).

Where to stay for BJJ/MMA

  • Jomtien / south — Venum, Rage, beach recovery. Jomtien guide.
  • East Darkside — Kombat Group, Rambaa, Jungle Gym. East Pattaya guide.
  • Central — ALFA BJJ, shorter commutes to nightlife. Central guide.
