Swimming & pools.

By Tim Paemi · 8 min read · Updated 2026-05-31 · How we rank →

Pattaya swimming means hotel pool day passes, Ramayana water-park days, beach mornings, and dedicated swim schools — not just gym changeroom showers. This guide maps every swim venue type in our directory.

Pattaya · 158 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

Pattaya swimming splits into four buckets: hotel and resort pools (day-pass or guest), public beach and municipal pools, swim schools and lap lanes, and water parks for family days. This guide maps all four — not every pool in Chon Buri, but every swim venue in our 158-venue directory.

Hub: Swimming category · Family-friendly sport · compare · plan my trip.

Hotel & resort pools (day-pass potential)

Five-star strips on Pratamnak, Na Jomtien, and Beach Road sell pool + gym access — policies change seasonally, always call ahead.

  • Fitz Club (Royal Cliff) — luxury racquet, swim, gym tier. Luxury clubs guide.
  • Andaz Jomtien — three pools, onsen, 24h gym. Na Jomtien guide.
  • Hilton Pattaya — infinity pool gym, central festival zone.
  • Centara Grand Mirage — water park + family pools. Childcare & pools.
  • Pattaya Marriott · Renaissance — Na Jomtien beachfront lap and leisure pools.

Water parks & active swim days

  • Ramayana Water Park — largest in Thailand/SEA; full-day Sattahip trip.
  • Columbia Pictures Aquaverse — themed slides, Na Jomtien corridor.
  • Underwater World — aquarium + touch pools (not lap swimming).

South-side planning: Na Jomtien & Sattahip guide · transport: Vehicle Rentals.

Beach swimming & open water

Jomtien and Wong Amat beaches suit morning laps before boat traffic. Open-water prep pairs with dive operators and kite schools (KBA Kiteboarding). Safety: swim parallel to shore, avoid jet-ski lanes, heed red flags in monsoon months.

Swim schools & lap training

Category page lists dedicated swim schools and club pools — filter on /category/swimming/. Combine with gym days: morning pool, afternoon lift at Jomtien or cheapest gyms.

Who should prioritise pools over gyms

  • Family holidays — water parks beat conventional gyms for kids. Family-friendly guide.
  • Low-impact / seniors — pool laps and hotel aqua aerobics. Seniors low-impact.
  • Fight campers on rest days — pool recovery between MT blocks. Training holiday.
  • Triathlon prep — Pattaya Triathlon community + open-water days.
