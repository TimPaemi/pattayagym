Overview

Diana Driving Range is one of Pattaya's most established dedicated outdoor driving ranges — a 30-bay floodlit facility with 200-yard range depth, professional teaching pros for instruction, and an on-site restaurant for pre/post-practice fueling. Located in the Soi Diana area of Central Pattaya (Soi Diana Inn is one of central Pattaya's notable arterials connecting Beach Road and Soi Buakhao), Diana provides walkable practice access for tourists staying in central Pattaya hotels as well as established service for local Pattaya golfers.

The 30-bay scale is meaningful — many Pattaya driving ranges offer 10-20 bays, and the 30-bay configuration ensures minimal waiting even at peak demand. The 200-yard range depth is appropriate for all-club practice including driver work without ball-flight loss to back-stop netting. The floodlit configuration extends practice into evening hours — typically until 22:00 — which is the standard for serious Pattaya practice ranges but distinguishes Diana from morning-only or daylight-only options.

For Pattaya tourists wanting walkable affordable practice, for Pattaya golfers seeking a higher bay-count alternative to single-purpose ranges, and for golfers wanting integrated restaurant amenities for full-day practice sessions, Diana Driving Range fills a meaningful niche between the most basic mat-bay ranges (Pattaya Golf Driving Range, 300 Yards Driving Range) and premium course-attached practice (Siam Country Club Waterside).

The 30-Bay Configuration

Why 30 Bays Matters

Most Pattaya driving ranges have:

  • 10-15 bays at smaller facilities
  • 20-25 bays at typical mid-tier ranges
  • 30+ bays at major/premium ranges
  • 40-50+ bays at very large facilities

Diana's 30 bays put it in the upper tier of dedicated Pattaya driving ranges:

  • Multiple simultaneous players without queue concentration
  • Group bookings (corporate, friends, family) supported
  • Lesson + casual practice can coexist without conflict
  • Reduced waiting during peak afternoon/evening hours
  • Tournament prep capacity

Bay Configuration

Standard outdoor range bay setup:

  • Numbered bays for organization
  • Artificial turf hitting mats with rubber tee
  • Shade structure at premium ranges (varies)
  • Tee height adjustability
  • Ball dispensing on rotation

The 200-Yard Range

Why 200 Yards Matters

Range depth determines what clubs can be effectively practiced:

  • 150 yards: Wedges, short irons (clear to back-stop with 7-iron and shorter)
  • 200 yards: Most irons, fairway woods (drivers may reach back-stop on full shots)
  • 250 yards: All clubs including driver
  • 300+ yards: Even longest hitters can hit driver freely

200 yards is the working minimum for full-club practice — most amateur golfers don't carry driver shots over 200 yards, so they can practice full driver swings without back-stop concerns. Strong amateurs and pros may need to dial back driver at 200-yard ranges.

For most users, 200 yards is sufficient — though serious practice would benefit from longer ranges (300+ yards) where available.

Pricing

Diana's pricing is competitive central Pattaya:

  • Tray (~40-50 balls): ~฿100-200 typical (slightly above some budget ranges)
  • Multi-tray packages discount per-tray
  • Lessons: ~฿800-1,500/hour with resident pros
  • Equipment rental typically ฿100-300 per session
  • Restaurant at standard Thai food pricing

This is mid-tier value — meaningfully cheaper than Golf Hub indoor simulator (~฿500-1,000/hour bay) but slightly more than the cheapest mat-bay ranges.
