Hotel gym day pass.

By TimPaemi · 4 min read · Updated 2026-06-09 · How we rank →

Resort gyms versus commercial chains — when a hotel day pass beats Tony's Gym, which Pattaya hotels admit visitors, and what to ask before you pay.

Pattaya · 157 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

Hotel gyms in Pattaya range from a single treadmill in a three-star lobby to championship-grade fitness at Hilton, Royal Cliff, and Andaz. Many sell day passes to non-guests — useful if your condo gym is closed, you want a pool day, or you are visiting for a week without a membership.

This guide maps which Pattaya hotel and resort venues admit visitors, what you typically get for the fee, and when a commercial gym (Tony's, Anytime) is better value.

Hotel gym vs commercial gym — when each wins

  • Hotel gym wins when you want pool + sauna + towel service in one fee, or your hotel includes gym access anyway.
  • Commercial gym wins for heavy free weights, 24-hour access, and monthly nomad memberships. See best gyms in Pattaya.
  • Luxury club wins for tennis, squash, and coached sport — Fitz Club at Royal Cliff. Luxury clubs guide.

Resort gyms that commonly offer visitor access

Policies change — always call ahead. These venues in our directory are hotel/resort fitness with public-facing contact details:

  • Hilton Pattaya Fitness — Central, high-rise gym with bay views; premium tier.
  • Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort — Naklua, family resort with fitness + water park; day-visitor packages sometimes include facilities.
  • Mövenpick Siam Hotel Pattaya — Pratamnak hill, mid-upper hotel gym.
  • Holiday Inn Pattaya — Central Beach Road, business-traveller gym.
  • Andaz Pattaya Jomtien Beach Resort — Jomtien luxury, wellness-focused.
  • Cape Dara Resort — Naklua boutique, smaller gym footprint.
  • InterContinental Pattaya Resort — cliffside Na Jomtien, spa + fitness.
  • Renaissance Pattaya Resort & Spa — Na Jomtien, Marriott-tier gym.
  • Pattaya Marriott Resort & Spa — Jomtien beachfront.

For pool-only days without a full gym session, see swimming pools guide — several hotels sell swim-only passes.

What to ask before you buy a hotel gym day pass

  • Is the fee gym only or gym + pool + sauna?
  • Are towels and locker included?
  • Guest peak hours — hotel guests get priority on small weight rooms
  • Minimum age and dress code (shoes required on gym floor)
  • Can you pay cash or only charge to room / card?

We do not publish live day-pass prices — hotel rates change seasonally. Message or call using the contact on each fitness venue page.

Pair hotel gym with other sport

Hotel fitness rooms are often cardio-biased. If you need barbells or Muay Thai pads the same week:

  • Morning hotel gym + afternoon Muay Thai session
  • Hotel pool recovery + evening yoga class
  • Week at Fairtex + day-pass Hilton weights — stay-and-train guide

FAQ — hotel gyms in Pattaya

  • Can tourists use hotel gyms in Pattaya? Many resorts sell day passes or facility packages to non-guests, especially in low season. Call the fitness centre directly — front desk staff do not always know current policy.
  • How much is a hotel gym day pass in Pattaya? Typically mid-to-premium tier versus budget commercial gyms — you are paying for pool, towel service, and location. Compare against commercial day passes before committing.
  • Which Pattaya hotel has the best gym? For serious equipment and sport breadth, Fitz Club (Royal Cliff) leads; for high-rise hotel gym with views, Hilton Pattaya. "Best" depends on whether you need weights, pool, or racquet sport.
  • Do I need booking for hotel gym day pass? Walk-in often works on weekdays; weekends and high season (Nov–Feb) — book ahead. Same-day pool-only passes at busy resorts can sell out.
