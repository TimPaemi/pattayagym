Regents International School Pattaya (Sports)

Overview

Regents International School Pattaya is one of the largest and most successful international schools on Thailand's Eastern Seaboard — a Nord Anglia Education school offering International Baccalaureate (IB) and A-Level curricula with a comprehensive sports infrastructure that puts it among the best-equipped international schools in Southeast Asia for student athletics. The school's sports facilities include two competition-standard outdoor swimming pools, a 400-metre all-weather running track, an air-conditioned double-size sports hall, a full-sized football pitch, an Astroturf sports pitch, a fitness centre, and refurbished changing rooms — infrastructure that rivals university-level sports complexes elsewhere.

The school offers students 200+ sport, cultural, musical, technical, and community-based activities — meaning sports are a meaningful pillar of the broader Regents experience rather than an afterthought. Specific sport programs span basketball, football (soccer), swimming, athletics, rugby, cricket, volleyball, tennis, and many others — covering virtually all major team sports plus individual disciplines.

For families relocating to or visiting Pattaya with children of school age — and especially for those prioritising British/international education with strong athletics programs — Regents represents the top tier of the Pattaya international school market. The school's facilities also occasionally host community sports events and school-attached programs for local Pattaya families, though primary use is by enrolled students.

Nord Anglia Education — Parent Network

About Nord Anglia

Nord Anglia Education is one of the world's largest international school networks:

  • Founded 1972 in the UK
  • 80+ schools globally in 30+ countries
  • 80,000+ students under the network
  • Premium positioning — typically among the highest-priced international schools in each country
  • Standardized curriculum + facilities across schools
  • Bulk educator recruitment drawing UK / international teaching talent

For Regents specifically:

  • Joined Nord Anglia network giving it global brand backing
  • Curriculum standardization with sister Nord Anglia schools
  • Resource sharing across schools (faculty exchange, joint events)
  • Reputation premium for international families relocating

Premium Tuition Tier

Nord Anglia schools globally are premium-priced:

  • Regents Pattaya tuition: typically ฿500,000-1,000,000+ per year depending on grade (verify current)
  • Comparable to top international schools in Bangkok, Singapore, Hong Kong, Dubai
  • Justifies premium through facility quality, faculty, curriculum, alumni network

This pricing positions Regents firmly in the upper-tier expat / wealthy Thai family demographic.

The Sports Facilities — World-Class for a School

2 Outdoor Swimming Pools

  • Two competition-standard pools
  • Audience seating for swim meets
  • Used for physical education, swim team training, competition events
  • Suitable for lap swimming, water polo, swim coaching
  • Pool meets hosted with other regional international schools

The two-pool configuration is meaningful — most schools have one pool. Two pools enable simultaneous lap swimming for two different age groups, or competition + training simultaneously.

400-Metre All-Weather Running Track

  • Olympic-standard 400m track distance
  • All-weather surface (typically synthetic / Mondo-style rubber)
  • Used for athletics meets, distance training, sprint training
  • Field events likely supported in track infield (long jump, javelin, discus, shot put)
  • Hosts regional meets between schools

A full 400m track at a school level is rare in Thailand outside Bangkok — Regents's track is meaningful infrastructure investment.

Air-Conditioned Double-Size Sports Hall

  • Indoor sports hall with AC
  • "Double size" = approximately 2 standard basketball courts can run simultaneously
  • Used for basketball, volleyball, indoor football, badminton, P.E. classes
  • AC environment is critical — Pattaya's tropical heat makes indoor sports difficult without cooling

Astroturf Sports Pitch

  • Synthetic grass all-weather pitch
  • Used for football training, hockey, mini-games
  • Year-round playable regardless of monsoon weather
  • Lower maintenance than natural grass

Full-Sized Football Pitch

  • Natural grass full-sized pitch
  • Football matches at competition standard
  • Hosts inter-school competitions
  • Combined with Astroturf for training rotation

Fitness Centre

  • Gym for older students — typically Year 9+ access
  • Cardio + strength equipment
  • Coach-supervised for safety
  • Sports team conditioning integration

Refurbished Changing Rooms

Inter-School Competition

Regents competes in regional and national international school sports leagues:

  • FOBISIA (Federation of British International Schools in Asia) games
  • Bangkok / Pattaya area inter-school events
  • National school championships in various sports
  • Travel sports for representative teams

This inter-school competitive structure gives student athletes meaningful game/match experience.

Community Access

The primary student body is enrolled Regents students. However, some sports facilities periodically host:

  • Sports camps for non-enrolled children during school holidays
  • Community swim programs at the pools
  • Tennis coaching with non-enrolled families
  • External sports events rented to community

For non-enrolled families, direct contact with the school to confirm community access programs is recommended.
