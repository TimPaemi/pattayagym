Overview

Fitz Club is the most decorated, best-equipped, and most-awarded luxury sports club in Pattaya — a 5-star multi-sport facility nested inside the Royal Cliff Hotels Group complex on Pratumnak Hill. The club holds the rare distinction of being the only sports club and gym in Pattaya to win the TripAdvisor Travellers' Choice Award for three consecutive years (2023, 2024, 2025) with a perfect 100% score — putting it in a category by itself among Eastern Seaboard sports facilities.

Where most Pattaya gyms specialise in one or two disciplines, Fitz Club delivers premium-tier facilities across racquet sports, swimming, fitness, wellness, kids' programs, and the new wave of "modern multi-sport" courts (pickleball, futsal, basketball, badminton). Combined with its luxury hotel-resort context — Royal Cliff has 7 swimming pools across the property, two private beaches, the Cliff Spa, multiple award-winning restaurants, and a 1973 heritage as Pattaya's most established luxury hospitality brand — Fitz Club becomes a destination, not just a gym.

For travellers, expats, and Pattaya residents wanting the single most comprehensive sports-and-wellness experience in the region, Fitz Club is the answer.

Parent Brand — Royal Cliff Hotels Group

To understand Fitz Club, you have to understand its parent. The Royal Cliff Hotels Group was established in 1973 and has grown into Pattaya's most institutional luxury 5-star hotel brand — a collection of properties on Pratumnak Hill including:

  • Royal Cliff Beach Hotel — flagship 5-star
  • Royal Cliff Beach Terrace — luxury beach resort
  • Royal Cliff Grand Hotel — premium full-service
  • Royal Cliff Pavilion Hotel — boutique pavilion accommodation

The group operates 7 swimming pools across the properties (including the Sky Aquarium Pool — described as the longest infinity-edge pool in Thailand, overlooking the sea). Two private beaches are reserved exclusively for hotel guests. Multiple award-winning restaurants, Thai cooking classes, and a 5-star spa (Cliff Spa) complete the resort ecosystem.

Fitz Club is the sports and fitness arm of this complex — accessible to hotel guests, members, and day visitors, with the same luxury standards applied to athletic facilities.

Awards & Recognition

The award shelf is exceptional:

  • TripAdvisor Travellers' Choice Award 2023 — first year, perfect score
  • TripAdvisor Travellers' Choice Award 2024 — second consecutive year, perfect score
  • TripAdvisor Travellers' Choice Award 2025 — third consecutive year, perfect score
  • Cliff Spa: TripAdvisor Travellers' Choice Award 2024
  • Royal Cliff Hotels Group: multiple TripAdvisor Travellers' Choice Awards across properties

Triple TripAdvisor Travellers' Choice with perfect score is unmatched by any other Pattaya sports club. This is what "best in class" looks like in measurable terms.

Racquet Sports — The Tennis Specialty

Fitz Club's racquet program is the deepest in Pattaya. The facility hosts the annual Fitz Club Singles Tennis Tournament (returning in 2025/2026 for its 18th edition), the Fitz Club Men's Doubles Tennis Tournament (24-26 April 2026), the Elite ITF Tennis Masters Championship (international ITF-sanctioned event), and the AssetWise Junior Tennis Championship for youth players.

Tennis Facilities

  • 7 floodlit tennis courts — playable day and night
  • Grand Slam standards (court surface and quality)
  • Hours: 07:00 – 20:00 daily (13 hours of accessible tennis daily)
  • Equipment rental: racquets, balls, socks, shoes — all available at reception
  • Professional coaching — including ITF and ATP-credentialed instructors for private and group lessons across all skill levels
  • Tournament hosting credentials — the most-active tournament venue in Pattaya for tennis

Squash

  • 2 indoor glass-back air-conditioned squash courts
  • Glass-back design enables spectator viewing and tournament hosting
  • Air conditioning makes year-round play comfortable (rare in Pattaya)

Table Tennis

  • Dedicated table tennis section
  • Equipment provided

New Multi-Sport Courts

Fitz Club has expanded with new multi-purpose CoolTop technology courts designed for:

  • Pickleball (rapidly growing globally — first-mover Pattaya facility)
  • Futsal
  • Basketball
  • Badminton

The CoolTop surface technology reduces heat absorption — a meaningful benefit for outdoor courts in Thai sun.

Fitness Center

The gym facility is described as state-of-the-art and is the headline reason for the TripAdvisor recognition:

  • Comprehensive cardio equipment (treadmills, ellipticals, bikes, rowers, stair climbers)
  • Comprehensive strength-training equipment — full machine circuit + free weights + Olympic platforms
  • Aerobics dance studio — group classes, dance fitness
  • Jogging track on the property
  • Personal training — certified fitness instructors
  • Professional fitness assessments — body composition, mobility, performance testing
  • Customised training programs — built around assessment data
  • Expert nutrition advice — included in higher membership tiers
  • Muay Thai studio room — dedicated space for Muay Thai training as part of the fitness offering

This puts Fitz Club's gym among the best-equipped commercial fitness centers in all of Eastern Thailand.

Swimming Pool & Aquatics

The Fitz Club pool complex layers seriously into the kids' market:

  • Main swimming pool — full-size, suitable for laps and recreational swimming
  • Kids' pool — separate shallow area for young children
  • Water slides — multiple slides for kids
  • Water scooters — small water-play scooters
  • Pool deck — sun loungers, shaded areas

Combined with the Royal Cliff group's 7 total pools across the properties (including the Sky Aquarium infinity pool), water access at Fitz Club is essentially unlimited.

Wellness — Cliff Spa

Adjoining the sports facilities is Cliff Spa — a TripAdvisor Travellers' Choice 2024 winner in its own right:

  • Panoramic sea-view treatment rooms
  • Highly skilled therapists
  • Full menu of treatments: massage, facials, body scrubs, body wraps, traditional Thai therapies
  • Sauna and steam room access (also available at the Fitz fitness center)

For active travellers wanting an integrated train-and-recover day, Fitz Club + Cliff Spa is essentially purpose-built.

Kids Program

Fitz Club is unusually strong on family/kids:

  • Kids swimming pool with water slides and scooters
  • AssetWise Junior Tennis Championship — annual youth tournament
  • Junior tennis coaching — kids' specific lessons
  • Multi-sport courts appeal to broader kids interests (basketball, badminton, futsal)

For traveling families wanting structured kids activities alongside adult sports/wellness, this is a top choice.

Membership & Day Access

Fitz Club operates a flexible access model:

Hotel Guests

Royal Cliff hotel guests have access included with their stay (depending on rate plan).

Members

Multiple membership tiers available with special privileges including access to Royal Cliff facilities (beaches, additional pools, etc.).

Day Visitors

Welcomed for daily fitness center use — meaningful for travellers staying elsewhere.

First-Time Visitor Trial

A complimentary trial is offered to first-time visitors, including:

  • Gym access
  • Pool access
  • Sauna and steam room
  • Plus 1 hour of either: tennis, pickleball, or squash

This is one of the most generous trial offers among Pattaya sports clubs and is the recommended way to evaluate the facility before joining.

Royal Wellness Sports Package

Royal Cliff offers a bundled hotel-stay + Fitz Club access + wellness program package — popular with sports-tourism travellers wanting an integrated holiday.

Pricing

Fitz Club does not publish detailed membership pricing online — typical for high-end private clubs. Indicative pricing based on Pattaya luxury sports club benchmarks:

  • Day pass / drop-in: ~฿500-1,500 (depending on facilities accessed)
  • Tennis court rental (1 hour): ~฿200-500 per court depending on time/membership
  • Tennis private lesson: ~฿1,500-3,000/hour with professional coach
  • Monthly individual membership: ~฿3,000-8,000 depending on tier
  • Family membership: ~฿5,000-12,000/month
  • Annual membership packages: discounted versus monthly
  • Spa treatments: ~฿1,500-5,000 depending on treatment

For exact 2026 rates, contact directly: +66 38 250 421 ext. 2621 / fitz@royalcliff.com / LINE @royalcliff.

Tournaments & Events Calendar

Fitz Club's tournament calendar is the most active in Pattaya tennis:

  • Fitz Club Singles Tennis Tournament — annual (18th edition recently)
  • Fitz Club Men's Doubles Tennis Tournament — 24-26 April 2026 (returning event)
  • Elite ITF Tennis Masters Championship — international ITF-sanctioned event (worldwide players invited)
  • AssetWise Junior Tennis Championship — annual youth tournament
  • Squash tournaments and league play
  • Multi-sport tournaments at the new CoolTop courts

This makes Fitz Club a destination for serious tennis players — both training and competing.

Location & Getting There

  • Address: Royal Cliff Hotels Group, Pratumnak Hill, Pattaya, Chonburi
  • Pratumnak Hill sits between South Pattaya and Jomtien — 5-10 minutes by taxi from either
  • Walkable from Royal Cliff hotel rooms
  • Access: day visitors enter via Royal Cliff main entrance and proceed to Fitz Club
  • Parking available on the property
  • Distance from Suvarnabhumi: ~125 km / 1.5h
  • Distance from U-Tapao: ~30 km / 30-40 min

Reputation Summary

Fitz Club is the gold standard for premium multi-sport facilities in Pattaya. Visitors and members consistently highlight:

  • Friendly and helpful staff — the 5-star service standard transfers to athletic facilities
  • Excellent equipment selection — both racquet sports and fitness sides
  • Highly-regarded swimming pool — both for swimming laps and family use
  • Cleanliness — meticulously maintained
  • Tournament-quality courts — serious tennis players treat it as their home club
  • Professional coaching — ITF/ATP credentials genuinely deliver

Among long-stay expats and Pattaya residents, Fitz Club membership signals a particular taste level — those wanting the most integrated, most credentialed, most luxury-aligned sports experience available in the city.
