★ About this venue

Coco Fitness

Overview

Coco Fitness is a central Pattaya commercial gym on the 4th floor of Mike Shopping Mall with direct sea views over Pattaya Beach and one of the most accessible mid-budget pricing structures in central Pattaya. The gym is open daily from 07:00-23:00 (16 hours, 7 days a week), accepts walk-ins at ฿299-300/day with towel and locker included, and offers monthly memberships at ฿1,399 — putting it firmly in the value commercial tier alongside Universe Gym, SUN Fitness, and Fitness 7.

For travellers and Pattaya residents wanting a real commercial gym in the central Beach Road / tourist hub area at unbeatable prices, Coco Fitness is one of the most convenient options. The sea views are a genuine quality-of-life feature — running on the cardio machines while looking out over Pattaya Beach is something only a handful of Pattaya gyms offer.

Coco Fitness sits in the value commercial tier with the best central / sea-view location of any gym in this tier.

Location — Mike's Mall 4th Floor

  • Address: 4th floor, Mike's Mall, Pattaya Beach Road
  • Access: elevator just outside the right-hand side of the mall entrance — direct lift to 4F
  • Tourist hub location — Mike's Mall is a major Pattaya landmark on Beach Road
  • Walking distance from hundreds of central Pattaya hotels
  • Sea views — gym faces Pattaya Beach
The Mike's Mall 4th-floor location is unusual — most Pattaya gyms are at street level or in dedicated buildings. The mall-floor placement gives Coco the sea-view advantage but means visitors need to navigate the mall to reach the gym (5-minute path from Beach Road).

Hours & Access

  • Daily 07:00 – 23:00 (16-hour daily window)
  • 7 days a week
  • Walk-in friendly — no membership required for daily access
The early-morning to late-night hours suit:
  • Travellers with flexible schedules
  • Local expats with day jobs
  • Late-evening trainers wanting post-dinner sessions
  • Early-morning trainers wanting pre-beach workouts

Pricing

Coco Fitness publishes transparent rates:

  • Day pass (walk-in): ฿299-300 per person (includes towel service and locker key for ID)
  • 5-visit pass: ฿999 (effective ~฿200/visit)
  • 2-week pass: ฿1,399 (effective ~฿100/day for daily users)
  • Monthly pass: ฿1,399 (effective ~฿47/day for daily users)
Note: The 2-week and monthly are priced identically — ฿1,399 — meaning monthly is the obvious choice if you're committing to more than 14 visits.

Facilities

  • Modern commercial gym setup
  • Free weights — full Olympic barbell + dumbbell rack
  • Resistance machines — full circuit for major muscle groups
  • Cardio machines — treadmills, ellipticals, bikes, rowers, stair climbers
  • Sauna — uncommon at this price tier
  • Clean changing rooms with showers
  • Towel service (included with day pass)
  • Locker key for ID (included with day pass)
  • Personal trainers available — additional fee
  • Sea views — facing Pattaya Beach
The combination of all-new equipment + sauna + sea views at ฿1,399/month is genuinely unusual at this price tier in Pattaya.
