Common questions about watersports / diving in Pattaya

Where can I learn to scuba dive in Pattaya?

No Limit Divers (PADI 5-Star IDC), Adventure Divers, Aquanauts, Real Divers (British family-run IDC), Mermaid's Dive Centre, and Dive Station (SSI). Open-water certification ฿11,000–฿16,000.

When is the best time for watersports in Pattaya?

Diving year-round, best visibility Nov–Apr. Kitesurfing best Nov–Mar. Sailing peaks Dec–Apr. Wakeboarding (Thai Wake Park) year-round.

Can I jet ski safely in Pattaya?

Yes, but use reputable operators. Jet ski scams have been a Pattaya problem historically — book through hotels, established beachfront operators, or use cable wakeboarding (Thai Wake Park) for a safer alternative.

Where to dive near Pattaya?

Coral Island (Koh Larn), Koh Sak, Koh Krok, Koh Khrok, and HTMS Khram (artificial reef wreck). Most operators run half-day and full-day boat trips with 2–3 dives.
