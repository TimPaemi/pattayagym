Common questions

Is Pattaya safe for solo female travelers training in gyms?

Generally yes — hotel-attached gyms, established yoga studios, and family-owned camps are very welcoming. Stick to verified venues with Western reviews and trust your read of each space.

Which Pattaya Muay Thai gyms welcome women?

Sityodtong, Fairtex, Kombat Group, Rage Fight Academy, and Venum Training Camp all run women-friendly programs. Most camps now explicitly welcome female fighters and recreational students.

Are there women-only fitness sessions in Pattaya?

A handful of yoga studios run female-only or trauma-informed sessions. Hotel gyms (Hilton, Centara, Andaz) provide the most reliably comfortable mainstream gym environments.
