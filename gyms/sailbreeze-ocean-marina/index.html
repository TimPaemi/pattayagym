Overview

SailBreeze is Thailand's most experienced sailing school — operating for over 20 years out of Ocean Marina Yacht Club Jomtien, which is Southeast Asia's largest marina with 450+ berths and capacity for boats up to 75 meters / 250 feet. The combination of deepest sailing-instruction pedigree in the country plus the regional flagship marina infrastructure makes SailBreeze the unambiguous top choice for Pattaya residents and visitors who want to learn sailing, charter yachts, or join organized cruising.

The school offers a complete progression pathway: adult sailing classes from absolute beginner, children's sailing programs, IYT (International Yacht Training) certification courses — the global standard for sailing licensing — and "Zero-to-Hero" intensive courses that take complete novices through to RYA/IYT-equivalent qualifications in a single residential program. Beyond instruction, SailBreeze runs the marina's yacht charter operation (hourly, daily, multi-day), mile-builder cruises (where students log nautical miles toward higher certifications while sailing actual passages), and day sailing adventures for those who just want a guided experience without instruction.

For Pattaya travelers wanting sailing experience, SailBreeze + Ocean Marina is genuinely the only legitimate option in the city. The setting on Na Jomtien's calm crescent beach with direct Gulf of Thailand access also makes the marina a destination in itself — the wharf-side restaurants, yacht-watching, and twilight cocktails are part of Pattaya's growing premium tourism offer.

Ocean Marina Jomtien — The Flagship Marina

Scale

Ocean Marina Yacht Club Jomtien is Thailand's premier marina resort complex and Southeast Asia's largest marina:

  • 450+ modern berths
  • Boats up to 75 meters / 250 feet accommodated
  • Direct Gulf of Thailand access
  • Crescent-bay calm water for sheltered berthing
  • Full-service marina — fuel, maintenance, chandlery, dive locker
  • Resort hotel attached — for visiting boat owners and guests
  • Multiple restaurants and bars waterside
  • Sailing club + sailing school integrated

Why Marina Scale Matters for Sailing Students

A large marina infrastructure provides:

  • Diverse boat fleet — students learn on multiple yacht types
  • Real-world traffic — sailing skills tested in busy waters
  • Charter availability — graduate students can rent boats
  • Mile-builder cruises to nearby destinations (Koh Samet, Koh Chang)
  • Maintenance support — boats kept in racing-ready condition
  • Community of sailors — networking, crewing opportunities

Course Catalog

Adult Beginner Courses

Discover Sailing (1-day intro)

  • No experience needed
  • Basic principles — wind, sail trim, helm
  • Day sail under instructor supervision
  • Decision point — pursue further instruction or one-shot experience
  • Cost: ~฿2,500-4,000

IYT Crew (Bareboat Skipper progression)

  • 2-3 day course
  • Theory + practical
  • First IYT certification level
  • Foundation for further qualifications

IYT Bareboat Skipper

  • 5-7 day course
  • Full bareboat charter qualification
  • Allows independent yacht rental without crew
  • Internationally recognized

Children's Sailing

  • Optimist class — small single-handed dinghies
  • Age 7-15 typical
  • Group lesson format
  • Progression to youth racing

IYT Certification

International Yacht Training is the dominant global sailing certification body alongside RYA (UK) and ASA (US):

  • Recognized in 60+ countries
  • Multi-tier progression from Crew → Master Yachtsman
  • Required for commercial yacht crew and charter
  • Maintained through continuing education

Zero-to-Hero Intensives

  • Complete novice to qualified skipper in single residential program
  • 2-4 week duration depending on level targeted
  • Combines ground school, dinghy training, yacht time, ocean passage
  • Most ambitious program offered

Yacht Charters (Non-Instruction)

  • Hourly charters for casual sailing
  • Day charters for full-day adventures
  • Multi-day cruises to Koh Samet, Koh Chang
  • Bareboat available for IYT-certified clients
  • Skippered charter for non-certified
  • Catamaran option for groups / parties

Mile-Builder Cruises

  • Sailing actual passages (e.g., Pattaya → Koh Chang → Cambodia coast)
  • Logged nautical miles count toward higher certifications
  • Real ocean experience beyond bay sailing
  • Multi-day commitment
  • Group cost-shared typically

Why Sailing in Pattaya?

Geographic Advantages

  • Calm Gulf of Thailand — sheltered vs. open Andaman
  • Year-round sailing — no winter freeze
  • Multiple offshore destinations — Koh Larn, Koh Samet, Koh Chang
  • Established marina infrastructure
  • Cheaper than Phuket — flagship Thai sailing destination but more expensive

Best Months

  • November-March: northeast monsoon, consistent winds, dry weather
  • April-October: southwest monsoon, lighter winds, occasional storms
  • Pattaya is workable year-round but November-March is peak
