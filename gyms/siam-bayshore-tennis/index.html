★ About this venue

Siam Bayshore Resort Tennis Courts

Overview

Siam Bayshore Resort Pattaya features six championship-level outdoor tennis courts at its 5-star resort property near Walking Street — making it the largest hotel tennis facility in central Pattaya and one of the few hotel-attached tennis venues that opens court access to the general public rather than restricting to hotel guests only. The resort spans over 20 acres with 270 elegantly appointed rooms and suites alongside the tennis facility, 2 outdoor pools and a children's pool with waterslide, Lotus Spa, free valet parking, and a serene retreat setting just steps from Pattaya's vibrant nightlife.

The 6-court layout matches Greta Sport Club's count (the only Pattaya facility with 6 covered courts), but Siam Bayshore's courts are outdoor championship-level rather than covered — making them weather-dependent but no less tournament-grade in surface and dimensions. The 600-metre walking distance from Pattaya Night Walking Street is meaningful: most central-Pattaya tennis players have to commute to East Pattaya, Greta in Sattahip, or other distant facilities. Siam Bayshore puts 6 quality courts within walking distance of the city's tourist core.

For Pattaya tennis players staying at central hotels, Siam Bayshore Resort Pattaya is the most accessible quality court access in the city — and for Siam Bayshore guests, the 6 courts are a meaningful resort amenity competing with FITZ Club and Greta in court count.

Hotel Profile

  • 20+ acre property in central Pattaya
  • Walking Street 600m away
  • Sand beach 15-min walk

Resort Amenities Beyond Tennis

  • 2 outdoor pools + children's pool with waterslide
  • Sun loungers and pool decking
  • Free valet parking
  • Limousine / town car service
  • Buffet breakfast (surcharge)
  • Lotus Spa offering massage, body wrap, body scrub
  • Multiple restaurants and bars

Why It Matters for Tennis Players

The integrated amenity bundle gives tennis players:

  • Pool recovery post-match
  • Spa services for sore muscles
  • Restaurant dining without leaving property
  • Professional service standards (5-star)
  • Family-friendly for non-playing companions
  • Walking Street proximity for evening entertainment

The 6 Tennis Courts

Court Configuration

  • 6 outdoor championship-level courts
  • Hard court surface (acrylic-painted concrete)
  • Open daily 07:00-18:00
  • Public access in addition to hotel guest priority
  • Standard ITF tennis dimensions with painted lines
  • Net systems with regular maintenance

Why 6 Courts Matters

  • Multiple simultaneous matches without booking pressure
  • Tournament hosting capability
  • League play possibility
  • Coaching and free play simultaneous without conflict
  • Beginner instruction + advanced match play can coexist

Limitations

  • Outdoor only — weather-dependent (rain, hot midday sun)
  • No covered courts — Greta's 6 covered remain unique
  • Not ITF-rated specifically — Greta's Plexipave has the only ITF Pattaya rating
  • Hotel guest priority typical at booking conflicts

Hours

Daily 07:00-18:00 — 11 hour operating window:

  • Morning (07:00-10:00): beat the heat, ideal play
  • Midday (10:00-15:00): hot, less demand
  • Afternoon (15:00-18:00): peak demand, social play

The 18:00 close is meaningful — most outdoor tennis facilities offer evening play with floodlights to ~22:00. Siam Bayshore's courts may not be floodlit, requiring closing before sunset.

Public Access

Unlike many hotel tennis facilities that restrict to guest use, Siam Bayshore allows public access to courts daily:

Booking

  • Direct booking via the hotel
  • Hotel guest priority during peak times
  • Day pass tennis access for non-guests
  • Equipment rental typically available

Pricing (Indicative)

Hotel-tier tennis pricing for non-guests:

  • Court rental (hour, non-guests): ~฿400-700
  • Court rental (hotel guests): included or discounted
  • Coaching: ~฿1,000-2,000/hour
  • Equipment rental: ~฿100-300 per session

Coaching

  • Resort-employed tennis pros typical at 5-star hotels
  • Private lessons available
  • Group clinics seasonally
  • Junior development programs likely
