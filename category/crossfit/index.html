03 Common questions

CrossFit / Functional in Pattaya — FAQ.

Is there a CrossFit gym in Pattaya?

Yes. Pattaya has a CrossFit-affiliate and functional-fitness gym, listed in this category, running classes built around varied functional workouts. Several of the city regular gyms also offer functional and HIIT training.

Can visitors drop in to a class?

Yes. Functional-fitness affiliates typically welcome dropping-in travellers. Contact the gym ahead to confirm class times and the drop-in fee. Beginners can usually join a foundations or scaled class.

Do I need CrossFit experience?

No. Functional-fitness classes are scaled to the individual, so beginners can train alongside experienced members. Tell the coach your experience level and any injuries before the session.
