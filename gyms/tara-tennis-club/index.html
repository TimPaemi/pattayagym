★ About this venue

Tara Tennis Club

Overview

Tara Tennis Club is one of the newest dedicated racquet-sport facilities in Pattaya — a 2022-built multi-sport club featuring three newly resurfaced hard courts (one covered, two outdoor) with full tennis + pickleball + basketball capability and an on-site Gourmet Primo cafe. The 2022 construction date is meaningful: where most Pattaya tennis facilities have decades-old surfaces with patchy maintenance, Tara's courts are still effectively fresh — playing surfaces still close to factory specifications, line paint still crisp, net hardware still tight.

The club operates from 06:00 to 22:00 daily — a full 16-hour window that accommodates early-morning runners-and-hitters, midday pros training, evening league play, and late-night casual social hits. With one covered court, Tara offers partial weather protection that lets play continue during rain (a meaningful advantage in Pattaya's monsoon-season afternoons), though it's not the fully covered 6-court complex that Greta Sport Club delivers.

For Pattaya residents and visitors who want fresh modern hard courts with multi-sport flexibility, walkable convenience, and the rare 16-hour operating window, Tara Tennis Club is among the city's strongest racquet club options. The pickleball capability is particularly notable — Pattaya's pickleball community has grown rapidly post-2020, and Tara's dedicated court markings make it one of the few venues purpose-built for the sport.

The 2022 Build

The 2022 construction date provides several advantages over older Pattaya tennis facilities:

Fresh Hard-Court Surfaces

  • Newly resurfaced acrylic-paint hard courts — typical 5-7 year resurface cycle, so still well within prime
  • Crisp line paint — visible, accurate
  • Even bounce consistent across the surface
  • Net systems still tight and functional

Modern Lighting

  • Recent floodlight installations — typical lux levels for night play
  • Energy-efficient LED likely (post-2020 era construction)
  • Glare-free design for player comfort

Multi-Sport Layout

  • Tennis lines primary
  • Pickleball lines painted on the courts (smaller, fits within tennis dimensions)
  • Basketball court likely shared with one of the tennis configurations

This multi-sport painting is a 2020s-era design philosophy — courts that serve multiple sports rather than single-purpose painting.

Three Hard Courts — Layout

Court 1 — Covered

  • Roof structure providing rain protection
  • Outdoor sides for ventilation
  • Most-prized booking slot during afternoon thunderstorms
  • Premium for league/coaching use

Courts 2 & 3 — Outdoor

  • Standard hard court configuration
  • Floodlit for evening play
  • Open-air ventilation (helpful in heat)
  • Weather-dependent — rain stops play

Pickleball Capability

Why Pickleball Matters

Pickleball has been the fastest-growing sport in the world since 2020:

  • Easier to learn than tennis
  • Lower physical impact — appeals to older players
  • Doubles format social — natural community building
  • Court is smaller — fits inside a tennis court (4 pickleball courts ~= 1 tennis)
  • Equipment cheaper than tennis
  • Rules simpler

Pattaya's pickleball community has grown rapidly post-2020, mirroring global trends.

Tara's Pickleball Setup

  • Painted lines on tennis courts for pickleball play
  • Net adjustment for pickleball height (lower than tennis)
  • Equipment availability — paddles, balls
  • Coaching available for pickleball
  • Members and walk-ins welcome

Basketball Capability

The mention of basketball capability is unusual for a tennis club — it suggests:

  • One court may have basketball lines painted overlapping tennis
  • Hoop installation (permanent or movable)
  • Half-court or full-court depending on space
  • Pickup play for residents seeking basketball — rare in Pattaya

Coaching & Equipment

Coaching

  • Tennis instructors for hire
  • Private lessons typically 1 hour
  • Group clinics seasonally
  • Junior development programs
  • Pickleball coaching for the growing community

Pro Shop

  • Some equipment for purchase in the on-site pro shop
  • Racquets, strings, balls, accessories
  • Limited inventory vs. dedicated tennis retailers

Gourmet Primo Cafe

The on-site Gourmet Primo Cafe adds a meaningful social-and-fueling layer:

  • Sandwiches — pre/post-match fueling
  • Drinks — water, electrolytes, coffee, soft drinks
  • Coffee — important for early-morning players
  • Social space for post-match conversations
  • Family-friendly vibe encouraging non-playing partners to wait comfortably

This integration of cafe + courts creates a club-day experience rather than just a court rental.

Hours

Daily 06:00-22:00 — a full 16-hour operating window:

Why 16 Hours Matters

  • Early morning (06:00-09:00): beat the heat, hit before work
  • Midday (09:00-15:00): less popular due to heat but available
  • Late afternoon (15:00-19:00): peak social play time
  • Evening (19:00-22:00): post-work casual + league play

This dawn-to-late-evening availability is meaningful — many Pattaya facilities close earlier (20:00-21:00 typical), limiting the working-day-followed-by-late-tennis demographic.

Pricing (Indicative)

Tara is positioned in the mid-tier accessible range:

  • Court rental (hour): ~฿200-400 typically
  • Covered court premium: maybe ฿100-200 surcharge
  • Coaching (private): ~฿800-1,500/hour
  • Group clinic: ~฿400-700 per session
  • Annual membership: ~฿8,000-15,000 (varies)
  • Equipment rental: ~฿100-200 per session
  • Pickleball play: typically slightly cheaper than tennis
