About

Pattaya is one of the world's great training destinations.

From legendary Muay Thai camps producing world champions, to a thriving CrossFit and functional fitness scene, to expansive golf, tennis, watersports and diving — this site catalogs it all in one place.

Pattaya gym FAQ

What is the best Muay Thai gym in Pattaya?

The best Muay Thai gym depends on your level and goals. Sityodtong (1959) and Fairtex (1971) are the most decorated heritage camps. Venum Training Camp is the modern beachside flagship. See our full ranked guide: Best Muay Thai gyms in Pattaya.

Are there 24-hour gyms in Pattaya?

Yes — several. Anytime Fitness Pattaya (key-fob 24/7 access, 2 locations), Jetts Fitness, and Fitness 7 all run round the clock. Most 5-star hotel gyms (Hilton, Andaz, Mövenpick) also offer 24-hour fitness for guests. See 24-hour gyms in Pattaya.

How much does a Pattaya gym membership cost?

Budget gyms run ฿1,500–฿3,000/month. Mid-tier chains like Anytime Fitness and Jetts sit ฿2,000–฿4,000/month with no contract. Muay Thai camps charge ฿4,000–฿15,000/month depending on prestige. 5-star hotel clubs run ฿฿฿฿. Most venues also offer drop-in rates ฿200–฿500. Browse the cheapest gyms guide.

Is Pattaya good for beginner Muay Thai?

Excellent — many camps have dedicated beginner programs in English. Kombat Group, Battle Conquer, and Fairtex are all explicitly beginner-friendly with structured fundamentals classes. See best for beginners.

Are there family-friendly gyms in Pattaya?

Many. Look for kids' programs, family pools, and supervised group classes. Centara Grand Mirage has a water park, Fitz Club runs kids' tennis and swimming, and several Muay Thai camps run kids' classes. See family-friendly Pattaya.
