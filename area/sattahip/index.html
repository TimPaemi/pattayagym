Sattahip / Far South — gyms & sport venues

Sattahip district — south of Jomtien — hosts Pattaya's biggest premium destinations: Greta Sport Club's 6 covered tennis courts, the world's largest water park (Ramayana, 184,000 sqm), Cartoon Network Amazone / Columbia Pictures Aquaverse, Chee Chan Golf Resort with Buddha mountain views, and several premium golf courses.
