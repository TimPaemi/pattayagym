Jomtien Beach.

South Pattaya beachfront. Family-friendly, long-stay-friendly, and the strongest concentration of watersports and yoga in the city.

34 venues · Jomtien Beach · Pattaya · Updated 2026-05-25

01 About this neighborhood

What Jomtien Beach is for.

Jomtien Beach is the southern half of greater Pattaya — a 6 km strip of beachfront stretching from Pratamnak Hill down to Na Jomtien. The vibe is calmer than Central Pattaya: long-stay condos rather than short-stay hotels, families and digital nomads rather than nightlife tourists, and a much higher density of yoga studios, watersports operators, and beachfront fitness than anywhere else in the city.

If you're booking a 1-3 month Pattaya stay and want to combine training with beach access and walkable food/shopping, Jomtien is the default neighborhood.

Walkable beach + condo zone, slower pace, plenty of co-working coffee.

03 Transport & access

How to get there.

Jomtien Beach Road runs north-south along the beach; Thappraya Road and the Pattaya Klang baht-bus (songthaew) route run parallel inland. The Soi numbering goes south from the Dolphin Roundabout. The baht-bus from Central Pattaya to Jomtien is ~฿10-20.

04 Landmarks & orientation

Where you are.

  • Jomtien Beach (6 km public beach, free access)
  • Pattaya Park Tower
  • Dolphin Roundabout (the northern gateway to Jomtien)
  • Ocean Marina Yacht Club (Sattahip end)
  • Boat departures to Koh Larn from Bali Hai Pier (technically Central, but the natural ferry origin for Jomtien stays)
05 Starter pick

If you're new here.

If you want the easiest first day: start at a Jomtien beachfront yoga studio for a morning class, then a Muay Thai session at one of the nearby camps, then dinner on the beach. Most accommodation here is walkable to all three.

04 Common questions

Jomtien Beach — FAQ.

Is Jomtien a good base for a Pattaya training trip?

Yes, especially for longer stays. Jomtien is the calmer, southern half of Pattaya - long-stay condos rather than short-stay hotels, families and digital nomads rather than nightlife. If you want to combine training with beach access, walkable food and a slower pace over a one to three month stay, Jomtien is the natural choice.

What sports is Jomtien best for?

Jomtien has Pattaya strongest concentration of watersports and yoga. Beachfront access means kitesurfing, paddleboarding, jet skis, parasailing and the boats out to Koh Larn, and the area has the most dedicated yoga and Pilates studios in the city, plus hotel resort gyms and family pools along the beach.

How do I get around Jomtien?

Jomtien Beach Road runs north to south along the beach, with Thappraya Road inland and a baht-bus (songthaew) route connecting to Central Pattaya for roughly 10 to 20 baht. Soi numbers count up heading south from the Dolphin Roundabout. A motorbike is handy for the long Jomtien strip but not essential.

Is Jomtien good for families?

Yes. The beach is calmer and the area quieter than Central Pattaya, with family pools, swimming clubs, kids classes and resort hotel gyms. It is one of the easier parts of Pattaya for a combined family sport-and-beach trip.
