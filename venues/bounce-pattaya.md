BOUNCE is the **Australian-founded international trampoline park franchise** that pioneered the modern indoor-trampoline-arena concept worldwide — interconnected wall-to-wall trampolines, slam-dunk lanes, dodgeball courts, performance airbags, and ninja warrior obstacle courses. The Pattaya branch sits on the **6th floor of Harbor Pattaya Mall**, the indoor-sport hub at the central end of Pattaya Klang Road that also hosts **Deep Climbing Gym** (10m wall) and **Bean Cow Climbing Gym** (bouldering + lead) on the same building's upper floors.

For families with kids in Pattaya — especially during the rainy season or hot midday hours when outdoor activities are punishing — BOUNCE delivers what indoor commercial spaces are designed for: **2-3 hours of structured high-energy exercise** in air-conditioned safety, with kids supervised by trained crew.

## Programming

BOUNCE runs:

- **General jump sessions** — pay per hour or per package
- **BOUNCE FIT classes** — adult fitness on trampolines (HIIT-style)
- **Kids' classes** — supervised programmes for younger jumpers
- **Birthday parties** — full party packages including cake, food, and dedicated space
- **School holiday programmes** — extended camps during Thai school breaks
- **Ninja Warrior course** at some larger BOUNCE locations

For Pattaya residents, BOUNCE FIT is an interesting alternative to traditional gym cardio — bouncing trains the deep stabilisers, raises heart rate fast, and is genuinely fun.

## Safety & rules

BOUNCE globally maintains high safety protocols — age zoning, jumper-per-trampoline limits, mandatory grip socks (often included or sold separately), no shoes, no jewellery, height/weight restrictions on certain attractions, and trained crew on the floor. Pattaya follows the same standards. Sign a waiver before entering; under-18s require parent or guardian signature.

## How to visit

The fastest path: head to **Harbor Pattaya Mall** on Pattaya Klang Road (central Pattaya), take the lift to the 6th floor. Buy a session at the BOUNCE counter, sign waivers, change into grip socks, follow the crew briefing, jump.

For Pattaya families planning a half-day at Harbor Mall, the natural multi-sport stack is:

- **BOUNCE** (kids, 1–2 hours)
- **Deep Climbing Gym** (adults / older kids, 1–2 hours)
- **Bean Cow Climbing Gym** (bouldering, 1 hour)
- **Mall food court / restaurants** for lunch

The whole Harbor building was designed to be Pattaya's indoor-active-family hub, and stacking it with BOUNCE + climbing makes the 6th-7th floor the most fitness-dense single location in the city.
