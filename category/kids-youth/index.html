03 Common questions

Kids / Youth Sports in Pattaya — FAQ.

What sports can children do in Pattaya?

Quite a lot: football academies, swimming lessons, trampoline parks, tennis and multi-sport coaching, plus kids programmes at several gyms and camps. This category collects the venues focused on children and youth.

What age groups are catered for?

It varies by venue. Some football academies and coaching programmes run from around age three up through the teens; others focus on specific age bands. Check each venue page for its age range or contact it directly.

Are the programmes open to visiting or short-stay families?

Many are. Trampoline parks and several academies take drop-ins and short courses, which suits holidaying families, while structured academies may prefer a term commitment. Contact the venue to ask about short-stay options.

Are sessions run in English?

Several youth programmes have English-speaking or international coaches, particularly the football academies and international-style coaching. This varies between venues, so confirm with the specific one.
