★ About this venue

BOUNCE is the Australian-founded international trampoline park franchise that pioneered the modern indoor-trampoline-arena concept worldwide — interconnected wall-to-wall trampolines, slam-dunk lanes, dodgeball courts, performance airbags, and ninja warrior obstacle courses. The Pattaya branch sits on the 6th floor of Harbor Pattaya Mall, the indoor-sport hub at the central end of Pattaya Klang Road that also hosts Deep Climbing Gym (10m wall) and Bean Cow Climbing Gym (bouldering + lead) on the same building's upper floors.

For families with kids in Pattaya — especially during the rainy season or hot midday hours when outdoor activities are punishing — BOUNCE delivers what indoor commercial spaces are designed for: 2-3 hours of structured high-energy exercise in air-conditioned safety, with kids supervised by trained crew.

Position in Pattaya market

Pattaya's kids/youth fitness landscape includes:

  • AF Academy — UEFA-A football academy
  • Kombat Group kids' Muay Thai — combat-sport intro
  • Manta Kids — swim academy at IG Center
  • Pattaya Sports Club — multi-sport youth
  • Centara Grand Mirage water park — themed adventure land
  • Cartoon Network Amazone — branded water park
  • Pattaya Park (Tower Jump) — original 1989 family complex
  • BOUNCE Pattaya — trampoline park (this venue)
  • HarborLand Terminal 21 Pattaya — indoor playground (different building)

BOUNCE differentiates by being pure fitness-energy in a contained indoor format. Kids actually exercise — bouncing burns serious calories, and 2 hours on the trampolines is a real workout. Parents can drop kids at BOUNCE while they hit Deep Climbing or Bean Cow upstairs in the same building, then everyone meets for lunch in the mall.

What's inside

Standard BOUNCE attractions adapted to the Pattaya space:

Zone What it is
Free Jump arena Wall-to-wall interconnected trampolines for general jumping, flips, and play
Slam Dunk lane Trampoline-assisted basketball dunking — bounce in, dunk, repeat
Dodgeball court Trampoline-floor dodgeball — kids and family format
The Big Bag Performance airbag — jump from height onto a giant inflated cushion
Wall Run Trampoline + angled wall — run up and bounce off
Battle Beam Bounce-and-joust beam — friendly combat balance challenge
Mini BOUNCE Toddler-specific zone (typically ages 4–7 or younger)

Specific zone availability varies by location and renovation cycles — verify the current Pattaya layout on arrival.

Programming

BOUNCE runs:

  • General jump sessions — pay per hour or per package
  • BOUNCE FIT classes — adult fitness on trampolines (HIIT-style)
  • Kids' classes — supervised programmes for younger jumpers
  • Birthday parties — full party packages including cake, food, and dedicated space
  • School holiday programmes — extended camps during Thai school breaks
  • Ninja Warrior course at some larger BOUNCE locations

For Pattaya residents, BOUNCE FIT is an interesting alternative to traditional gym cardio — bouncing trains the deep stabilisers, raises heart rate fast, and is genuinely fun.

Safety & rules

BOUNCE globally maintains high safety protocols — age zoning, jumper-per-trampoline limits, mandatory grip socks (often included or sold separately), no shoes, no jewellery, height/weight restrictions on certain attractions, and trained crew on the floor. Pattaya follows the same standards. Sign a waiver before entering; under-18s require parent or guardian signature.

Pricing

Standard BOUNCE pricing model:

  • First hour: ฿400–฿600
  • Second hour add-on: discounted
  • Grip socks: ฿80–฿120 (required, kept by jumper)
  • Family / multi-pack discounts typical
  • Birthday party packages: tiered, usually ฿7,000–฿20,000+ depending on size

Verify current Pattaya rates on their website or Facebook page.

Pros

  • International franchise standards — equipment, safety, programming proven globally
  • Air-conditioned — relief from Pattaya heat / monsoon
  • Same building as climbing gyms — multi-sport day for active families
  • Real fitness output — kids get a genuine workout, not just screen-substitute play
  • Birthday party programming — popular with Pattaya expat-resident families
  • Adult BOUNCE FIT classes — alternative cardio option for parents
  • Mall-attached — food, restrooms, parking handled by the building infrastructure

Cons

  • Mall-attached pricing — not the cheapest option vs. a public playground
  • Peak weekend crowds can shorten effective jump time
  • Age restrictions at some zones — check before bringing toddlers
  • Grip socks required — additional cost first visit (kept after, so lower over time)
  • No pool / outdoor element — pure indoor trampoline

Best for

  • Families with kids ages 4–14 wanting an indoor-energy outlet
  • Rainy-season Pattaya activity days (Jun–Oct)
  • Hot midday hours (12:00–15:00) when outdoor play is brutal
  • Birthday parties and group events
  • Adults wanting BOUNCE FIT cardio variety
  • Combined "kids at BOUNCE / parents at Deep Climbing or Bean Cow" multi-sport days

Not best for

  • Toddlers under 4 (verify minimum age first)
  • Outdoor-loving families preferring beach play
  • Budget-only families (public play areas are cheaper)
  • Anyone with recent injuries, pregnancy, or trampoline-contraindicated conditions

How to visit

The fastest path: head to Harbor Pattaya Mall on Pattaya Klang Road (central Pattaya), take the lift to the 6th floor. Buy a session at the BOUNCE counter, sign waivers, change into grip socks, follow the crew briefing, jump.

For Pattaya families planning a half-day at Harbor Mall, the natural multi-sport stack is:

  • BOUNCE (kids, 1–2 hours)
  • Deep Climbing Gym (adults / older kids, 1–2 hours)
  • Bean Cow Climbing Gym (bouldering, 1 hour)
  • Mall food court / restaurants for lunch

The whole Harbor building was designed to be Pattaya's indoor-active-family hub, and stacking it with BOUNCE + climbing makes the 6th-7th floor the most fitness-dense single location in the city.
