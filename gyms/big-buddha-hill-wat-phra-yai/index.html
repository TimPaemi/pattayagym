Big Buddha Hill (Wat Phra Yai) — Pratamnak Landmark + Stair
Climb.

Pattaya iconic 18m golden Buddha at peak of Pratamnak Hill. Free entry. Accessed via 100+ step grand staircase (genuine cardio climb). 360 degree panoramic Pattaya Bay + Jomtien views. Sunrise/sunset photography. Active temple with resident monks. Dress code applies.
