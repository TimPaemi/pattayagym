Common questions about fitness / gym in Pattaya

Which Pattaya gym is open 24 hours?

Anytime Fitness Pattaya (key-fob 24/7), Jetts Fitness, and Fitness 7 all run round the clock. Most 5-star hotel gyms (Hilton, Andaz, Mövenpick) also offer 24-hour fitness for guests.

How much is a gym membership in Pattaya?

Budget gyms ฿1,500–฿3,000/month. Mid-tier no-contract chains (Anytime Fitness, Jetts) ฿2,000–฿4,000/month. Hotel club day-passes ฿500–฿1,500. Drop-in rates ฿150–฿400.

Do Pattaya gyms have month-to-month options?

Yes — most chains and many independent gyms offer no-contract monthly memberships. Anytime Fitness and Jetts are explicitly contract-free.

Are there female-friendly gyms in Pattaya?

Yes — chains like Anytime Fitness and hotel club gyms (Hilton, Andaz, Mövenpick, Royal Cliff Fitz Club) consistently rank well for safety and comfort. Most boutique studios are female-friendly by default.
