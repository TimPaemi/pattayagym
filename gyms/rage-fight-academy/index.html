Rage Fight Academy

Overview

Rage Fight Academy is one of Pattaya's strongest multi-discipline combat sports academies, located in south Pattaya — just five minutes from Dongtan and Jomtien beaches. The academy combines Muay Thai, Boxing, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, MMA, Cross Training, Fitness and weight-loss programming under one roof, and is particularly well-regarded for its BJJ program alongside its Muay Thai. With three full-sized rings, a spacious matted BJJ/MMA area, and an active competition fight team, Rage is a serious training environment that still welcomes complete beginners.

A notable practical advantage: the academy supports Muay Thai education visas for foreigners wanting to legally extend their stay in Thailand to train long-term — a meaningful differentiator for travelers planning multi-month commitments.

Disciplines

  • Muay Thai — full traditional program, twice-daily
  • Western Boxing — dedicated boxing program
  • Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu — strong BJJ curriculum on dedicated mats
  • Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) — integrated cage/mat training
  • Cross Training — combined conditioning across disciplines
  • Fitness & weight-loss programs — tailored programming for non-fighter clients
  • Private coaching — available across all disciplines

Facility

The academy is built across multiple training zones:

  • Two dedicated Muay Thai and Boxing areas
  • Three full-sized rings
  • Spacious matted BJJ and MMA training zone
  • Heavy bags, banana bags, pad stations
  • Strength and conditioning equipment
  • Showers, lockers, changing rooms
  • Friendly, welcoming social area

The "two areas + three rings + matted zone" setup means multiple disciplines can run simultaneously without crowding — uncommon in Pattaya outside the resort camps.

Schedule

Mon-Sat with daily multi-discipline programming. Specifics confirmed at ragefightacademy.com/training/, but the typical structure is:

  • Morning: Muay Thai, Boxing, BJJ classes in parallel
  • Afternoon/evening: Muay Thai, MMA, BJJ rolling sessions
  • Sundays generally rest; private bookings possible

Trainers

The coaching team includes expert instructors across each discipline, with reviewers consistently praising:

  • Detailed technical expertise (not just cardio sessions)
  • Patient teaching approach — beginner-friendly atmosphere
  • Knowledgeable BJJ coaches in particular receive strong praise
  • English-speaking across the staff
  • Active fight team competing in local and international events — fighters available as training partners

Pricing

Reasonable mid-tier pricing:

  • Drop-in single session: ~฿500 (~$15)
  • Monthly unlimited: ~฿8,500 (~$259)
  • Weekly packages: available
  • Private 1-on-1: premium rate, all disciplines
  • Long-stay packages with visa support — quoted on request
  • Family / multi-person discounts — sometimes available

This positions Rage in the mid-tier: significantly less than Kombat Group's all-inclusive packages, slightly higher than the budget Thai camps but with substantially more discipline variety.

Education Visa Support

A major practical feature: Rage Fight Academy can sponsor Muay Thai Education Visas (Non-ED) for foreigners wanting to extend their stay in Thailand legally for long-term training. This is a real differentiator — many camps don't offer this, and arranging it independently is bureaucratically painful. For students planning 3-12 month training commitments, this alone can be the deciding factor.

Accommodation

No on-site accommodation, but the gym is in a well-developed part of south Pattaya / near Jomtien with abundant hotel, condo, and apartment options at every price point. Many long-stay students rent monthly condos in the Jomtien area for ~฿8,000-15,000/month.

Location & Getting There

  • Area: South Pattaya, near Dongtan and Jomtien beaches
  • 5 min from Dongtan Beach, slightly more from main Jomtien Beach Road
  • Easy songthaew or taxi access
  • Pattaya central is a 10-15 minute drive

Pros

  • Genuinely strong BJJ program — rare in Pattaya
  • True multi-discipline academy (Muay Thai + BJJ + Boxing + MMA + Fitness)
  • 3 full-sized rings + dedicated BJJ/MMA mat zone
  • Expert coaches with patient teaching reputation
  • Education Visa sponsorship for long-stay students — major practical advantage
  • Active fight team for training partners
  • Beach proximity (Dongtan, Jomtien)
  • English-speaking staff
  • Welcoming to beginners and foreigners

Cons

  • No on-site accommodation
  • Less of an old-school Thai-camp pedigree (newer establishment vs. Sityodtong/Fairtex)
  • Mid-tier pricing — not the cheapest in Pattaya

Reputation Summary

Held in high regard especially for BJJ travelers — one of the few Pattaya gyms where BJJ practitioners consistently report positive experiences and serious technical instruction. Combined with strong Muay Thai and the visa-support service, Rage is a top pick for long-stay multi-discipline athletes.

Best For

Multi-discipline athletes (especially BJJ + Muay Thai cross-trainers) • Long-stay students wanting visa support • Travelers staying in Jomtien/Dongtan • Beginners wanting patient, structured instruction • Active fight-team aspirants

Not Best For

Lineage purists looking for old-school Thai camp authenticity • Travelers wanting on-site accommodation • Pure budget seekers (cheaper budget Thai camps exist)
