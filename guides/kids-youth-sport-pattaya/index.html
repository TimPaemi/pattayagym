Kids & youth sport.

By Tim Paemi · 8 min read · Updated 2026-05-31 · How we rank →

Pattaya kids sport means football academies from age 3, trampoline parks at Harbor Mall, and Muay Thai programmes at major camps — nine dedicated venues in our directory plus cross-category options. This guide maps each path for relocating and holiday families.

Pattaya · 158 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

Pattaya kids sport splits into three lanes — football academies (the densest category), indoor trampoline parks at Harbor Mall, and Muay Thai kids programmes at major camps. This guide maps all nine listed kids/youth venues plus realistic add-ons for relocating families.

Hub: Kids & youth category · Family-friendly · Childcare & pools · compare · plan my trip.

Football academies — the main cluster

AF Academy Football School accepts children from age 3 — youngest in Pattaya — with multi-location training (Naklua, Jomtien, Pratamnak, Central). Russian, English, and Thai coaching; indoor and outdoor fields. Phone +66 96 078 8308.

  • FAST PRO Football Academy — UEFA-A licensed European coach, ages 4–15, Horseshoe Point + IP Soccer Club locations. Premium technical programme.
  • Planet Football (FC Planet) — beginner squads through competitive academy and senior team, ages 5+ to adults.
  • Rusich Club Pattaya — Russian-language HSIF Thailand programme, ages 5–16, after-school rhythm.
  • Pattaya City Football Academy — Bronze-level Academy License (Football Association of Thailand), ages 6–14.

Quick pick: youngest starters → AF Academy · Russian-speaking family → Rusich · premium European coaching → FAST PRO · competitive pathway → Planet Football.

Trampoline & indoor play — Harbor Mall stack

Harbor Pattaya Mall (Central Pattaya Road) stacks kids activities on upper floors — same building as Deep Climbing Gym (8F) and climbing/community options.

  • BOUNCE Pattaya — international franchise, 6F. Free Jump arena, slam dunk lane, dodgeball, performance airbag, Mini BOUNCE for toddlers, BOUNCE FIT adult cardio.
  • JumpZ Trampoline Park — 6F Harbor Mall, AC family sessions daily 10:30–19:30.

Half-day family stack: morning trampoline → lunch in mall → afternoon climb at Deep → evening parent Muay Thai while kids rest. Pool add-on: swimming & pools guide.

Muay Thai for kids (cross-category)

Many Muay Thai camps run kids programmes — Fairtex, Venum, Kombat Group routinely train children. Verify age policies on each camp page under Muay Thai category. Safety context: Is Muay Thai safe?

Not listed under kids-youth but relevant: Tarzan Adventure kids zipline course (Sattahip), EasyKart kids tier on Thepprasit.

International school sport

Regents International School Pattaya runs basketball, football, swimming, athletics, rugby, and cricket — for enrolled students only, not public drop-in. Relocating families: Pattaya School Guide.

Parent training windows

Parents training Muay Thai or gym while kids are occupied — pair football academy drop-off with morning pads, or Harbor Mall trampoline session with parent iron at central chains. Full playbook: childcare & family pools.
