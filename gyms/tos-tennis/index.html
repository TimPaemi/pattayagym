Overview

TOS Tennis Club (also operated as TOS Tennis Academy) is the closest tennis facility to Jomtien Beach — a 2-court tennis venue tucked inside the Baan Suan Lalana residential development with swimming pool, locker room, and training gym integrated on-site. The location advantage is meaningful: where most Pattaya tennis facilities cluster in central Pattaya City or out toward Sattahip, TOS Tennis sits steps from Jomtien Beach in the south Pattaya beach belt where many traveling tennis players actually stay.

The 2-court setup is the most modest in the Pattaya tennis scene — Greta has 6 covered courts, Tara has 3, Pattaya Tennis & Badminton Inter Club has 5. But the integrated swimming pool + locker room + training gym add a small-club lifestyle layer that pure tennis-only facilities don't offer. Players can train, shower, lift weights, swim, and recover in a single residential club setting.

The current court conditions are fine for beginner play but would not be recommended for advanced play — the courts need resurfacing (planned at some point per the operators). For Jomtien-based travellers and Baan Suan Lalana residents who prioritise convenience, beach proximity, and integrated club amenities over premier-tier playing surfaces, TOS Tennis serves the niche.

Baan Suan Lalana — The Setting

What Baan Suan Lalana Is

Baan Suan Lalana is a residential development near Jomtien Beach:

  • Mid-tier condominium / villa community
  • Mixed Thai + expat residents
  • Leisure-lifestyle positioning — pool, gym, tennis on-site
  • Walking distance to Jomtien Beach
  • Jomtien Beach Road area vibe

The TOS Tennis facility serves both Baan Suan Lalana residents (who likely have member rates) and non-residents booking court time.

Why Beach Proximity Matters

For tennis-playing travellers staying at Jomtien Beach hotels:

  • Walking-distance courts vs. 30+ minute drive to Greta or Khao Mai Kaeo
  • Beach time + tennis combination in a single day
  • No transportation hassle
  • Convenience for casual play

For Pattaya tennis players, this walking-from-beach access is genuinely unique among the city's tennis facilities.

The 2 Courts

Configuration

  • 2 hard tennis courts (acrylic / concrete surface)
  • Outdoor
  • Standard tennis dimensions with painted lines
  • Lighting probable for evening play
  • Open-sky configuration — no covered courts

Court Condition Caveat

The courts need resurfacing — confirmed by tennis-community guides. The operators have plans to resurface "at some point" but timeline isn't published.

For players, this means:

  • Older surface paint with possible patches and wear
  • Bounce inconsistency in worn areas
  • Line paint potentially faded
  • Acceptable for casual + beginner play
  • Not recommended for tournament prep or advanced play
  • Future improvement when resurfacing happens

For honest evaluation: TOS Tennis is a "good enough" tennis facility, not a premium one. The convenience and amenity integration are the value, not the courts themselves.
