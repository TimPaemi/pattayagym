★ About this venue

Pattaya Bowl

Overview

Pattaya Bowl is a North Pattaya ten-pin bowling alley at 463/78 M.9, Soi Regent Marina, Srinakorn Centre, Pattaya 2nd Road — operating with a generous 10:00-02:00 daily schedule that makes it one of Pattaya's late-night-friendly indoor recreational venues. The 16-hour operating window is meaningful: most Pattaya bowling alleys close 22:00-23:00, leaving Pattaya Bowl as one of the rare options for post-Walking-Street late-night bowling (alongside other extended-hours venues like Major Cineplex Bowl and S.F. Strike Bowl).

Pattaya hosts multiple bowling alleys — including S.F. Strike Bowl, P.S. Bowl, Wave Bowl at Royal Jomtien Resort, and Pattaya Bowl. The market serves a mix of Thai families (bowling is popular in Thai middle-class entertainment), tourist groups, expat residents, and birthday/group bookings. Bowling is a low-skill-floor activity — anyone can participate regardless of fitness level — making it suitable for mixed-age, mixed-ability group outings (corporate, family, friends).

For Pattaya travellers and residents wanting late-night indoor recreation, group entertainment, weather-independent activity, or family bowling outings, Pattaya Bowl delivers a straightforward bowling experience in a North Pattaya / 2nd Road location convenient to many central hotels and condos.

Bowling in Thai Recreation Culture

Why Bowling Matters in Thailand

Bowling has been a popular Thai middle-class entertainment for decades:

  • Indoor air-conditioned environment — heat-resistant
  • Low physical skill floor — anyone can play
  • Family and group friendly — multi-generational participation
  • Birthday and corporate group activity
  • Date activity — popular Thai dating spot
  • Affordable vs. premium club entertainment
  • Weather-independent — works year-round

Most Thai cities of meaningful size have bowling alleys integrated into shopping malls or standalone facilities. Pattaya's bowling scene reflects this national pattern.

The Late-Night Hours Advantage

Why 02:00 Close Matters

Pattaya is a 24-hour entertainment city — Walking Street, beer bars, restaurants all operate late into the night. Most recreational venues close earlier:

  • Most malls: 22:00
  • Most restaurants: 23:00-01:00
  • Most fitness gyms: 22:00-23:00
  • Walking Street nightlife: until 02:00-03:00
  • Pattaya Bowl: until 02:00

This makes Pattaya Bowl a meaningful post-dinner / pre-clubbing / late-night-with-friends option — bowling at 23:00-01:30 is genuinely possible.

Late-Night User Profile

  • Friends groups post-dinner, pre-clubbing
  • Tourist groups with extended evening plans
  • Date-night couples
  • Birthday parties running late
  • Bachelor / hen parties
  • Russian / European tourists with night-active travel patterns

Standard Ten-Pin Bowling Format

Equipment Provided

  • Bowling balls in varied weights (typically 6-16 lbs)
  • Bowling shoes rental — sanitary slip-resistant
  • Lane assignments with electronic scoring
  • Lane bumpers for children's games (typically optional)

Game Format

  • 10 frames per player standard
  • Pin reset automatic between rolls
  • Strike (10 pins one ball) = next two rolls' value as bonus
  • Spare (10 pins two balls) = next roll's value as bonus
  • 300 perfect game = 12 strikes
  • Average score: 70-90 for casual players, 150-180 for skilled

Pricing (Indicative)

Typical Thai bowling alley pricing:

  • Per game per person: ~฿80-150 typical
  • Shoe rental: ~฿30-50 per session
  • Multiple-game packages discount per-game
  • Group bookings sometimes negotiable rate
  • Member cards at major chains

For a typical group of 4-6 friends:

  • 2 games each: ~฿800-1,500 total
  • Plus shoes: add ~฿120-300
  • Beer / snacks: add typical Thai pricing

Amenities

Standard Thai bowling alley amenities typically include:

  • Bar / drinks — beer, soft drinks, water
  • Snack bar — basic Thai food, fries, popcorn
  • Karaoke rooms at some alleys
  • Pool tables at some alleys
  • Arcade games at some alleys
  • Birthday party rooms for celebrations
