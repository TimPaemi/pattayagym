Common questions about swimming in Pattaya

Where can the public swim in Pattaya?

Pattaya Public Pool (Jomtien) ฿20–฿100. Hard Rock Pool (largest free-form in SE Asia, day-pass). Cartoon Network Amazone water park. Most 5-star hotel pools offer day-pass access.

Are there free pools in Pattaya?

Most public pools are very low-cost (฿20–฿100), not free. Pattaya Beach and Jomtien Beach are free for ocean swimming. Some condo pools open to non-residents on day-pass arrangements.

Are there swim lessons for kids in Pattaya?

Yes — Hard Rock, Centara Mirage, Andaz, and several dedicated swim schools run kids' programs. Group lessons typically ฿400–฿800/session.

What is the largest water park in Pattaya?

Ramayana Water Park (184,000 sqm, 26 slides) is the largest in Pattaya area. Cartoon Network Amazone is the second-largest themed park. Centara Mirage has on-site water park access for hotel guests.
