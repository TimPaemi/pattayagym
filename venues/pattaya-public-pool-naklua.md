# Naklua Public Swimming Pool

## Overview

The Naklua Public Swimming Pool is **one of only two municipal public swimming pools in Pattaya** — providing **the most affordable, accessible, working-population swimming option in the city**. Located in the **Naklua area north of central Pattaya**, the pool serves **the Thai resident community, working-class families, and budget-conscious expats** who want **lap swimming, recreational pool access, and basic swim infrastructure without the premium pricing of hotel pools or club memberships**.

Pattaya — a city of **300,000+ residents plus millions of annual tourists** — has remarkably few municipal swimming facilities. The Naklua public pool and its sister facility (the **South Pattaya / Jomtien public pool**) collectively serve the entire **non-tourist non-hotel non-private-club swimming demand** of the city. This makes them **critical community infrastructure** — even if they're modest in scale and amenities compared to private alternatives.

For Naklua-area residents and visitors who want **a functional swimming pool at minimal cost**, the public pool is the natural choice. The pool likely operates with **standard Thai municipal pool pricing** (typically ฿20-100 per visit depending on age and resident status) — dramatically cheaper than any private alternative.

## What "Public" Means in Thai Context

### Municipal Operation

Thai public pools are typically:

- **Operated by Pattaya City Municipality** (Tessabaan Mueang Pattaya)
- **Funded partially by tax revenue** + small entry fees
- **Open to anyone** — Thai residents, expats, tourists
- **Family-friendly** with designated kids' areas
- **Lifeguard-supervised** during peak hours
- **Maintained to basic safety standards**

### Pricing Standard

Thai municipal pools charge:

- **Adult Thai resident:** ~฿20-50 per visit
- **Adult non-resident:** ~฿50-150 per visit
- **Child:** ~฿10-30 per visit
- **Senior discount** typical

These rates are **dramatically cheaper than any private pool option** — Hard Rock Pool day pass (~฿1,000-2,500), First Serve Sports Club monthly membership (~฿1,200), even mid-tier hotel pool day passes (~฿300-700).

## Naklua — The Geographic Setting

### Naklua Context

**Naklua (นาเกลือ)** is the **Thai community area north of central Pattaya**:

- **More residential / less tourist** than central Pattaya
- **Mix of Thai middle-class housing and growing expat condos**
- **Local fishing port** — historical Pattaya economy origin
- **Quieter pace** than Walking Street area
- **Naklua Beach** — separate from Pattaya Beach proper
- **Walking Street** ~5 km south
- **Songthaew (baht bus) accessible** from anywhere on Pattaya-Naklua Road

### Why Public Pool Fits Naklua

The Naklua location aligns with public pool demographics:

- **Local Thai community** drives daily use
- **Lower tourist density** = quieter pool experience
- **Working families** with children
- **Long-stay Thai-friendly expats** integrated with local life
