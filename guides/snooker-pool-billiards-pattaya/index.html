Pool & snooker.

By Tim Paemi · 6 min read · Updated 2026-05-31 · How we rank →

Pattaya billiards means air-conditioned halls on Second Road, multi-table venues like SF Strike Bowl, and casual league nights at Megabreak — low-impact social sport for expat groups and rainy-season backup.

Pattaya · 158 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

Pattaya pool and snooker is mostly late-night social sport — air-conditioned halls on Second Road, resort-adjacent tables in Wongamat, and multi-table venues with food service. Not a cardio category, but it fits a training week when you want low-impact evenings or rainy-season backup.

Hub: Clubs & social sport · Digital nomad fitness · compare · plan my trip.

Where to play pool & snooker

SF Strike Bowl — flagship multi-table hall

SF Strike Bowl is Pattaya's headline pool hall — multiple billiards tables, snooker setups, and a bowling lane stack on one floor. Budget–mid tier; popular with expat groups. Pair with Central Pattaya guide if you base on Second Road.

Megabreak & Pattaya Bowl

  • Megabreak Pool Hall — dedicated pool tables, hourly hire, casual league nights. Central–south corridor.
  • Pattaya Bowl — bowling plus pool tables; family-friendly rainy-day option near tourist zones.

Club lounges with tables

Pattaya Sports Club and some hotel bars run pool tables for members or guests — verify table fees on arrival. Racquet-first clubs: tennis & badminton guide.

How pricing usually works

  • Hourly table hire — ฿100–฿300/hour typical for standard pool; snooker tables sometimes higher.
  • Peak nights — Friday–Saturday may need booking at SF Strike during high season.
  • Equipment — house cues included; serious players bring their own tip/chalk.

Fit into a training week

  • Mon–Thu: Muay Thai or gym mornings — day pass guide
  • Fri: pool night at SF Strike or Megabreak (low impact, social)
  • Sat: hash or run — running & clubs guide
  • Sun: recovery swim — pools guide

Who this is for

  • Digital nomads — evening sport without booking a court
  • Groups — four players rotate on two tables
  • Rainy season — indoor backup when beach runs cancel
