01 About this neighborhood

What Pratamnak Hill is for.

Pratamnak Hill (also spelled Pratumnak — both are correct) is the elevated ridge separating Central Pattaya from Jomtien Beach. It's where the highest-end resorts cluster: Hilton, Royal Cliff Hotels Group (Fitz Club), Mövenpick Siam, Centara Grand Mirage. The streets are quiet, the views are panoramic over Pattaya Bay, and the vibe is much more "wellness retreat" than "tourist strip".

For sport, this is the luxury end of Pattaya — top-tier fitness clubs with multiple racquet sport courts, full-service spas, championship-class swimming, and on-call personal trainers for any discipline. Day passes and short stays are pricier but the experience is materially different from the chain gyms downtown.

03 Transport & access

How to get there.

Pratamnak Soi 4, Pratamnak Soi 5, and Pratamnak Soi 6 run up the hill. Best accessed by taxi/Grab; the songthaew network is thinner here. The hill is between Beach Road (Central Pattaya) and Jomtien Beach Road — both are 5-10 min by car.

04 Landmarks & orientation

Where you are.

  • Big Buddha Hill / Wat Phra Yai (free staircase climb, panoramic Pattaya view)
  • Khao Phra Tamnak viewpoint
  • Royal Cliff Hotels Group (luxury cluster)
  • Cosy Beach (small, hotel-adjacent)
  • Buddha Hill viewpoint at the south end
05 Starter pick

If you're new here.

If the trip is wellness/luxury: book Hilton Pattaya, use the Fitz Club day pass for racquet sports + swimming, climb Big Buddha Hill at sunrise. That's a complete Pratamnak day.

04 Common questions

Pratamnak Hill — FAQ.

Is Pratamnak a good base for training?

Yes. Pratamnak Hill sits between Central Pattaya and Jomtien and is one of the most fitness-dense neighbourhoods in the city, popular with long-stay residents who want training close to home without the central nightlife.

What sports is Pratamnak best for?

Pratamnak is strongest for gyms and fitness, with a good spread of training options on and around the hill, plus quiet roads and the Buddha Hill stair climb for outdoor cardio. Its central position makes venues in both Central Pattaya and Jomtien easy to reach.

How do I get around Pratamnak?

Pratamnak Hill connects Central Pattaya and Jomtien, and baht-buses run the loop between them. The hill is walkable in parts, but a motorbike helps with the gradient and for reaching Jomtien-side venues.

Who is Pratamnak best for?

Long-stay visitors who want a quiet, central, fitness-focused base - close to both halves of Pattaya without being in the middle of either.
