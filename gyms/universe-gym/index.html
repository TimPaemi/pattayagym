★ About this venue

Universe Gym

Overview

Universe Gym is one of the largest commercial fitness gyms in Pattaya and a long-time favorite of both Pattaya residents (locals + nomads + expats) and visiting fitness travellers. Located in Welcome Town, just off Sukhumvit Road in North Pattaya, the multi-level facility features at least five distinct training zones, offering everything from modern resistance machines and cardio equipment to free weights, functional fitness rigs, and ample floor space. With day passes starting at ฿140, monthly memberships at ฿1,400, and couples pricing that genuinely offers value, Universe Gym is one of the best-priced serious commercial gyms in Pattaya.

For travellers and Pattaya residents wanting a real, fully-equipped commercial gym at unbeatable prices — without the boutique-tier markup of Fitz Club or chain markup of Jetts/True Fitness — Universe Gym is the default value pick.

Universe Gym sits in the value commercial tier — meaning fully-equipped real gym at value pricing. This is the best price-to-quality ratio in Pattaya commercial fitness for visitors who don't need luxury amenities.

Location & Hours

Address

Welcome Town, 47/113 Moo 9, Nongprue, Bang Lamung District, Chonburi 20150

The gym is located in Welcome Town — a residential and commercial complex just off Sukhumvit Road in North Pattaya. The location is convenient for:

  • North Pattaya residents (walking or short scooter)
  • Naklua-area visitors (~10 min by taxi)
  • Central Pattaya visitors (~15-20 min by songthaew/taxi)
  • Long-stay condo residents in the Welcome Town / Pattaya Klang corridor

Operating Hours

  • Monday-Friday: 08:00 – 22:00 (14 hours)
  • Saturday-Sunday: 09:00 – 22:00 (13 hours)

Late-evening hours suit expat residents with day jobs and travellers who want post-beach evening sessions.

The Facility — Five Training Zones

Universe Gym is multi-level and divided into at least five distinct training areas:

Ground Floor Areas (Two)

  • Main floor — most modern equipment, full circuit of resistance machines, plus cardio (treadmills, ellipticals, bikes, rowers, stair climbers)
  • Free weight area — Olympic barbells, plates, full dumbbell rack, benches

Upstairs Areas (Three)

  1. Additional cardio equipment — overflow capacity for peak times
  2. Floor space — yoga, stretching, abs work, mobility, body-weight training
  3. Functional fitness zone — large rig with miscellaneous functional equipment (battle ropes, kettlebells, plyo boxes, TRX, medicine balls)

The multi-zone layout means Universe Gym rarely feels crowded even during peak times. With 5+ distinct areas, members can find space for whatever workout they're doing.

Equipment Quality

Reviews describe the equipment as "medium age, mostly in good condition with plenty of it available". This is honest — Universe Gym is not a brand-new facility with the latest 2025-2026 machines. It's a long-standing operation with well-maintained equipment of varying vintage. The ground floor's modern machines are the most recent additions; the functional rig upstairs is robust and varied.

For most users, this is a feature: proven equipment, plenty of it, all working — without paying boutique prices for the latest electronic touchscreen treadmills.

Pricing

Standard Rates

  • Day pass: ฿140
  • 10-day pass: ฿1,200 (effective ~฿120/day — slight discount)
  • Monthly: ฿1,400 (effective ~฿47/day if used 30x — exceptional value)

Couples Pricing

Universe Gym offers couples discounts that meaningfully reduce per-person cost:

  • Couples day pass: ฿210 (effective ฿105/person — 25% saving)
  • Couples 10-day pass: ฿1,800 (~฿90/person/visit)
  • Couples monthly: ฿2,100 (effective ฿35/person/day — best value in the gym)

For travel partners, the couples monthly at ฿2,100 total is one of the best gym deals in Pattaya — less than what a single hotel-gym day pass costs at premium hotels.

  • Among the largest commercial gyms in Pattaya — 5+ distinct training zones

Cons

  • No spa, sauna, or pool — pure gym, not a wellness resort

  • No personal training listed prominently — bring your own program

Best For

  • Functional fitness enthusiasts — upstairs functional rig
  • Cardio-focused travellers — multiple cardio zones
  • Daily-pass users — ฿140 walk-up is among the cheapest in Pattaya
  • Travellers staying in North Pattaya / Welcome Town / Naklua

Not Best For

  • Beach-adjacent travellers without transport (other gyms more central)
