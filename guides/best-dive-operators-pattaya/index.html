Common questions

How much does Open Water dive certification cost in Pattaya?

PADI Open Water typically ฿11,000–฿16,000 over 3–4 days. SSI similar. Includes manual, gear, boat trips, and pool/confined-water sessions.

Where do Pattaya dive boats go?

Coral Island (Koh Larn), Koh Sak, Koh Krok, Koh Khrok for shallow reefs. HTMS Khram (artificial reef wreck, ~30m) for advanced divers. Most operators run half-day or full-day boats with 2–3 dives.

Are Pattaya dive sites good for beginners?

Yes — most reef dives are 8–18m with calm, clear water year-round. Visibility peaks Nov–Apr (15–25m). Gulf of Thailand currents are mild compared to Andaman Sea destinations.
