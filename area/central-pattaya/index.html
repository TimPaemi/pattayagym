Central Pattaya — gyms & sport venues

Central Pattaya — the Beach Road / Walking Street / Soi Buakhao corridor — is dense with 24-hour commercial gyms, hotel pools, walk-in Muay Thai, indoor climbing at Harbor Pattaya Mall, and tourist-friendly fitness operators. Most central hotels are within 10 minutes' walk of multiple training options.
