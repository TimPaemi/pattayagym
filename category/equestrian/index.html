Common questions about equestrian in Pattaya

Where can I ride horses in Pattaya?

Thai Polo & Equestrian Club (Asia's largest polo + equestrian operation, 250 hectares, 250+ horses) and Horseshoe Point Resort (1,500 acres, multi-discipline equestrian + adventure resort).

Can beginners take riding lessons?

Yes — both major venues offer lessons for first-time riders through advanced equestrians, plus polo introduction programs. Book in advance to confirm coach availability.

Is there polo in Pattaya?

Yes — Thai Polo & Equestrian Club hosts professional polo tournaments and offers polo introduction days for spectators and participants. Pattaya is one of Southeast Asia's leading polo destinations.
