# Greta Sport Club Pattaya

## Overview

Greta Sport Club is **the Pattaya region's premier indoor tennis facility** — a **6-court fully-roofed tennis complex** integrated into the **Greta Resort** in Na Jomtien (Sattahip District), approximately **18 kilometres south of Walking Street and central Pattaya**. The club's defining feature is what no other Pattaya racquet venue offers: **all six tennis courts are completely covered by a permanent roof structure**, protecting players from **direct sun, hot-season heat (March-May), and rainy-season storms** — eliminating the typical Thai-tennis problem of postponed sessions due to weather.

The courts use the **Plexipave surface system, certified by the International Tennis Federation (ITF) at Category 3**, with **500 lux uniform glare-free floodlight illumination** for evening play. The combination of **roof cover + ITF-certified surface + tournament-grade lighting** puts Greta Sport Club in a **distinct quality tier from any other Pattaya tennis facility**, where most courts are open-air with patchy maintenance.

The club is **embedded in the Greta Resort property** — meaning members and their guests have access to the **swimming pool, gym, spa, restaurant, sauna, and beach tennis courts** alongside the main tennis offering. For tennis players relocating to or visiting Pattaya who **prioritise court quality and weather-protected play**, Greta Sport Club is unambiguously the top choice.

## Tennis Court Specifications

### Court Surface

**Plexipave** is a globally recognized hard court surface system used at:
- **US Open and ATP tournaments** worldwide
- Numerous **professional and ITF-grade venues**
- Known for **consistent ball bounce, durability, and pace control**

Greta's installation is **rated ITF Category 3**, which on the ITF court pace classification system corresponds to **Medium** pace — neither slow (clay) nor fast (grass), the most common professional indoor/hard court speed. This rating is meaningful: it means **the courts have been independently audited and certified to professional consistency standards**.

### Roof Cover

All 6 courts are **permanently covered by a roof structure**:

- **Sun protection** — vital in tropical Thailand where direct sun makes 11am-3pm play punishing
- **Rain protection** — eliminates monsoon-season cancellations
- **Strong-light reduction** — players read ball trajectory better without sun glare
- **Heat moderation** — enclosed but ventilated structure, cooler than open-air

This is genuinely **rare in Thailand** outside Bangkok. Most provincial tennis courts are open-air; Greta's covered configuration makes daily reliable play possible year-round.

### Floodlighting

- **500 lux uniform illumination** — meets ITF night-play standards
- **Glare-free design** — pole positioning and shielding optimise for play, not periphery
- **Evening play extends well past sunset** — usable until ~22:00

### Court Number

**6 courts** is enough for:
- Multiple simultaneous group classes
- Tournament hosting (round-robin format)
- Pickup and league play scheduling
- Less waiting time than 2-3 court venues

## Beach Tennis

In addition to the 6 main tennis courts, Greta Sport Club has **beach tennis courts** — a sport that's grown rapidly in Italy and Mediterranean Europe, and is gaining traction in Thailand:

- **Played on sand** with paddle-style racquets and depressurized tennis balls
- **Doubles format** typical
- **Closer to padel/badminton hybrid** than traditional tennis
- **Italian heritage** of the sport aligns with Greta's brand
- **Lower impact** than hard-court tennis — popular with older players and beginners

This makes Greta one of the **few venues in Pattaya offering legitimate beach tennis facilities**.
