# AF Academy Football School

## Overview

AF Academy is **a Russian-network children's football academy with a Pattaya branch** alongside its original locations in **Yaroslavl and Moscow, Russia**. The academy operates **two training fields in Pattaya** — an **outdoor field in South Pattaya** and an **indoor field in Central Pattaya** — making it **the only Pattaya kids football operation with weather-independent training capability** for its central sessions. Programming spans from **age 3 (basic ball control) through teenagers age 14+ (game tactics)**, with structured age-band curricula at each level.

For Pattaya families — both Thai families and the large Russian expat community based in Pattaya — AF Academy is one of the leading kids football choices, alongside **Pattaya City Football Academy** (Bronze License Academy of the Football Association of Thailand) and **Rusich Club** (separate Russian-language football operation).

## Network — Russia + Pattaya

AF Academy operates an **international children's football academy network**:

- **Yaroslavl, Russia** — original / flagship
- **Moscow, Russia** — major Russian capital branch
- **Pattaya, Thailand** — Southeast Asian outpost serving Russian expats and local families

The Russian-network heritage means:

- **Russian-language coaching** available
- **Russian football pedagogy** — typically emphasises technical skills, ball control, structured drilling
- **Connection to the Russian children's football ecosystem** (tournaments, talent pathway)
- **Cultural fit for Pattaya's large Russian demographic**

## Programs by Age Band

The academy structures programs across age tiers:

### Ages 3-6 — Foundation

For the youngest learners:

- **Ball control fundamentals** — first touch, dribbling basics
- **Physical abilities development** — coordination, balance, agility
- **First team play exposure** — basic positioning, simple games
- **First knowledge of football rules** — what scoring is, what fouls are
- **Play-based pedagogy** — fun-first approach for early ages

### Ages 7-9 — Beginner Football

For the elementary-age tier:

- **Special program for beginner football players**
- **Kicking skill development** — proper technique, passing accuracy
- **Position learning** — exposure to defender, midfielder, forward roles
- **Game intelligence basics** — when to pass vs. dribble, basic spacing
- **Match experience** — proper games begin at this tier

### Ages 14+ — Teenagers

For the older youth tier:

- **Ample playing time** — match-focused programming
- **Game tactics teaching** — formations, set pieces, strategic thinking
- **Position specialization** — refined role development
- **Pre-competitive level** — preparation for tournament play

## Facilities

### Two Training Fields

**Outdoor Field — South Pattaya:**
- **Open field** training
- **Sessions may be rescheduled** due to weather (rain, extreme heat)
- Standard outdoor pitch dimensions
- Good for sunny-season training (Nov-Feb especially)

**Indoor Field — Central Pattaya:**
- **Indoor facility** — weather-independent
- **Year-round reliability** — sessions never cancelled for weather
- More limited dimensions but more consistent
- Particularly valuable in Pattaya's rainy season (Jun-Oct) and hot season (Mar-May)

### Other Amenities

- **Changing rooms with showers** for all students
- **Equipment provided** for trial sessions
- **Multiple coaches** per session for proper student-to-coach ratios
