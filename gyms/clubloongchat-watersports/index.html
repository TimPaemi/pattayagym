★ About this venue

Clubloongchat Watersports

Overview

Clubloongchat is a family-run beachside watersports school and rider community on Na Jomtien Beach, Pattaya — and it carries one of the deepest legacies in Thai watersports. Founded by the patriarch known affectionately as "Loongchat" (literally "Uncle Chat"), the operation has a 40-year family legacy of passion and expertise, and is the original windsurfing school on Jomtien Beach with over 30 years of teaching experience, plus Pattaya's first private kitesurf school. It now teaches and rents across the full modern wind-and-water sports range: Kitesurf, Wingfoil, Windsurf, SUP, Sailing, E-foil, and Hobie Catamarans.

For travellers wanting professional, certified, multi-discipline watersports instruction in Pattaya — the kind that progresses you from absolute zero to actually riding — Clubloongchat is the established institution. The combination of multi-decade pedagogy, complete equipment rental, master-coach private 1:1 instruction, and a beachside community vibe makes it the default top-tier choice for serious wind-and-water sports in Pattaya/Jomtien.

The Loongchat Legacy

The school's identity centers on its founder, "Loongchat" (Uncle Chat), a living-legend figure in Thai watersports who pioneered windsurfing on Jomtien Beach over 30 years ago. The school remains family-run, with the patriarch's expertise now joined by a next generation of master coaches. The 40-year family legacy translates into:

  • Deep institutional knowledge of Pattaya wind/tide patterns
  • Complete equipment library built up over decades
  • Professional teaching methodology refined through thousands of students
  • Reputation as Pattaya's go-to watersports authority — the school other operators reference

When the website describes the operation as "a passion meets adventure" and emphasises a family-run business, this isn't marketing flourish — it's an accurate description of a multi-generational operation passing knowledge between family members.

Disciplines Offered

Clubloongchat is unusually broad — most watersports schools specialise in one or two disciplines, but Clubloongchat genuinely covers the full wind-and-water sports range:

Kitesurfing (Pattaya's First Private School)

The headline modern discipline. Clubloongchat is Pattaya's first private kitesurf school.

Kitesurf course tiers:

  • Discovery Lesson — 90 minutes — beginner introduction, kite handling on land/shallow water, no committing to full course yet
  • 4-Hour Course — kite handling fundamentals, safety basics, water-start preparation
  • 8-Hour Course — full beginner-to-rider progression, from land practice to riding with the board

All kitesurf lessons are private 1:1 with experienced Master Coach — safety-first methodology.

Wingfoil (The Newest Discipline)

Wingfoiling is the fastest-growing watersports discipline globally (popularized in the late 2010s and early 2020s) — combining a hand-held wing with a hydrofoil board. Clubloongchat is fully equipped for wingfoil with both lessons and gear rentals on Pattaya's calm waters.

Windsurfing (The Original)

The school's foundational discipline — 30+ years of teaching expertise. Windsurfing instruction for absolute beginners through advanced sailors. Equipment rental for self-directed sailors.

Stand-Up Paddleboarding (SUP)

The most beginner-friendly water discipline. Lessons and rentals available — popular as a casual activity for travellers, families, and as cross-training for surfers and kitesurfers.

Sailing

Hobie Catamarans for rent and sailing instruction available — Pattaya is well-suited to small-boat sailing with its protected gulf waters.

E-foil

Electric hydrofoil — the newest and most futuristic water sport, combining motor propulsion with foil flight. Offered at the school for the early adopters.

Master Coach Methodology

Clubloongchat's instruction model is personalized 1:1 private coaching with Master Coaches:

  • Safety first — every lesson begins with safety briefing and equipment check
  • Personalized to the student — pace, technique focus, conditions adjusted per individual
  • Premium gear — the latest brand-name equipment, properly maintained
  • Beachside support — coach in radio contact during open-water sessions
  • Progressive curriculum — students move through structured stages (kite handling → water relaunch → body drag → board start → first ride)

This contrasts with group lessons typical at budget watersports schools where 4-6 students share one coach. Clubloongchat's 1:1 model produces faster progress at higher cost.

Pattaya Wind & Tide Conditions

Clubloongchat publishes a detailed Pattaya watersports seasons guide, and even hosts a live wind metre on its website for real-time conditions.

Wind Patterns

  • Primary wind direction: South Westerly
  • Wind range: 7-30 knots through the year
  • Beginner-friendly conditions: 8-15 knots — predictable, not too strong, ideal for learning
  • Peak monsoon periods: very strong winds (advanced riders only)

Best Months for Each Discipline

  • Kitesurf prime time: during stable wind months — beginner and experienced riders both
  • Windsurf: year-round with seasonal variation
  • Wingfoil: flexible — works in lighter winds than kitesurf, broader season
  • SUP: calm-water seasons, year-round generally
  • Sailing: year-round, varying conditions

The live wind metre on their site lets travellers plan a session based on actual conditions rather than guesswork.

Beach Setup & Equipment

The school operates directly on Na Jomtien Beach — students walk from the school to the water with gear in minutes, eliminating the typical Pattaya-watersports hassle of coordinating multiple operators and transport.

  • Premium-quality rental gear (latest models, well-maintained)
  • Equipment for all disciplines under one operator
  • Beachside services — wash-down, gear storage, drying space
  • On-water rescue support during lessons (small boat / radio contact)

Location & Getting There

  • Na Jomtien Beach, Pattaya — south of central Jomtien, less crowded waters
  • From central Pattaya: ~15-20 min by car/taxi
  • From Jomtien Beach Road: ~5-10 min south
  • 2 hours from Bangkok (the school's marketed proximity)
  • Beachfront — direct ocean access, no transport between school and water

Pros

  • 40-year family legacy — original kitesurf school in Pattaya, original windsurf school on Jomtien (30+ years)
  • Full multi-discipline coverage — kitesurf, wingfoil, windsurf, SUP, sailing, e-foil, Hobie cat
  • Private 1:1 Master Coach instruction — safety-first, personalized
  • Beginner-friendly Discovery Lessons for first-time water-sports trial
  • Premium equipment library — latest gear, well-maintained
  • Beachside operation — direct water access, no transport coordination
  • Live wind metre on website — real-time condition planning
  • Detailed seasons guide — informed visit timing
  • Less-crowded Na Jomtien location — calmer water, more space
  • English-speaking instruction — accessible for international visitors
  • Hobie Cat rental — small-boat sailing option

Cons

  • Premium pricing — 1:1 private instruction is more expensive than group lessons
  • Wind-dependent — sessions canceled or shifted during no-wind periods
  • Na Jomtien location — requires transport from central Pattaya (15-20 min)
  • Booking essential during high season — lessons fill up
  • Beach-dependent operation — limited indoor backup for poor weather days

Reputation Summary

The default top-tier watersports school in Pattaya. Particularly strong for:

  • First-time kitesurfers and wingfoilers wanting professional safety-first introduction
  • Multi-discipline athletes wanting to progress across kitesurf + wingfoil + windsurf with one school
  • Returning watersports travellers who already know they want quality instruction
  • Equipment renters who want premium gear and beachside service
  • Pattaya residents developing watersports skills over multi-year progression

The 40-year legacy is the differentiator. Most Pattaya beach-watersports operators are 1-5 year operations or pure jet-ski rental businesses; Clubloongchat is a multi-generational watersports institution that sits in a different category.

Best For

  • Kitesurf beginners wanting Pattaya's first private school
  • Wingfoilers at any level
  • Windsurfers wanting deep institutional expertise
  • SUP / casual paddlers seeking quality rental gear
  • Multi-discipline watersports travellers
  • Hobie Cat and small-boat sailors
  • Returning Pattaya watersports visitors

Not Best For

  • Pure budget watersports seekers (cheaper jet-ski operators along Jomtien Beach Road)
  • Travellers based in central Pattaya without transport to Na Jomtien
  • Group-lesson preferers (Clubloongchat is 1:1 private)
  • No-wind day visitors (sessions wind-dependent)
