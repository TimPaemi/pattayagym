Overview

Cross Pattaya Pratamnak is a 5-star hotel on Phra Tamnak Hill whose Cloud Club fitness centre and dedicated PLAYROOM games space position it as a hotel-integrated fitness-and-recreation destination rather than a standalone gym. The hotel operates with a wellness-and-leisure lifestyle pitch that blends serious fitness equipment with family-friendly recreation amenities — making it distinctive among Pattaya hotel fitness offerings.

Cloud Club itself is fully equipped for varied workout intensities — treadmills and cardio for high-energy training, strength equipment for resistance work, and a quiet yoga space for low-impact mind-body practice. Complimentary towels, refreshments, and snacks are provided to members and guests, and private yoga lessons or personal trainer sessions can be arranged with 24 hours notice.

The PLAYROOM is the hotel's distinguishing feature — a dedicated recreation room with board games and indoor football (likely a foosball or small-pitch indoor football setup), marketed with a funky and stylish atmosphere. This positions Cross Pattaya Pratamnak as a family / group / millennials-or-younger demographic hotel rather than a pure-business or pure-couples property.

For visitors staying at Cross Pattaya Pratamnak — and for Pratamnak Hill residents who can access the facility through day passes or memberships — the property offers a distinctive blend of serious fitness, wellness yoga, and active recreation that no other Pattaya hotel quite matches.
