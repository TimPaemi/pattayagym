Common questions

What is the best golf course in Pattaya?

Siam Country Club Old Course is the headline championship layout. Phoenix Gold Golf, Burapha (36-hole, Thailand Open host), and Pattana Sports & Resort all rank top-tier. Best is subjective — fast greens, tight fairways, or scenic views all have strong contenders.

How much does golf in Pattaya cost?

Green fees ฿1,500–฿5,000 weekday, ฿2,500–฿7,500 weekend. Premium courses peak at ฿8,000+. Plus caddie ฿500–฿800 (mandatory at most courses) and cart ฿800–฿1,500.

Are caddies required at Pattaya golf courses?

Yes at virtually all courses — Thai golf tradition. Caddies typically expect ฿500–฿800 base plus tips. Most are excellent with course knowledge and pace; many speak basic English.

When is the best time to golf in Pattaya?

Cool dry season Nov–Feb is peak — book tee times 2–4 weeks ahead, especially weekends. Hot season Mar–May has cheaper rates and quieter courses. Rainy season Jun–Oct sees afternoon storms but morning play is fine.
