★ About this venue

Pattaya Tennis & Badminton Inter Club

Overview

Pattaya Tennis & Badminton Inter Club is the most comprehensively multi-sport racquet facility in central Pattaya — a single-property club delivering 5 hard tennis courts, indoor badminton, dedicated pickleball courts (one outdoor and one indoor), and on-site fitness equipment, with coaching available across all racquet disciplines and a basic pro shop on-site. Located on Soi Toongklom-Talman 25 in Pattaya City, the club has emerged as a primary destination for Pattaya's pickleball community as well as continuing to serve traditional tennis and badminton players.

The pickleball setup is particularly distinctive: two dedicated pickleball courts with permanent lines and nets rather than improvised tennis-court conversions. The outdoor pickleball court is high-grade hard surface suitable for serious play, while the newly constructed indoor pickleball court features a softer plastic pickleball mat designed to prevent knee injuries and prioritise player comfort — recognizing pickleball's appeal to older players for whom joint comfort is critical.

For Pattaya racquet sport players who want multi-discipline access in one facility, dedicated pickleball infrastructure, badminton + tennis combinations, and modern fitness equipment, the Inter Club is the city's most efficient single-stop racquet venue.

Why Pickleball Has Exploded in Pattaya

Pickleball's growth in Pattaya mirrors global trends:

Sport Characteristics

  • Smaller court — quarter the size of a tennis court
  • Lower physical demand than tennis — appeals to 50+ age group
  • Easier learning curve — playable in first session
  • Doubles format social — built-in 4-player community
  • Lower equipment cost than tennis ($50-150 paddle vs. $200-300 racquet + strings)
  • Less physical risk — slower ball, smaller court, less running

Pattaya Demographics Match

  • Large 50+ expat population — ideal pickleball demographic
  • Active retiree community wanting low-impact sport
  • Social networks that pickleball integrates
  • Year-round outdoor play weather (with monsoon caveats)

Inter Club's Pickleball Lead

By investing in dedicated pickleball courts (rather than improvised lines), the Inter Club positioned itself early as the Pattaya pickleball home — a community advantage that's hard to replicate.
