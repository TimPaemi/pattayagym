03 Common questions

BJJ / Grappling in Pattaya — FAQ.

Is there a BJJ (Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu) gym in Pattaya?

Yes. Pattaya has a dedicated Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu academy, listed in this category, training both gi and no-gi grappling. It is the city main home for BJJ.

Can beginners train BJJ in Pattaya?

Yes. The academy welcomes complete beginners alongside experienced grapplers, with fundamentals classes to learn the basics safely. No experience is needed to start. Contact the academy about beginner sessions and whether a gi is provided or required.

Can visitors drop in to train BJJ?

Yes. Visiting and travelling grapplers are welcome to drop in for sessions or train over a longer stay. It is good practice to message the academy ahead to confirm class times and mat-fee or package options.
