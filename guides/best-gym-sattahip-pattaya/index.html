Na Jomtien & Sattahip.

By TimPaemi · 7 min read · Updated 2026-06-02 · How we rank →

South of Jomtien is a different Pattaya — yacht marinas, water parks, U-Tapao airport, and 5-star resort gyms without Walking Street noise. This guide is the far-south sport belt only.

Pattaya · 157 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

Na Jomtien and Sattahip are Pattaya's quiet south — past the Jomtien tourist strip into marina country, water parks, golf fairways, and U-Tapao airport. This guide covers the far-south belt where Ocean Marina anchors Southeast Asia's yacht scene, Ramayana runs Thailand's largest water park, and 5-star resort gyms line Na Jomtien Beach. It does not repeat central Jomtien walk-in gyms — see best gym Jomtien for beach-road Muay Thai and Thepprasit camps.

Hub pages: Sattahip / far south · Jomtien · compare · plan my trip. Flying in? U-Tapao (UTP) sits inside Sattahip district — ideal first-night base before central Pattaya.

Na Jomtien vs Sattahip — where the pin lands

Na Jomtien is the southern extension of Jomtien Beach — Marriott, Renaissance, Andaz, Mövenpick, Ocean Marina, Ramayana corridor. Still beach-resort territory, 15–25 minutes south of central Jomtien traffic.

Sattahip district stretches further — Bang Saray fishing village, Nong Nooch gardens, Buddha Mountain (Khao Chi Chan), Tarzan zipline, Chee Chan Golf. Baht-bus coverage thins out; car or scooter essential for daily sport — Pattaya Vehicle Rentals.

Why stay south of Jomtien

  • U-Tapao airport access — land and train same day without Beach Road traffic.
  • Marina & sailing — Ocean Marina, SailBreeze IYT school, yacht charter hub.
  • Family water days — Ramayana, Columbia Pictures Aquaverse (former Cartoon Network Amazone).
  • Golf without Bangkok drive — Chee Chan, Bang Saray courses, Horseshoe Point nearby east.
  • Quiet recovery — resort pools and spa between fight camp blocks or dive days.

Trade-off: fewer budget Muay Thai walk-ins than Jomtien Soi 7 or central WKO. Serious MT long-stayers often live Jomtien north and visit south for recovery weekends.

Ocean Marina & sailing

Ocean Marina Jomtien is Southeast Asia's largest marina — 455 wet berths, yachts to 75m, Top of the Gulf Regatta host, customs clearance for international arrivals. On-site SailBreeze Sailing School runs IYT certification and charters. ฿฿฿฿. This is Pattaya's sailing authority address — not a gym, but core south-side sport infrastructure.

Water parks & active family days

  • Ramayana Water Park — largest in Thailand/SEA; full-day trip, 20 km south.
  • Columbia Pictures Aquaverse — themed slides, Na Jomtien corridor.
  • Tarzan Adventure — jungle zipline, Sattahip.
  • Nong Nooch Garden — cultural show includes Muay Thai demo.

Golf & outdoor south

  • Chee Chan Golf Resort — Buddha Mountain views, premium Na Jomtien/Sattahip layout.
  • Bangpra International · Phoenix Gold — mid-district courses.
  • Khao Chi Chan (Buddha Mountain) — free walking and meditation, dawn recommended.

Getting around & where to stay

Sukhumvit Road runs the full coast — Grab reliable but adds up daily. Monthly south-side condos cluster Na Jomtien and Bang Saray. Long-stay housing: Pattaya Villa. Eat local: Restaurant Guide.

Who should not base here: nightlife-focused short trips needing Walking Street walkability — use Central Pattaya. Budget MT monthly on ฿4k camp tier — East Darkside or central WKO instead.

U-Tapao airport corridor — who lands here

Utapao–Rayong–Pattaya International Airport (UTP) sits south of Pattaya city. Fighters and families flying UTP often base Na Jomtien or Bang Saray to avoid an extra 45–60 minutes to central Beach Road. Kombat Group transfers market to this corridor; resort guests at Andaz and Mövenpick expect taxi or pre-booked pickup — confirm price before baggage claim.

Sample south-side weekly rhythm

  • Mon/Wed/Fri AM: Ocean Marina swim or hotel gym · PM Muay Thai at Jomtien walk-in or Venum.
  • Tue: Ramayana or Cartoon Network water park — family rest from lifting.
  • Thu: Chee Chan or Phoenix golf — early tee, heat management.
  • Sat: KBA kite if wind season · or Tarzan zipline Sattahip belt.
  • Sun: Pool recovery — lap pools guide.

Resort day-pass reality

5-star gyms (Andaz, Hilton south, Dusit) often restrict day passes to guests or concierge approval. Do not assume walk-in like Tony's Gym — email or call front desk with dates. Mid-tier resort fitness is more flexible; always carry passport copy for hotel gym registration.

Marina sport vs gym membership

Ocean Marina is infrastructure — berthing, customs, regattas — not a monthly gym. SailBreeze sells courses and charters. Pair marina mornings with afternoon iron at Jomtien commercial gyms or hotel fitness. Kite schools operate on wind windows; gym contracts should not assume daily kite availability.

Bang Saray and Cartoon Network belt

Cartoon Network Amazone and Ramayana anchor family sport days south — not Muay Thai, but essential if partners and kids share the trip. Chee Chan Golf sits between marina life and water parks — book tee times before heat peak. Bang Saray food sois are cheaper than Beach Road — good for long-stay athletes watching budget.

Scooter vs car for south belt

Na Jomtien to Sattahip water parks is 15–25 minutes by car; scooters work if you have Thailand experience — highway sections are fast. UTP airport pickups: confirm camp/hotel quote includes tolls. Night returns from marina restaurants — carry small torch for unlit sois.

Long-stay athletes stack south base with occasional central Pattaya fight night — budget ฿800–1,200 round-trip Grab when you do not want to ride tired after pads.

Frequently asked questions

What gyms are in Sattahip and far south Pattaya?

Ramayana Water Park, Cartoon Network Aquaverse, Regents school sport, Thai Sky Adventures skydive, Bira Circuit, and golf toward Phoenix / Treasure Hill belts. Hub: Sattahip area.

Is Sattahip good for a training holiday base?

Strong for golf, water parks, and quiet residential training — weak for nightly Beach Road convenience. Many fighters stay central or Jomtien and day-trip south for adventure.

How far is Sattahip from central Pattaya?

20–40 minutes by car depending on venue — plan taxis or rent from Pattaya Vehicle Rentals. U-Tapao airport (UTP) sits inside the district for arrivals.

Where should families train in the south belt?

Ramayana and resort pools — pair with family-friendly guide. Confirm child height rules before booking karting or skydive add-ons.
