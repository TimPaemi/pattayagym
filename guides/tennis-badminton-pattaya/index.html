Tennis & badminton.

By Tim Paemi · 12 min read · Updated 2026-06-01 · How we rank →

Pattaya runs floodlit hard courts on Pratamnak, six covered ITF courts south at Greta, budget badminton halls on the Darkside, and seven tennis courts at Fitz Club inside Royal Cliff. Padel and pickleball have their own guide — this page is tennis and badminton only, sourced from our venue files.

Pattaya · 158 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

Pattaya has more tennis and badminton than most visitors expect — floodlit hard courts on Pratamnak, six covered ITF Plexipave courts south at Greta, budget badminton halls on the Darkside, and the luxury tier at Fitz Club inside Royal Cliff. Padel and pickleball have their own guide; this page covers tennis, badminton, and multi-racquet clubs in our 158-venue directory.

Racquet sport here splits into clear tiers: premium resort clubs (Fitz, hotel courts), mid-tier outdoor clubs with coaching and social leagues, and indoor badminton halls for humid evenings. Start at tennis / padel / squash category, shortlist on compare, and map hotels on plan my trip.

Tennis vs badminton vs padel — know what you are booking

  • Tennis — outdoor hard courts dominate; Greta is the standout covered ITF Plexipave option when rain threatens. Fitz Club lists seven tennis courts with daily play 07:00–20:00.
  • Badminton — indoor wood-floor halls; hourly court hire; ideal 18:00–22:00 when outdoor tennis is still hot and sticky.
  • Padel / pickleball — separate facilities on Pratamnak and Mabprachan — padel & pickleball guide, not repeated here.
  • Squash — Fitz Club runs two air-conditioned squash courts alongside tennis — rare in Pattaya outside hotel clubs.

1. Fitz Club — luxury tier (Royal Cliff, Pratamnak)

Fitz Club — Racquets, Health & Fitness at Royal Cliff Hotels Group is Pattaya's headline racquet address: seven tennis courts, two AC squash courts, table tennis, swimming, gym, and professional coaching. TripAdvisor Travellers' Choice 2023, 2024, 2025 (three consecutive years, perfect score in venue files). ฿฿฿฿ tier.

Fitz hosts recurring tournaments — men's doubles, annual singles (18th edition noted in press), and ITF Masters events. Day-pass and guest policies vary by season; contact +66 38 250 421 or fitz@royalcliff.com before you travel. Compare full resort sport: luxury sports clubs guide.

Best for: visitors who want one premium address for tennis, squash, gym, and pool without juggling multiple venues. Pair Pratamnak padel at Play Padel — five minutes from InterContinental hill — see padel guide.

2. Central & social tennis — mid-tier outdoor

Pattaya Tennis Club

Pattaya Tennis Club anchors central Pattaya outdoor tennis — multiple floodlit hard courts, coaching for all levels, equipment rental, drop-in pickup play, and member-led social leagues. Typical hours 06:00–22:00 with evening floodlights. ฿฿ tier — more accessible than Greta or Fitz without condo-only restrictions.

Best for: expats and tourists who want walk-up tennis without hotel membership. Tell drivers "Pattaya Tennis Club central" and confirm exact court location at booking — address in our file is central Pattaya (verify pin on arrival).

Tara Tennis Club

Tara Tennis Club runs three hard courts including one covered roof (resurfaced 2022 in venue research). Useful when you need partial rain cover without driving to Na Jomtien.

TOS Tennis Academy

TOS Tennis Academy at Baan Suan Lalana is the closest serious tennis to Jomtien Beach — Jomtien residents avoid 30-minute taxis to Pratamnak for every session. See Jomtien area guide for gym pairing.

Pattaya Sports Club

Pattaya Sports Club is a non-profit multi-sport club on 3rd Road with ~4,700 members — tennis among football, pool, and social events. Member culture matters; confirm guest day-pass rules before you arrive.

3. Hotel & resort courts — guest priority

  • Siam Bayshore Resort — three outdoor hard courts near Walking Street; hotel guests and day visitors.
  • Centara Grand Mirage — two tennis courts plus water park and family facilities.
  • InterContinental Pattaya — hilltop Pratamnak courts adjacent to Play Padel cluster.

Hotel courts often prioritise in-house guests — always call ahead for visitor rates and court availability. Not a substitute for club coaching programmes at Pattaya Tennis Club or Fitz.

4. South — Greta covered courts (Na Jomtien / Sattahip)

Greta Sport Club at Na Jomtien / Sattahip runs six covered ITF-rated Plexipave tennis courts — Pattaya's strongest answer to rainy-season scheduling. When central outdoor courts cancel, Greta is where serious players relocate.

Pair with Na Jomtien & Sattahip guide if you base south. Distance from Beach Road is 20–35 minutes by car depending on traffic — plan taxi budget for twice-weekly league play.

5. Badminton in Pattaya — indoor halls

Badminton suits humid evenings when outdoor tennis is brutal — wood-floor halls, hourly court hire, casual community nights. Shuttle sport is also easier on knees than hard-court tennis for seniors cross-training — see seniors low-impact guide.

Euro Badminton

Euro Badminton on Soi Chaiyaphruek 2 (East Pattaya) combines courts with pool and Thai-food restaurant — budget tier, expat-friendly social vibe.

Diamond Badminton

Diamond Badminton on South Pattaya 3rd Road lists multiple wood-floor courts and equipment rental — central access after work.

Pattaya Tennis & Badminton Inter Club

Pattaya Tennis & Badminton Inter Club stacks tennis, badminton, pickleball, and table tennis under one roof — useful if your group mixes racquet sports and you want one membership conversation.

Pricing and booking — what to expect

  • Court hire — outdoor tennis typically ฿200–฿800/hour depending on venue and peak time; Fitz and Greta premium higher — confirm at desk.
  • Coaching — most clubs offer private and junior lessons; book English-speaking coaches ahead in high season (Nov–Feb).
  • Equipment — rental racquets and shuttles common; serious players travel with their own strings and shoes.
  • Leagues — Pattaya Tennis Club and Fitz run social/competitive play; ask on arrival for current ladder or tournament calendar.
  • Rain policy — outdoor courts cancel; Greta covered or badminton halls are the backup plan May–October.

We do not publish live prices — venue tiers (฿–฿฿฿฿) in compare are the honest band until you confirm on site.

FAQ

Where is the best tennis in Pattaya?

For premium facilities and coaching, Fitz Club at Royal Cliff. For accessible central outdoor play, Pattaya Tennis Club. For covered rain-proof courts, Greta Sport Club south.

Can I play tennis near Jomtien without driving to Pratamnak?

Yes — TOS Tennis Academy serves Jomtien-side players. Pratamnak leagues still require taxi for Fitz or padel clusters.

Where is badminton cheapest in Pattaya?

Euro Badminton and Diamond Badminton sit in budget–mid tiers in our files. Confirm hourly court rate at reception — prices change.

Is padel the same as tennis?

No — different court, walls, and scoring. See padel & pickleball guide. Inter Club offers pickleball alongside tennis and badminton.

Do I need membership for one visit?

Many mid-tier clubs allow day play or guest passes; Fitz and hotels are stricter. Call ahead with your dates and skill level.

What should I bring?

Non-marking court shoes, towel, water, and sun protection for outdoor day sessions. Badminton halls are indoor — lighter clothing.
