★ About this venue

Elite Gym & Fitness Pattaya

Overview

Elite Gym & Fitness Pattaya is one of the newest and most heavily invested commercial gym builds in the city — a 23 million baht facility opened in September 2022 that occupies 950 square metres of fully air-conditioned space on Sukhumvit Road, between Soi 42 and 44, opposite Soi 65. The construction budget alone — roughly USD 650,000+ — signals a meaningful capital commitment to high-end equipment, modern infrastructure, and clean design that distinguishes Elite from older neighborhood gyms in the area.

The Sukhumvit location places Elite firmly in the East Pattaya / Naklua corridor — accessible by the city's main north-south arterial, easy reach from East Pattaya condos, Naklua hotels, and Sukhumvit-corridor expat residences. Operating 06:30 to 22:00 seven days a week, the gym serves the early-bird-to-late-evening demographic without the 24/7 commitment of Jetts or Fitness 7, but with a more polished and recently outfitted environment than most independent gyms.

For 2022+ Pattaya residents and visitors who prioritise brand-new equipment, fresh facilities, AC comfort, and modern training environment, Elite Gym & Fitness is among the strongest options on the Sukhumvit / East Pattaya / Naklua axis.

23 Million Baht Build — What It Bought

  • Quality flooring appropriate for both cardio + free weights
  • Mirror walls + lighting professionally installed
  • HVAC system for year-round comfort

Equipment

  • State-of-the-art equipment per The Pattaya News reporting
  • Brand-new cardio machines (commercial-grade)

  • Adequate free-weight section
  • Functional training zones

Amenities

  • Reception / member services

  • Air conditioning throughout
  • Cleaning supplies + protocols

For a Pattaya gym, 23M baht is a serious investment — many independent gyms operate on much smaller construction budgets. Elite's positioning reflects that investment: modern, polished, premium-tier without being premium-priced.

Schedule & Hours

Daily 06:30-22:00 — 15.5 hour operating window:

  • Early morning (06:30-09:00): beat the heat
  • Midday (09:00-15:00): quieter, AC welcome
  • Afternoon (15:00-19:00): peak demand
  • Evening (19:00-22:00): post-work session

Not 24/7 like Fitness 7 or Jetts — but 06:30 start time accommodates very early risers, and 22:00 close handles most evening schedules.

The Sukhumvit Road corridor serves the residential East Pattaya / Naklua expat population — a demographic that includes:

Elite's location captures this demographic that's not optimally served by central Pattaya / Walking Street area gyms.
