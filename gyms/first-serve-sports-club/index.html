★ About this venue

First Serve Sports Club

Overview

First Serve Sports Club is a multi-sport private club in Pattaya that combines swimming with Swimming Association of Thailand-trained instructors, tennis courts and coaching, a gym, yoga programming, and a restaurant + juice bar serving international and Thai cuisine — all under a single membership-style facility. The club differentiates from pure-swimming-pool venues like the public Naklua and Jomtien municipal pools (and from hotel pools like Hard Rock) by offering certified swim coaching pedigree alongside multi-sport amenities — making it the most comprehensive single-venue sports club for swimming-focused families in Pattaya.

The swim coaching specifically holds Swimming Association of Thailand training credentials — meaning instructors are certified to the Thai national governing body's standards rather than informal or self-taught coaching. Coach Nook is highlighted in club reviews as very professional and highly recommended, and the broader instructor team is consistently praised as professional. Lessons available in Thai or English (English classes have limited time slots), making First Serve accessible to Pattaya's mixed Thai-and-international family demographic.

The pricing is strikingly accessible: ฿1,200/month for all-day unlimited swimming (swim as many times per day as you want) OR ฿1,000/month for evening-only access (18:00-21:00). For dedicated swimmers and families with children needing structured swim education, First Serve delivers certified coaching plus open-swim access at independent-club pricing.

The Swim Coaching Pedigree

Swimming Association of Thailand (SAT)

The Swimming Association of Thailand (สมาคมว่ายน้ำแห่งประเทศไทย) is Thailand's national governing body for competitive swimming:

  • Member of FINA (now World Aquatics) — the international swimming body
  • Sets coaching certification standards in Thailand
  • Operates Thai national swim teams for Olympic, Asian Games, regional competition
  • Certifies coaches through formal pedagogical programs
  • Sanctions competitive events at all levels

A coach trained by the Swimming Association of Thailand holds credentials beyond informal private-pool teaching:

  • Stroke technique taught to international standards
  • Progressive curriculum appropriate to age and skill
  • Safety / drowning-prevention protocols formally trained
  • Competitive swim preparation knowledge
  • Therapeutic / rehabilitation swim coaching where relevant

Coach Nook — Featured Trainer

Reviews specifically highlight Coach Nook as:

  • Very professional
  • Highly recommended for swim lessons
  • Patient instruction for beginners
  • Effective progression for committed swimmers

The named-coach-who-cares positioning matters in swim coaching, where individual relationship between coach and student determines progress.

Bilingual Coaching

Lessons in Thai or English:

  • Thai lessons — most schedule slots, primary language
  • English lessons — limited time slots, secondary language
  • Mixed language classes possible for international families

For Pattaya's international families wanting English-language swim coaching, First Serve is one of the few options outside premium private clubs and hotel-based programs.

Membership Pricing — The Value Proposition

Swim-Focused Membership

  • All-day unlimited swimming: ฿1,200/month — swim as many times per day as you want
  • Evening-only swimming (18:00-21:00): ฿1,000/month

What Makes This Value

  • Compared to single-session pool day passes (~฿200-500), monthly membership pays for itself in 4-5 visits
  • Compared to Hard Rock Pool day pass (~฿1,000-2,500), monthly membership is ~half the cost for unlimited
  • Multiple-times-per-day option suits training swimmers, fitness-seekers, families

Coaching Add-Ons

  • Private lessons — typically ฿400-1,000/lesson depending on coach and duration
  • Group lessons — discounted per-student
  • Junior development packages — multiple lessons bundled
  • Tennis coaching — separate pricing

Multi-Sport Membership

For full club access including tennis, gym, yoga, and pool, monthly fees likely scale to ฿2,500-4,500/month depending on package. Direct contact recommended.

Programs

Swim Lessons by Age

  • Toddler swim (12 months+) — water familiarization, parent-child format
  • Pre-school (3-5 years) — basic floating, kicking, blowing bubbles
  • Children (6-12 years) — formal stroke development, technique
  • Teens (13+) — advanced strokes, conditioning, possibly competitive swim
  • Adults (all ages) — technique refresher, fitness swimming, fear-of-water work
  • Senior swimmers — gentle approach, low-impact fitness

Lesson Format

  • Private 1-on-1 sessions
  • Small group (2-4 students) sessions
  • Class (5-8 students) sessions
  • Frequency: typically 1-3 sessions per week
  • Progression assessment: periodic skill checkpoints
