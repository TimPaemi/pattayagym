Pattaya Golf Driving Range

Overview

Pattaya Golf Driving Range is the city's go-to practice facility for golfers wanting affordable warm-up, swing work, or lesson sessions without paying championship-course green fees. Open from approximately 07:00 to 22:00 daily — a generous 15-hour window — the range is priced at approximately ฿60 per tray of ~40 balls, making it one of the most affordable golf practice facilities in central Pattaya. The range offers club rental for casual visitors who don't bring their own gear, and professional lessons through resident teaching pros for golfers wanting structured improvement.

The range fills a critical gap in the Pattaya golf scene. The city's championship courses (Khao Kheow, Laem Chabang, Treasure Hill, Phoenix Gold, etc.) are 40-50 minutes from central Pattaya and require a half-day commitment plus ฿1,500-4,500 in green fees. For golfers who want to warm up the swing before a course round, work on specific shot patterns, take a lesson, or simply hit balls without a full course session, the in-city driving range is dramatically more accessible.

The Pattaya driving range scene also includes Diana Driving Range (30 floodlit bays, 200-yard range, pro instructors, on-site restaurant), Golf Hub Pattaya (indoor simulator + state-of-the-art club fitting on Soi Buakhao + East Pattaya), Siam Country Club Waterside Range (premium real-grass chip and putt area), and 300 Yards Driving Range (independent operation). Pattaya Golf Driving Range sits in the affordable mainstream tier — a working range for working golfers.

Driving Range Practice — Why It Matters

For Pattaya golfers, range practice delivers value that course play can't:

Pre-Round Warm-Up

  • Loosen muscles before tee time
  • Calibrate distance with each club
  • Find swing rhythm — first 10 balls vs. teeing off cold
  • Mental preparation for the round
  • 20-30 minute warm-up at ~฿60 cost is invaluable

Skill Improvement

  • Repetition without consequences — bad shots don't cost strokes
  • Specific shot pattern work — fade vs. draw, low vs. high
  • Swing mechanics corrections in feedback loop
  • Long-term improvement that can't happen during scored rounds

Lesson Sessions

  • Structured instruction with pro
  • Video analysis sometimes available
  • Drill repetition in safe environment
  • Cheaper per-shot than course-based instruction

Equipment Testing

  • Try new clubs before purchasing
  • Distance comparison between options
  • Feel evaluation in real swing conditions

Pricing & Format

Tray Pricing

  • ~฿60 per tray of approximately 40 balls
  • Multiple tray purchases for extended sessions
  • Ball quality typically range balls (not premium tour balls but adequate for practice)

Hours

  • 07:00-22:00 daily typical
  • Early morning: cool, less crowded
  • Lunch hours: hot, often empty
  • Evening (16:00-21:00): most popular, after work
  • Late night (until 22:00): cooler, lit by floodlights

Mat Bays

  • Artificial turf mats with rubber tees
  • Numbered bays for organised booking
  • Some bays may have shade structures for sun protection
  • Bays vary in distance to back-stop net (200-280 yards typical)

Club Rental

  • Available for casual visitors without their own gear
  • Limited brand selection typical
  • Rental cost: typically ฿100-300 per club per session
  • Bag rental also available

Lessons

  • Resident teaching pros available
  • Hourly lesson pricing: typically ฿800-1,500 per hour
  • Package lessons discount per-hour cost
  • Group clinics seasonally
  • Junior development programs

Daily Routine

A typical session at Pattaya Golf Driving Range:

  1. Arrival — pay for tray (or trays)
  2. Bay assignment — pick available bay
  3. Stretching / warm-up — 5-10 minutes
  4. Wedge / short iron warm-up — 5-10 balls of half-swings
  5. Iron progression — work through 9, 7, 5 irons
  6. Driver work — 10-15 balls full swing
  7. Specialty shots — fades, draws, punch shots
  8. Cool-down — back to wedge for feel
  9. Total time: 30-45 minutes typical

Pros

  • Affordable — ~฿60 per tray
  • Long hours — 07:00-22:00
  • Walkable / driveable central Pattaya location
  • Club rental for travellers without gear
  • Professional lessons available
  • Floodlit for evening practice
  • Year-round weather-resilient (some bays have shade structures)
  • Beginner-friendly environment
  • Pre-round warm-up convenient for any Pattaya course player
  • Casual atmosphere — no membership pressure

Cons

  • Mat bays rather than real grass — different feel from course
  • Range balls rather than tour balls
  • No course feel — pure repetition without strategic decisions
  • Mid-tier maintenance — basic infrastructure
  • No indoor / AC option — heat-dependent
  • Limited shade in some bays
  • Crowded peak evening hours
  • Not suitable for short-game practice typically (no chipping/putting greens)

Best For

  • Pre-round warm-up for Pattaya course players
  • Beginners taking first golf lessons
  • Casual golfers without time for full courses
  • Practice-focused improvement seekers
  • Travellers without their own gear needing rental
  • Budget golfers wanting affordable practice
  • Late-evening practice sessions
  • Junior development programs

Not Best For

  • Premium real-grass practice — try Siam Country Club Waterside
  • Indoor / simulator training — try Golf Hub Pattaya
  • Short-game / chipping practice — most ranges don't have chipping greens
  • Tournament-grade equipment testing — try Golf Hub Pattaya for fitting
  • Walking + putting practice — try a course's practice green
