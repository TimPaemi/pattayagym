# Discovered venues — wave 2 (Thai-language)

Discovery date: **2026-07-20**. This is the approved discovery-only stop point; no
full venue records were created.

## Method and decision rules

The sweep used Thai-script category queries across Pattaya, Jomtien, Naklua,
Pratamnak, Bang Lamung, Sattahip and Sriracha, then repeated the most productive
queries for East Pattaya / Nong Prue, Huai Yai, Bang Saray and Laem Chabang. It
also checked public sports facilities and hotel facilities explicitly offered to
non-residents.

Every new-looking result was checked against all 157 existing `data.js` records.
The comparison used address, phone, coordinates and host venue where available;
name similarity alone was not treated as an identity match. The table deliberately
does **not** repeat every obviously existing venue returned by the searches. It
does include deceptive alternate-name matches, host-venue programs and the closed
Diamond Badminton listing because those are useful cross-check findings.

This is discovery evidence, not the four-source research required for a full
record. A current Maps listing or recent operator activity is sufficient for
`OPEN` here, but hours, public access and location-specific review data still need
full verification. No prices were collected at this stage.

Status vocabulary:

- `OPEN`: current listing/activity supports operation; eligible for full research.
- `UNCLEAR`: plausible identity, but current operation or exact site is not firm.
- `PRE-OPENING`: announced or labelled opening soon, not yet trading.
- `TEMPORARILY-CLOSED` / `CLOSED`: current closure finding.
- `NOT-A-VENUE`: a coach/program at a host site rather than an independent venue.

## Assessment and next batch

The hypothesis **holds**. The 157-venue directory is materially incomplete for the
actual Thai-patronised sports market. Sixty-three currently open, uncovered
identities is too large to dismiss as search noise, even before held-back listings
are resolved. Thai-script searching was especially valuable for badminton,
taekwondo, swimming instruction and municipal facilities; geographic modifiers
were decisive for Sriracha, East Pattaya, Bang Saray and Laem Chabang.

There is an important scope choice before records are commissioned. **Twenty-five of
the 69 uncovered identities are in Sriracha, Laem Chabang, Bang Phra or outside a
confident Pattaya-area assignment.** If the directory remains limited to the six
current Pattaya area pages, those should not silently enter the same batch. Even
after setting them aside, the Pattaya/Jomtien/Nong Prue/Huai Yai/Bang Saray/Naklua/
Sattahip side still contains a substantial research queue.

Recommended first eight after review, selected for current status, distinct
identity and evidence quality: **Chilli Padel Club, Prime Padel Pattaya, JP
Badminton, Badminton-Khaonoi, Swimming Kids Pattaya, Baby Shark Swim School, James
GYM and MAVINN Muay Thai**. Full records should still wait for approval and must
meet the four-independent-source rule.
