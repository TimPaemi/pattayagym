Train 1 week or 1 month.

By TimPaemi · 7 min read · Updated 2026-05-29 · How we rank →

One week is a sampler. One month is where habits form. This guide picks camps, budgets, and weekly rhythms for each trip length — so you do not book a resort month package for a seven-day experiment, or a walk-in gym for a serious four-week block.

Pattaya · 157 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

“How long should I train?” is the question behind half the emails we get. One week and one month are the two most common trip lengths for foreigners landing in Pattaya — and they need completely different camp choices, budgets, and recovery plans. This guide is the length-of-stay layer on top of training holiday planning and beginners guide.

Train Muay Thai in Pattaya for 1 week

What one week can realistically achieve

In seven days you will not become a fighter. You can: learn stance and guard, hold pads without flinching, throw a basic combo on command, and understand whether you want a month two. Most ethical gyms will keep you on fundamentals and pads — not hard sparring.

Recommended rhythm: 5 training days, 2 rest days. Example: Mon–Fri one session per day (morning OR afternoon), weekend beach walk + massage. If you feel destroyed by day 3, drop to 4 days — quality beats ego.

Best camps for a 1-week trip

Fairtex Pattaya — highest beginner throughput, English operating language, on-site hotel and pool. You land, check in, train — zero logistics stress. ฿฿฿. Best when this is your first time in Thailand and you want a resort frame around training.

Kombat Group Thailand — all-inclusive packages (meals, room, transfers, two-a-days). Huai Yai, East Pattaya. Best when you want one invoice and a camp bubble for the week. Pair with stay-and-train guide.

Battle Conquer Gym — air-conditioned central gym. Best when heat is your main worry and you are self-booking a hotel nearby.

Pattaya Thai Boxing & Fitness (Jomtien) — ~฿300 walk-in pad rounds, 200m from beach. Best “day 1 test” before committing to a bigger camp. See Jomtien guide.

1-week budget ranges (2026, per person)

  • Budget experiment: hostel + walk-in sessions — ฿8,000–18,000 total.
  • Mid package: hotel + 5–7 drop-ins or small pack — ฿18,000–30,000.
  • Resort all-inclusive week: Fairtex/Kombat-style — ฿25,000–45,000.

1-week mistakes to avoid

  • Booking two-a-days every day from day 1 — shin and hip fatigue will peak day 4.
  • Choosing a camp 40 minutes from your hotel — taxi fatigue eats the trip.
  • Hard sparring on day 2 because someone at the bar said you should “test yourself.”
  • Skipping English-speaking verification if you need fluent coaching.

Train Muay Thai in Pattaya for 1 month

What one month can realistically achieve

Four weeks is where adaptation shows: better cardio, cleaner kicks, pad rhythm that feels natural, optional light sparring if you opt in. Many long-stay travellers use month one to decide whether to extend to 3–6 months or add a fight date.

Recommended rhythm: Week 1 — one session/day, learn recovery. Week 2 — add technique focus, maybe second session 2×/week. Weeks 3–4 — stable 5–6 days/week if sleep and nutrition hold. Schedule at least one full rest day per week.

Best camps for a 1-month stay

WKO Muay Thai (ISS Gym) — ~฿4,000/month culture, English-operating, Sakmongkol on pads. Central Pattaya. Best value-for-coaching month for independent travellers who arrange their own apartment.

Petchrungruang Gym — authentic family camp since 1986, ฿ tier, functional English. Best for month-long immersion without resort pricing.

Sor Klinmee — budget authentic, drop-in ~฿200–300, two-a-days. Best for adventurous monthly budgets under ฿10k training spend.

Fairtex / Kombat Group — monthly packages available; premium but removes housing friction. Best if you do not want to apartment-hunt.

Rage Fight Academy — education-visa pathway advertised; Thappraya near Jomtien. Best if you are serious about MMA/BJJ alongside MT for 90+ days — confirm visa legality with Pattaya Visa Help, not gym sales alone.

1-month budget ranges (2026, per person)

  • Train-only + budget apartment (Jomtien/Naklua): ฿25,000–45,000 total.
  • Mid tier gym + decent condo: ฿45,000–70,000.
  • All-inclusive resort month: ฿70,000–120,000+.

Food, scooters, and nightlife are not included — budget another ฿10,000–25,000 for living costs depending on lifestyle.

Housing for a month (train but do not sleep at camp)

Most monthly trainers rent independently:

  • Jomtien — quiet, beach, good if training Venum/Rage/Jomtien walk-in gym (Jomtien guide).
  • Naklua / Wongamat — near Fairtex, Sityodtong (Sityodtong).
  • Central Pattaya — nightlife proximity; commute to east-side camps costs time.

1-month recovery stack

  • Public pools — cheapest gyms guide lists Jomtien/Naklua pools.
  • Yoga — Jomtien/Thepprasit studios in Jomtien guide.
  • Massage — ubiquitous; sports massage ฿300–600/hr common.
  • Injury — Pattaya Medical for clinics if pain is sharp, not dull soreness.

Sample day schedules

Typical 1-week day (single session)

  • 07:00 — light breakfast
  • 08:00–10:00 — morning session (pads + technique)
  • 11:00–15:00 — pool / rest / explore
  • Evening — early sleep; avoid heavy alcohol if training AM

Typical 1-month day (week 3+, two-a-day optional)

  • 07:00–09:00 — morning session
  • 10:00–14:00 — work remotely (coffee guide) or rest
  • 15:00–17:30 — afternoon session OR yoga/swim
  • 18:00 — protein-heavy meal (restaurant guide)

Fights and stadium trips during your stay

One week: watch MAX Muay Thai if cards align — tourist-friendly English. One month: add a Bangkok weekend to Lumpinee or Rajadamnern. Do not book a fight yourself in week one unless you already compete — gyms will guide timeline.

FAQ

Is one week enough to learn Muay Thai in Pattaya?

Enough to learn basics and decide if you love it. Not enough for fight readiness. Five focused sessions beat seven sloppy exhausted ones.

Is one month enough to get good?

You will improve noticeably — cardio, combos, pad flow. “Good” is relative; many tourists train monthly for years. Month one builds the habit loop.

Should I train twice a day on a 1-week holiday?

Only from day 4–5 if recovery is strong. Resorts push two-a-days — listen to your shins and sleep.

What visa do I need for 1 month training?

Many nationalities use 60-day tourist exemption or tourist visa. Longer stays need proper planning — visa guide. Do not trust gym “visa guarantee” marketing without independent verification.

Fairtex or WKO for one month?

Fairtex if you want resort infrastructure and English everywhere. WKO if you want monthly value and will rent your own place. Compare on compare tool.

Can I switch gyms mid-month?

Yes — Pattaya’s advantage is camp density. Pay drop-in or weekly at gym two before cancelling gym one’s monthly contract.
