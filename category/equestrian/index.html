03 Common questions

Equestrian in Pattaya — FAQ.

Can I go horse riding near Pattaya?

Yes. There are equestrian facilities in the Pattaya area offering riding, lessons and, in one case, polo, set in countryside locations. Both venues in this category are listed with their details.

Is horse riding suitable for beginners and children?

Generally yes. Equestrian centres typically offer lessons and led rides for beginners and children alongside facilities for experienced riders. Contact the venue to confirm what is available for your level and age group.

Do I need to book ahead for riding?

Yes. Riding sessions and lessons are scheduled and depend on horse and instructor availability, so book ahead rather than turning up. The countryside location also means planning your transport in advance.
