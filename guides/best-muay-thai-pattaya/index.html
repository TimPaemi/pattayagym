Common questions

What is the best Muay Thai gym in Pattaya for beginners?

Sityodtong, Fairtex, and Kombat Group all run dedicated beginner programs in English. Most camps will pair beginners with patient pad-holders for the first 2-4 weeks of fundamentals.

How much does Muay Thai cost in Pattaya?

Drop-in sessions ฿300-฿500. Monthly unlimited training ฿4,000-฿15,000 depending on prestige. All-inclusive resort camps with accommodation ฿20,000-฿60,000/month.

Do I need to be fit to start Muay Thai in Pattaya?

No. Most camps explicitly welcome total beginners. Expect to be tired in week one — by week three, you'll be coasting through 2-hour sessions.
