Padel & pickleball.

By TimPaemi · 6 min read · Updated 2026-06-02 · How we rank →

Pattaya has dedicated padel and pickleball courts before most Thai cities — indoor Pratamnak, lakeside Mabprachan, and social leagues. This guide maps where to book.

Pattaya · 157 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

Padel and pickleball are the fastest-growing racquet sports in Southeast Asia — and Pattaya already has dedicated courts while Bangkok is still catching up. This guide maps every bookable padel and pickleball venue in our directory, how they differ, what to budget, and where to stay so you are not commuting 40 minutes for a one-hour court.

Start with racquet sports in Pattaya, Pratamnak racquet listings, and Naklua & Pratamnak gym guide if you combine court sport with Muay Thai or gym training.

Padel vs pickleball in Pattaya — which sport?

Padel is enclosed-court tennis with walls — doubles-first, social, explosive growth in expat circles. Pickleball is smaller court, slower pace, huge in North America and gaining fast among 50+ and beginner racquet players in Thailand. Pattaya has two dedicated padel clubs and one dedicated pickleball facility, plus multi-racquet centres that include pickleball courts.

If you want nightlife-adjacent sport with serious coaching infrastructure, padel on Pratamnak is the default. If you want beginner-friendly social leagues and lower physical load, pickleball on Pratamnak Soi 6 is the entry point.

Play Padel Pattaya — indoor courts, Pratamnak

Play Padel Pattaya at Kasetsin Soi 3, Pratamnak, was Pattaya's first dedicated padel address and among Thailand's first indoor padel installations. Hourly court booking, equipment rental, clinics for all levels. ฿฿฿. Walkable from the Royal Cliff / InterContinental luxury cluster — good for hotel guests who want padel without a long taxi.

Pick this if: you want reliable indoor play in rain season (May–Oct). Skip if: you need budget hourly rates — compare with lakeside option below.

Pattaya Padel Club — Mabprachan Lake

Pattaya Padel Club on Mabprachan Reservoir (east Pattaya / Darkside side) offers lakeside outdoor padel — different vibe from indoor Pratamnak. Pattaya's second dedicated padel facility. ฿฿. Roughly 20–25 minutes from central Pattaya or Jomtien; plan a scooter or Grab.

Pick this if: you like outdoor sport away from the beach strip. Pair with east Pattaya venues if you train Muay Thai at Kombat Group or Sanit Sport Club nearby.

Pickleball Pattaya — dedicated Pratamnak courts

Pickleball Pattaya on Pratumnak Soi 6 runs two full-sized courts, paddles and balls, ball machine, strong lighting for evening play, and beginner lessons from an English-speaking licensed coach. ฿฿. Social leagues and 50+ groups are common here — check their site for current schedule.

Pick this if: you are new to racquet sport or want structured beginner coaching. Skip if: you only play padel — this is pickleball-specific.

Multi-racquet venues (tennis + pickleball + badminton)

  • Pattaya Tennis & Badminton Inter Club — pickleball, badminton, tennis, table tennis under one roof.
  • Fitz Club (Royal Cliff) — seven tennis courts, squash, luxury tier; padel players sometimes cross-train here for footwork.
  • Pattaya Tennis Club, Tara Tennis Club, TOS Tennis — tennis-first but relevant if you want racquet sport without padel/pickleball specialization.

Where to stay and train around the courts

Most dedicated padel/pickleball is on Pratamnak Hill — stay Pratamnak or Naklua for <15 min to Play Padel and Pickleball Pattaya. See Naklua & Pratamnak gyms for Muscle Factory, resort gyms, and Muay Thai north side.

Jomtien long-stays: 20–30 min to Pratamnak courts — viable if you train at the beach but budget taxi time for league nights.

Sample weekly rhythm (padel + gym)

  • Mon/Wed/Fri AM: padel clinic or open play at Play Padel
  • Tue/Thu: strength at Muscle Factory or SUN Fitness
  • Sat: pickleball social at Pickleball Pattaya
  • Sun: rest — Pattaya Restaurant Guide recovery meal

Prices and booking (what to expect)

Dedicated padel hourly rates in Pattaya typically run ฿800–1,500+ per court-hour depending on peak time and indoor vs outdoor — always confirm on the venue website before budgeting a month. Pickleball walk-in and league fees vary; beginner lesson packages are often the best first purchase.

We do not publish live prices as sole truth — venues change rates seasonally. Each listing links to official contact or booking pages.

FAQ

Where can I play padel in Pattaya?

Play Padel Pattaya (indoor, Pratamnak) and Pattaya Padel Club (lakeside, Mabprachan). Full racquet category: tennis / padel / squash.

Is there pickleball in Pattaya?

Yes — Pickleball Pattaya on Pratumnak Soi 6 is the flagship dedicated facility. Some multi-sport clubs also list pickleball.

Do I need my own paddle or racket?

Both dedicated clubs offer rental. Bring court shoes with non-marking soles; Thailand humidity wears grips fast — budget ฿200–400 for a basic overgrip bundle at local sport shops if you stay a month.

Can beginners join without a partner?

Pickleball Pattaya runs beginner sessions and social play where partners rotate. Padel is doubles-heavy — clinics and club nights are the easiest entry; call ahead for solo placement.

Padel near Jomtien Beach?

No dedicated padel on Jomtien strip — plan 15–25 min to Pratamnak. Combine with Jomtien gym guide if you live south.

Is racquet sport good for Muay Thai recovery days?

Light pickleball or padel footwork sessions work as active recovery; avoid hard padel the day before a fight camp sparring block. Training rhythm: 1 week vs 1 month guide.

Padel vs pickleball — which to learn first

Pickleball has a lower learning curve — smaller court, underhand serve, social doubles culture. Good for mixed-age groups and active recovery between fight weeks. Padel is faster, wall-play heavy, and equipment-specific — invest in a lesson before assuming tennis skill transfers. Pattaya has more dedicated pickleball social nights than padel league depth; padel is growing with Pratamnak indoor courts.

Court booking and peak hours

Evenings 17:00–20:00 fill first at covered Pratamnak courts — book via club LINE or website where listed. Midday heat makes outdoor pickleball brutal May–October; morning slots or indoor padel win. Weekend expat leagues spike Nov–Feb — tourists should book 48 hours ahead.

Combine racquet with Muay Thai without overtraining

Padel footwork and lateral stops stress ankles already battered from shin conditioning. Schedule racquet on rest days or light technical MT days — not the morning before hard sparring. Hydration and electrolytes matter double in Pattaya humidity. Strength maintenance: Pratamnak iron or central day pass.

Equipment brands and balls

Padel rackets differ from tennis — rent at club first before buying. Pickleball uses perforated plastic balls — outdoor balls vs indoor balls affect bounce; clubs stock appropriate type. Sunglasses and hat for outdoor pickleball midday sessions.

Leagues and expat social play

Pickleball Pattaya runs the most approachable social ladder for newcomers — introduce yourself as beginner on first visit. Padel doubles needs four — clubs often post "looking for fourth" on LINE groups linked from venue pages. Post-match food: Restaurant Guide near Pratamnak.

Indoor padel avoids midday UV — book 08:00 or 17:00 slots in April heat. Wear court shoes with non-mark soles; black running soles mark acrylic courts and annoy clubs.
