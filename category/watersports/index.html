03 Common questions

Watersports / Diving in Pattaya — FAQ.

Can I learn to scuba dive in Pattaya?

Yes. Pattaya is one of Thailand most accessible places to learn, with several PADI and SSI dive centres, including 5-star rated operators, running courses from intro Discover Scuba experiences up to professional instructor training. Dive sites include nearby islands and several wrecks. Check each operator page for its certifications and course range.

What watersports can you do in Pattaya besides diving?

Plenty: kitesurfing and wing-foiling (especially off Jomtien), stand-up paddleboarding, sailing and yacht charter, jet skis, parasailing, wakeboarding at cable parks, and snorkelling trips to Koh Larn. This category lists operators for each activity.

When is the best season for diving and watersports?

Diving is possible year-round, with visibility generally best in the dry season. Kitesurfing depends on wind: Jomtien main wind season runs roughly November to March, with a secondary summer window. Operators run regardless of season, so contact them for current conditions.

Do I need experience or my own equipment?

No. Dive courses start from complete beginner level, and kitesurf, paddleboard and sailing schools cater to first-timers with all equipment included. Bring swimwear and sun protection; everything else is provided. Diving courses include a short medical questionnaire.
