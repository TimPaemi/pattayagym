Overview

Pattana Sports Resort is the largest integrated sports-and-golf resort in the Pattaya region — a 400-acre property in Si Racha combining a 27-hole championship golf course, multi-sport training facilities, multiple accommodation tiers, a 7,000-capacity stadium, and conference and event infrastructure under a single resort operation. Marketed as "Actively Together", Pattana positions itself as the destination for sports-themed group travel — corporate retreats, sports tournaments, training camps, family active vacations, and golf groups — rather than as a pure-golf or pure-resort product.

The 27-hole golf layout — divided into the Andreas, Brookei, and Calypso nines — gives Pattana the same routing flexibility as Khao Kheow Country Club and Laem Chabang International, with players combining any two nines for an 18-hole round. The course rolls through 400-acre property with mountain and lake views, fast greens, strategic bunkers, and tree-lined paths — a classic championship-tier configuration.

What distinguishes Pattana from neighboring Pattaya golf options is the integrated sports resort layer: the 7,000-capacity stadium (rare in resort properties), the multi-sport training facilities, and the four accommodation tiers (hotel rooms, mansion-style lodgings, villa home stays, affordable apartments) all on one property. Sports teams, training camps, and tournaments can run on-site without leaving the property — a value proposition no other Pattaya golf resort offers at this scale.

The 400-Acre Property

Geographic Setting

  • Si Racha District, Chonburi — north of Pattaya
  • 40 km from central Pattaya — ~40-45 minute drive
  • 90 minutes from Bangkok — strategic for Bangkok-day-trip and conference travel
  • Neighboring coastal areas — Bangsaen, Pattaya, Rayong all accessible
  • Mountain and lake views dominate the property

Land Use

The 400-acre property accommodates:

  • 27-hole championship golf course (largest land share)
  • Multi-tier accommodation (4 distinct types)
  • 7,000-capacity stadium for sports events
  • Multi-sport training facilities (varied sports)
  • 3 restaurants with course views
  • Outdoor pool + children's pool
  • Spa with aromatherapy services
  • Meeting and event facilities
  • Driving range and practice areas

This scale enables simultaneous group activities — a corporate retreat can have golfers on the course, training sessions in the stadium, families at the pool, and conferences in meeting rooms all running concurrently.

The 27-Hole Golf Course

Layout

  • 27 holes — divided into three nine-hole courses
  • Names: Andreas, Brookei, Calypso — each with unique character
  • Par 72 for typical 18-hole round combinations
  • Multi-tee design for varied skill levels

Course Characteristics

The course design emphasises:

  • Rolling fairways — natural Si Racha land contours
  • Fast greens — premium agronomy
  • Strategic bunkers placed for shot decision-making
  • Tree-lined paths between holes
  • Mountain backdrop in many holes
  • Lake views integrated into specific holes

Three Nines

Each nine has a distinct character and challenge profile:

  • Andreas — typically considered the most demanding test
  • Brookei — balanced strategic mid-tier
  • Calypso — most accessible of the three

Players combining Andreas + Brookei typically get the championship-tournament round; Brookei + Calypso plays as a friendlier mixed-ability round.

Sports Resort — Beyond Golf

The 7,000-Capacity Stadium

A 7,000-capacity stadium is rare for a Pattaya / Eastern Seaboard resort property. Possible uses include:

  • Football / soccer matches — friendly tournaments, training camps
  • Athletics events — track and field, running events
  • Concerts and performances — non-sports event hosting
  • Corporate sports events — large-scale team-building
  • Tournament hosting — regional sports finals

This scale puts Pattana in a category usually occupied by university or municipal facilities.

Multi-Sport Training Facilities

Beyond the stadium, the resort offers:

  • Football / soccer fields
  • Tennis courts (likely multi-court)

  • Fitness center for resort guests
  • Track and field facilities

This makes Pattana a destination for sports training camps — local teams, international visitors, school sports programs all use the resort for intensive training.
