★ About this venue

Flight of the Gibbon — Khao Kheow Canopy Zipline

Overview

Flight of the Gibbon is the largest canopy zip-line tour in all of Thailand — a 3-kilometre course with 24 platforms routed through the jungle canopy inside Khao Kheow Open Zoo (which itself sits inside the Chompoo Wildlife Sanctuary) approximately 1 hour drive from Pattaya / 90 minutes from Bangkok. The course combines ziplines, double ziplines, abseiling stations, sky bridges, and spiral staircases into a 2.5-hour guided experience with two Sky Rangers per group for safety and rainforest interpretation.

The canopy setting is fundamentally different from Pattaya's other zipline options (Tarzan Adventure 25-platform jungle course, Pattaya Park Tower Jump 56th-floor cable). Flight of the Gibbon delivers a genuine rainforest canopy experience inside a protected wildlife sanctuary — including the rare possibility of spotting wild gibbons while ziplining. The location's protected status, the 3 km course length (longer than Tarzan's 25 platforms), and the package inclusions (hotel pickup + Khao Kheow Open Safari Park tour + local Thai lunch) make it a full-day adventure-tourism experience rather than a short add-on activity.

For Pattaya visitors who want the most ambitious zipline experience available within day-trip distance — and especially those willing to pair the zipline with the world-class Khao Kheow Open Zoo (one of Thailand's best wildlife parks) — Flight of the Gibbon is genuinely the apex of regional canopy adventure.

What "Flight of the Gibbon" Means

The Brand Origins

Flight of the Gibbon is a Thailand-based canopy zipline operator that pioneered eco-conscious zipline tourism in Asia:

  • Original location: Mae Kampong / Chiang Mai (mountain north Thailand)
  • Pattaya / Khao Kheow location added later
  • Conservation messaging integrated into tours
  • Wildlife habitat protection as part of the brand mission
  • Sky Ranger guides trained as both safety + naturalist interpretation

Why Conservation Framing Matters

Most adventure tourism (skydive, kart, shooting) is purely thrill-driven. Flight of the Gibbon's framing emphasises:

  • Education about Thai rainforest ecosystems
  • Wildlife conservation awareness
  • Eco-tourism standards
  • Protected sanctuary access
  • Gibbon population restoration programs (the brand contributes to)

For environmentally-conscious travellers, this matters meaningfully.

The Khao Kheow Setting

Geographic Context

Khao Kheow Open Zoo is a 2,000-acre wildlife sanctuary in Si Racha District (Chonburi):

  • 8,000+ animals from 300+ species
  • Open zoo concept — animals in expanded enclosures vs. cages
  • Multiple zones — wildlife sanctuary, recreation, research breeding
  • 57 minutes from Pattaya typical drive
  • Combinable with Khao Kheow Country Club (Pete Dye golf nearby)

The Flight of the Gibbon course routes through the canopy of the surrounding rainforest — meaning:

  • Genuinely wild rainforest (not landscaped tourist gardens)
  • Wildlife visible from canopy elevation
  • Protected-area access rare for tourist activities
  • Conservation context integrated

Wild Gibbon Possibility

The course name reflects the possibility of spotting wild gibbons in the canopy:

  • Pileated gibbons native to the region
  • Wild populations in Khao Kheow sanctuary
  • Sightings not guaranteed but possible
  • Sky Rangers trained to spot and identify

This wildlife crossover is fundamentally different from purely-engineered ziplines at urban / commercial parks.

The 3 km Course / 24 Platforms

Course Elements

The 24-platform course features:

Ziplines (Primary)

  • Multiple cable spans between trees / platforms
  • Various lengths — short technical to long flight
  • Heights vary across the course
  • Sea views in some sections (Khao Kheow elevation)

Double Ziplines

  • Side-by-side cables for paired riders
  • Shared experience with friend / partner
  • Race or relaxed depending on group dynamic

Abseils

  • Rope descent from platforms
  • Controlled speed with brake mechanism
  • Adventure variety beyond pure zipline

Sky Bridges

  • Elevated walkways between trees
  • Stability practice — narrow walking platforms
  • Visual drama — looking down 20-30m+

Spiral Staircases

  • Spiral ascending between platform tiers
  • Wood / metal construction integrated with trees
  • Architectural feature of course

Why 3 km Matters

3 kilometres of canopy course is substantial:

  • Most canopy tours globally are 1-2 km
  • Tarzan Adventure (Pattaya): ~1.5 km estimated
  • 3 km = top-tier ambition in Asia
  • Multi-element variety keeps the experience engaging across the length

What's Included

All-Inclusive Day Package

Flight of the Gibbon's standard package includes:

  • Hotel pickup + return transport from Pattaya / Bangkok
  • The 2.5-hour zipline tour itself
  • Tour of Khao Kheow Open Safari Park — included free
  • Local Thai lunch — typical Thai food at sanctuary restaurant
  • Two Sky Rangers per group — safety + interpretation
  • Helmet + harness + carabiner equipment
  • Insurance typically

For a single ticket, the package delivers a full 6-8 hour adventure-tourism day — meaningful value vs. shorter activities.

Safety & Requirements

Physical Requirements

  • Minimum height: 1 metre (3 feet 3 inches)
  • Maximum weight: 130 kg
  • Pregnant women: not allowed
  • Heart conditions: consult physician
  • Recent surgery: verify clearance
  • Closed-toe shoes mandatory

Safety Equipment

  • Helmet (mandatory)
  • Climbing harness (mandatory, fitted at start)
  • Pulleys / carabiners professional-tier
  • Backup safety lines at every transfer
  • Sky Rangers trained for emergency response
  • First aid at base

Sky Rangers

The two Sky Rangers per group are:

  • Trained safety professionals — proper rope rescue training
  • Multi-year experience typically
  • Multi-language capability (English / Thai standard, others varied)
  • Naturalist interpretation — point out wildlife, plants, ecology
  • Group size management — keep guests on schedule and safe

Pricing (Indicative)

Flight of the Gibbon Pattaya pricing:

  • Standard package (zip line + zoo + lunch + transfer): ~฿3,500-5,000
  • Premium packages with extra activities: ~฿5,000-7,000
  • Group / family discounts available
  • Online booking discounts via Klook, Headout, GetYourGuide, KKday (often 20-35%)
  • Special promotions during low season

This is premium-tier adventure pricing but the all-inclusive package delivers meaningful value vs. paying for each component separately.
