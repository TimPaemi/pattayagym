★ About this venue

Pattaya Kart Speedway

Overview

Pattaya Kart Speedway is the first karting track in Pattaya — opened on Thepprasit Road Soi 9 in 1987 and continuously operating for 35+ years, making it one of the longest-running motorsport venues in the city alongside the Bira Circuit FIA-rated motor racing track. While the newer EasyKart Pattaya Thepprasit (also Thepprasit Road) brings fresher modern infrastructure, Pattaya Kart Speedway brings the original-and-still-active Pattaya karting heritage plus a meaningfully expanded multi-activity offering including ATV riding, paintballing, bungee jumping, and other sporting activities integrated into a single adventure compound.

The two-track configuration mirrors EasyKart's: 450-metre beginner track for amateurs, children, and double-seater karts, plus a 750-metre professional track for experienced drivers wanting higher-speed action. The 750m pro track is slightly shorter than EasyKart's 800m racing track but the 35-year operating heritage plus bundled adventure activities give Pattaya Kart Speedway a different value proposition.

For Pattaya tourists wanting multi-activity adventure days combining karting + ATV + paintball + bungee under single venue management, Pattaya Kart Speedway is genuinely the only consolidated option. EasyKart is karting-only; Pattaya Kart Speedway is the multi-activity adventure park.

The 1987 Heritage

Why 35+ Years Matters

Continuous operation since 1987 puts Pattaya Kart Speedway in the foundational generation of Pattaya tourism infrastructure:

  • Pre-Walking Street modern character — Thepprasit was rural at the time
  • Generations of Pattaya tourists have visited
  • Established crew and operations with deep institutional knowledge
  • Track / equipment maintenance routines refined over decades
  • Community relationships with local Pattaya tourism operators

This heritage doesn't make Pattaya Kart Speedway "better" than EasyKart, but it gives it a different cultural resonance — generations of visitors have raced here.

Two-Track Configuration

Beginner Track (450 metres)

  • For amateurs, children, and family
  • Wider turns for confidence-building
  • Lower speed limits
  • Mini-karts and double-seater carts available
  • Safety priority over speed
  • Suitable for ages 7+ with adult supervision

Professional Track (750 metres)

  • For experienced drivers
  • Tighter turns + faster straights
  • Higher speed limits
  • Single-seater faster karts
  • Real racing feel
  • Suitable for age 15+ with prior experience

Multi-Activity Adventure Compound

Beyond Karting

Pattaya Kart Speedway integrates several adventure activities into one venue:

ATV Riding

  • All-Terrain Vehicle rides on dirt circuits
  • Combinable with karting for kart-and-ATV days
  • Suitable for older teens and adults
  • Helmet + safety gear provided

Paintballing

  • Paintball field on-site
  • Equipment rental — markers, masks, ammunition
  • Group bookings for friend / corporate parties
  • Different day-out from karting

Bungee Jumping

  • Bungee tower on-site
  • Adrenaline activity complement to other adventure
  • Safety harness + cable professional setup
  • Weight / health restrictions apply

Other Sport Activities

  • Various additional sport offerings at the compound
  • Confirm current options at venue — menu varies seasonally

Why Multi-Activity Matters

For groups (bachelor parties, corporate events, family adventure days, friend trips), the single-venue convenience is meaningful:

  • One booking vs. coordinating multiple venues
  • Single transport trip
  • Combined pricing packages
  • Group discounts apply across activities
  • Mixed-energy day — karting fast, paintball tactical, bungee adrenaline, ATV exploration

Hours

Daily 09:30-18:30 — 9-hour operating window:

  • Morning sessions less crowded
  • Afternoon peak — most popular hours
  • Earlier close than EasyKart (which goes to 01:00) — daytime activity only
