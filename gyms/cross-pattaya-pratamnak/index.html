★ About this venue

Cross Pattaya Pratamnak

Overview

Cross Pattaya Pratamnak is a 5-star hotel on Phra Tamnak Hill whose Cloud Club fitness centre and dedicated PLAYROOM games space position it as a hotel-integrated fitness-and-recreation destination rather than a standalone gym. The hotel operates with a wellness-and-leisure lifestyle pitch that blends serious fitness equipment with family-friendly recreation amenities — making it distinctive among Pattaya hotel fitness offerings.

Cloud Club itself is fully equipped for varied workout intensities — treadmills and cardio for high-energy training, strength equipment for resistance work, and a quiet yoga space for low-impact mind-body practice. Complimentary towels, refreshments, and snacks are provided to members and guests, and private yoga lessons or personal trainer sessions can be arranged with 24 hours notice.

The PLAYROOM is the hotel's distinguishing feature — a dedicated recreation room with board games and indoor football (likely a foosball or small-pitch indoor football setup), marketed with a funky and stylish atmosphere. This positions Cross Pattaya Pratamnak as a family / group / millennials-or-younger demographic hotel rather than a pure-business or pure-couples property.

For visitors staying at Cross Pattaya Pratamnak — and for Pratamnak Hill residents who can access the facility through day passes or memberships — the property offers a distinctive blend of serious fitness, wellness yoga, and active recreation that no other Pattaya hotel quite matches.

Equipment & Layout

The Cloud Club is fully equipped for activities of every intensity:

  • Treadmills — multiple units, brand-name (typical 5-star hotel spec)
  • Stationary bikes — upright + recumbent
  • Ellipticals / cross-trainers
  • Possible rowers / SkiErgs at premium hotel level

Strength Zone

  • Pin-loaded machines for full muscle group coverage
  • Free weights — dumbbells, possibly some barbells
  • Cable machines
  • Functional training equipment (TRX, kettlebells, etc.)

Yoga / Mind-Body Space

  • Quiet space for yoga — separate from main gym floor
  • Mats, blocks, straps typical equipment
  • Used for stretching, yoga, mobility, meditation
  • Class capacity for small group sessions

Amenities

  • Locker rooms with showers

PLAYROOM — The Distinguishing Feature

What It Is

The PLAYROOM is Cross Pattaya Pratamnak's most distinctive amenity:

  • Dedicated games / recreation room
  • Board games available for guest use
  • Indoor football — likely foosball table and/or small indoor football pitch / goal setup
  • Funky and stylish atmosphere — design-forward
  • Family / group friendly
  • Low-pressure recreation — not fitness-intense

Why This Matters

Most Pattaya hotels offer gym + pool + spa as standard. The PLAYROOM adds:

  • Active recreation beyond fitness — sometimes you don't want to "work out"
  • Family bonding — kids and adults can play together
  • Group entertainment — friend trips can game indoors during rain
  • Millennial appeal — shared experiences over dedicated sweat sessions
  • Differentiation — competitor hotels don't have this

Pratamnak Hill Setting

Geographic Advantages

  • Phra Tamnak Hill location
  • Pratamnak beach opposite the hotel
  • Hidden Pratamnak hill beach minutes away — quieter alternative
  • Walking access to Pattaya Beach (north) and Jomtien Beach (south)
  • Sea views from many hotel rooms
  • Quieter than central Pattaya nightlife
  • Buddha Hill (Khao Phra Tamnak) small temple at top is local landmark

Combined with Hotel Amenities

The setting + amenities combination creates a self-contained leisure destination — guests don't need to leave the hotel to have fitness, food, recreation, beach, and spa all available.

Hotel Tier — 5-Star

The 5-star designation signals:

  • Premium room rates (typically ฿3,000-7,000+/night)
  • High service standards — concierge, room service, etc.
  • Multiple restaurants and bars
  • Comprehensive amenities beyond fitness
  • Modern design and infrastructure

This positions Cross Pattaya Pratamnak meaningfully above mid-tier hotels (Manhattan Pattaya) in price and service.

Day Pass / Non-Guest Access

Direct day pass policies vary — typical 5-star hotel approaches:

  • Hotel guests free access to Cloud Club + pool + PLAYROOM
  • Non-hotel-guest day pass — often available, typically ฿500-1,200
  • Short-term gym membership for Pratamnak residents — sometimes offered
  • Combined spa + fitness packages for premium experience

Direct contact recommended to verify current rates and availability.
