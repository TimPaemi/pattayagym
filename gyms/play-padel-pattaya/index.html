Overview

Play Padel Pattaya is the first dedicated padel facility in Pattaya AND the first indoor padel courts in all of Thailand — a genuinely pioneering venue for the world's fastest-growing racquet sport. Located on Kasetsin Soi 3 in Pratumnak, the facility features 6 air-conditioning units per court and a 11.5-metre high roof, providing fully climate-controlled play regardless of outside weather. Padel — a hybrid of tennis and squash played in glass-walled courts with a perforated paddle — has exploded globally over the past decade (estimated 30M+ players worldwide as of 2025), and Play Padel brings that boom to Pattaya in a first-mover position.

For travellers and Pattaya residents wanting the year-round comfort of indoor AC padel in Thailand's climate, Play Padel is essentially the only option in Pattaya. The competition (Prime Padel Pattaya, Pattaya Padel Club) is more recent and operates differently — Play Padel's first-mover + climate-controlled combination is its core differentiator.

Why Padel — and Why First in Pattaya Matters

Padel is the racquet sport revolution:

  • Originated in Mexico (1969) — invented by Enrique Corcuera
  • Spain became the global epicenter — now a national sport with 5M+ Spanish players
  • Global expansion 2015-2025 — fastest-growing racquet sport globally
  • Played on enclosed glass-walled courts with perforated solid paddles and depressurized tennis balls
  • Easier to learn than tennis, more dynamic than squash — broad demographic appeal

Thailand was a late adopter — most padel facilities in Asia opened post-2020. Play Padel Pattaya was the first dedicated padel club in Pattaya AND the first indoor padel facility in Thailand. This first-mover advantage means:

  • Established player community — the Pattaya padel scene revolves around Play Padel
  • Trained licensed coaches ready before competitor facilities opened
  • Tournament hosting infrastructure developed over multiple years
  • Brand recognition — the de facto "Pattaya padel" reference

The Climate-Controlled Court Specification

Play Padel's facility specs reflect serious investment:

Per Court

  • 6 AC units per court — extraordinary cooling capacity
  • 11.5 metre high roof — meets international padel ceiling height standards (no obstruction for high lobs and overhead shots)
  • Fully indoor and climate-controlled — playable regardless of outside conditions
  • Glass walls — international padel standard
  • Synthetic turf surface — proper padel court surface

Why AC Matters

In Thailand:

  • Year-round average temperatures 28-35°C with high humidity
  • Outdoor padel becomes punishing March-May (hot season) and during midday year-round
  • Humidity affects ball physics — depressurized balls lose performance differently than tennis balls
  • Indoor AC = consistent ball behavior + comfortable play + year-round usability

For serious padel players, AC indoor courts are the gold standard that most casual outdoor courts cannot match.

Coaching

The facility employs licensed trainers who:

  • Teach beginners — proper grip, basic strokes, court positioning
  • Develop intermediate players — strategy, shot variety, doubles play
  • Coach advanced players — match tactics, tournament prep
  • Run group clinics — multi-student structured sessions
  • Private 1-on-1 lessons — for focused development

Padel licensing typically follows the International Padel Federation (FIP) standards or national federation certification (Spanish federation FEP standards being the global benchmark).

Pricing & Booking

Play Padel publishes pricing on its Prices and Membership page. Indicative based on Pattaya padel market:

  • Court rental (1 hour): ~฿800-1,200 per court (4 players split this)
  • Per-player effective rate: ~฿200-300/hour for 4-player doubles
  • Membership packages: discounted rates for regulars
  • Coaching session: ~฿1,500-3,000/hour with licensed trainer
  • Equipment rental: rackets and balls available
  • Multi-court group bookings: discounted

For exact 2026 rates: playpadelpattaya.com or contact via Facebook / Instagram.

Schedule & Booking

  • Daily availability — bookable in 1-hour slots
  • Online booking via website
  • Peak hours (evenings, weekends) book up quickly
  • Off-peak discounts typically available
