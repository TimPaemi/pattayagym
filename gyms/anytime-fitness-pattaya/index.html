Anytime Fitness is the world's largest 24/7 gym franchise — over 5,000 clubs across 50+ countries — and Pattaya carries two well-run locations giving members the same key-fob, walk-in-anytime, train-anywhere model that built the brand. If you live in Pattaya part-time, travel a lot, or simply want a clean, lights-always-on, no-drama box to lift in at 4am after a long flight, this is the answer.

The Pattaya footprint splits across two clubs: Anytime Fitness Bukis Point South Pattaya sits inside Bukis Point Mall on Thappraya Road, walking distance from Jomtien and Pratamnak — the core South Pattaya residential corridor where most expat retirees and digital nomads actually live. Anytime Fitness "Again Pattaya" is the second club, mid-city. Both are franchised by the same Thailand operator, share equipment standards, and are accessible by any Anytime Fitness member globally with a single key-fob.

Position in Pattaya market

Pattaya's gym landscape is barbell-heavy and bro-coded — Tony's Gym, Universe Gym, Elite Gym Pattaya, Fitness 7 — most of these are either old-school iron palaces or mall-based 24-hour boxes (Jetts, Fitness Plus). Anytime Fitness is the clean, predictable, globally-standardized option. You walk in and it looks identical to the Anytime Fitness in Sydney, Manila, or Manchester: same mid-tier Life Fitness / Matrix / Precor gear, same friendly-but-not-pushy front desk, same "swipe in, train, swipe out" rhythm. For people who travel for work, who left a US/UK/AU Anytime Fitness membership behind, or who just want a gym that isn't going to test their tolerance for grunting and dropped plates — this is the spot.

Locations

Anytime Fitness Bukis Point — South Pattaya

Bukis Point is a small lifestyle mall on Thappraya Road, the spine connecting Pattaya proper to Jomtien Beach. The gym is on an upper level, well-lit, and benefits from mall security plus its own access control. Convenient parking, easy scooter drop-off, and surrounding F&B (cafés, healthy lunch spots, a few salons) mean members rarely need to leave the building between work, training, and a post-session coffee.

This is the busier of the two clubs because of catchment — Pratamnak high-rise condo dwellers, Jomtien residents, and Thappraya Road expats all funnel through here. Peak hours are early evening (17:30–19:30) on weekdays. Pre-7am and post-21:00 it's near-empty in the best way.

Anytime Fitness "Again Pattaya"

"Again" is its own small Facebook-page community (af.againpattaya). Slightly different layout, similar equipment lineup, and a more locally-focused mid-city catchment. Worth scoping both before committing if you live equidistant.

Membership model

The Anytime Fitness Thailand offer is built around friction reduction:

  • 24-hour access via secure key-fob — walk in any hour, any day, any club globally
  • Manned hours with staff and trainers usually 09:00–21:00 (varies by location)
  • No long contracts — most members are on rolling monthly or short-term plans
  • Worldwide reciprocity — your Pattaya fob works at any of 5,000+ Anytime Fitness clubs globally for up to ~30 days/year free, or extended for a small fee
  • 1-day free trial — drop in, try the gym, sign up if it fits

Pricing is mid-market ฿฿: more than Tony's Gym (cheap iron palace) and Jetts (mass-market), less than the Hilton/Cape Dara hotel club day-pass culture. Personal training is bookable per-session or as packages with the in-house trainers.

Programming & coaching

Coaches in Pattaya tend to be a mix of Thai certified PTs and resident expat trainers (rotation varies — ask at the desk). Standard offerings:

  • 1-on-1 personal training (strength, fat loss, sport-specific prep)
  • Small group training (HIIT, kettlebell, conditioning)
  • Free fitness assessment for new members
  • Specialized classes (varies by club and season — check the schedule)

Don't expect Equinox-grade boutique class production. Do expect competent, accountable coaching that actually shows up to your booked sessions.

How to visit

The fastest path: drop a message via the Bukis Point Facebook (anytimefitness.southpattaya) or the "Again Pattaya" page (af.againpattaya) requesting a free trial pass. Show up during manned hours (mid-morning or mid-afternoon is ideal — quietest tour windows) with workout gear and a passport / Thai ID. The team will walk you through the floor, kit you with a temporary fob, and let you train. If you sign up, you keep the fob and gain immediate 24-hour access plus reciprocity at every Anytime Fitness club worldwide.

For travelling members from another country, your existing membership often covers Pattaya for free up to your home plan's reciprocity allowance — check with your home club before flying so you don't pay twice.
