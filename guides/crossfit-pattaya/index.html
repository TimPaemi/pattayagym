CrossFit in Pattaya.

By Tim Paemi · 6 min read · Updated 2026-06-01 · How we rank →

There is one CrossFit affiliate in Pattaya — Jungle Gym in Nong Prue. This guide covers class schedules, the multi-discipline campus, who should live on the Darkside, and what to do when you cannot commute from central or Jomtien.

Pattaya · 157 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

Pattaya has one legitimate CrossFit affiliate — CrossFit Pattaya @ Jungle Gym in Nong Prue (East Pattaya / Darkside). That single-box reality does not mean functional fitness stops elsewhere: hotel Technogym floors, iron gyms, and combat S&C classes fill the gap for Beach Road tourists. This guide explains the affiliate honestly, who should commute to Nong Prue, and what to do if you cannot.

CrossFit Pattaya @ Jungle Gym — the only affiliate

Address: 165/4 Moo 6, Nong Prue, Bang Lamung 20150. Phone: +66 84 818 3994. Website: junglegympattaya.com. Owner-head coach Murray runs programming; venue copy describes CrossFit as the premier programme while "the Jungle Gym is our identity" — meaning the campus is a multi-discipline playground, not a minimalist white-wall box.

Scheduled CrossFit classes (on file)

  • Monday–Friday: 09:00–10:00 and 19:00–20:00
  • Saturday: 09:00
  • Open gym — extended hours beyond class blocks; confirm at reception

Reviews in our venue file highlight Murray as attentive on form, scales WODs for ability, and varies programming — important if you visit from another affiliate and expect familiar cycle structure.

What else is on the same campus

Jungle Gym is unusual for a CrossFit affiliate:

  • Personal training, small-group training, nutrition programming
  • DNA testing for training/nutrition profiles (uncommon in Pattaya)
  • MMA and self-defence
  • Target archery, archery tag, dodgeball, slacklining, yoga
  • Brand partners: Under Armour and Decathlon listed on file

Families with mixed interests: one parent WODs while kids try archery — pair with kids & youth sport guide.

Who should base on the Darkside for CrossFit

  • Long-stay functional fitness athletes — monthly packs beat daily taxis from central Pattaya.
  • Fight campers — stack morning MT at Kombat Group or Sor Klinmee with evening or off-day WODs at Jungle Gym.
  • Visiting CrossFitters — drop-in once; confirm drop-in fee and fundamentals requirement on WhatsApp before you fly.
  • Residents near Mabprachan / Nong Prue — Castra, east camps, and Jungle Gym form a coherent belt.

Who should not commute daily: one-week central-Pattaya holidays tied to Walking Street — use alternatives below and treat Jungle Gym as a single field-trip day.

Geography — Nong Prue and the Darkside

Nong Prue sits east of Sukhumvit's tourist core — scooter or car is normal; baht-bus combinations are slower. Plan 25–40 minutes from Beach Road at rush hour. If you train MT at Kombat (Huai Yai) and WOD at Jungle Gym, housing on the Darkside saves hours weekly. Area context: East Pattaya guide · East × CrossFit hub.

FAQ

Is Jungle Gym the only CrossFit box in Pattaya?

Yes — it is the only CrossFit affiliate listed in our 157-venue directory for Pattaya city. Bangkok and Phuket have multiple affiliates; plan travel days if you need a second box culture.

Can beginners attend CrossFit classes?

Beginner-friendly is on file, but affiliates often require an intro fundamentals block — ask Murray's team before dropping into a random WOD. Beginners guide for non-CrossFit options.

What time slot is best for tourists?

19:00 classes fit fight campers who train mornings. 09:00 classes suit retirees and nomads on the Darkside. Saturday 09:00 is the weekend anchor — arrive early in high season.

Can I combine archery and CrossFit same day?

Same campus — yes, but shoulder fatigue matters. Many members split archery to rest days. Book both at reception and disclose if you sparred or pad-worked that morning.

How do I get from Jomtien to Jungle Gym?

Taxi or owned scooter is typical; public transport is slow. If you are Jomtien-based without transport, Jomtien gyms plus hotel gym may be more sustainable than daily commutes.

What if CrossFit classes are full?

Use open gym if permitted, or shift to Castra, Jetts, or combat S&C that week. Do not skip recovery — two-a-days without sleep destroy technique on both MT and WODs.

Programming expectations — what WODs feel like here

Affiliate programming at Jungle Gym follows classic CrossFit structure: warm-up, skill or strength piece, then metcon. Murray's reviews emphasise variety — you are unlikely to repeat the same chipper three weeks in a row. Olympic lifting appears when coach prescribes; do not expect a dedicated powerlifting gym's bar path coaching unless you book PT. Gymnastics movements scale to ring rows, box jumps, and banded pull-ups when athletes lack muscle-up capacity.

If your home box programmes strictly on competition templates, communicate goals — holiday maintenance, fat loss, or fight-camp engine work — so scaling makes sense. Pair engine work with Muay Thai safety guide if you are new to two-a-days.

Visiting from another affiliate — drop-in checklist

Message ahead with your home box name, certification level, and any injuries. Bring indoor shoes, jump rope if you use your own, and belt-friendly shorts. Pattaya heat means hydrate before evening WODs; air-con on the floor helps but metabolic stress is still high after morning pads. If you scale poorly, ask Murray for substitution options — ego lifting after MT camp is how shoulders get angry on vacation.
