01 About this neighborhood

What East Pattaya / Darkside is for.

East Pattaya — locally called "Darkside" because it's east of the railway line — is the inland expat residential half of the city. Mabprachan Lake is the geographic center; the surrounding villages (Pong, Nong Prue, Huai Yai) are where many long-term foreign residents live because rent and houses are 30-50% cheaper than the beach side.

Sport-wise, East Pattaya punches above its weight. Several of the best-respected serious Muay Thai gyms are here (Sanit Sport Club, Kombat Group at Huai Yai). Most of the elite golf courses (Phoenix Gold, Treasure Hill, Burapha) are in the East Pattaya / Sriracha border region. And there's less tourist friction — gyms here cater to people who actually live in Pattaya.

03 Transport & access

How to get there.

Sukhumvit-Pattaya highway is the main north-south spine; Tepprasit Road is the major east-west connection from beach to inland. Mabprachan Lake circle road loops around the eastern center. A car or scooter is much more practical than baht-bus.

04 Landmarks & orientation

Where you are.

  • Mabprachan Lake (residential expat center)
  • Sukhumvit Road (Highway 3)
  • Tepprasit Road (connects beach to inland)
  • Huai Yai (rural, near Chak Nok Lake — Kombat Group territory)
  • Pong / Nong Prue villages
  • Si Racha-Pattaya highway (Highway 7) for Bangkok access
05 Starter pick

If you're new here.

If you're doing a 1-3 month training stay and want lower rent + serious training: rent a house near Mabprachan Lake, train at Sanit Sport Club or commute to Kombat Group, and use East Pattaya golf courses for cross-training.

04 Common questions

East Pattaya / Darkside — FAQ.

Is East Pattaya (the Darkside) a good base for training?

East Pattaya, known locally as the Darkside, is the inland residential side around Mabprachan Lake. It is the value end of a long Pattaya stay: lower rents, quieter roads and a community feel, suited to a serious one to three month training stint on a budget.

What sports is East Pattaya best for?

East Pattaya has value-tier gyms and Muay Thai, several golf courses, equestrian facilities, and the Mabprachan Lake loop that runners and cyclists use. It is less about beachfront sport and more about training, golf and outdoor cardio.

How do I get around East Pattaya?

A car or motorbike is effectively essential here - the Darkside is spread out and baht-bus coverage is thin compared with central Pattaya. The Mabprachan area connects to the city via Sukhumvit and the Highway 7 corridor.

Who is East Pattaya best for?

Budget-conscious long-stay trainees and golfers who want lower costs, more space and a quieter pace, and do not mind needing their own transport.
