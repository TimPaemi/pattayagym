# Pattaya Archery Club

## Overview

Pattaya Archery Club is **a non-profit archery organization founded in 2006** that's dedicated to **enabling archers of all nationalities and levels — from total beginner to competitive expert — to enjoy archery while in Pattaya**. The club operates **scheduled shooting sessions Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday, and Sunday from 10:00 to 12:00**, with **a quiet relaxing atmosphere, beginner coaching, equipment provision, and surprisingly comprehensive on-site amenities including bungalow accommodation, swimming pool, tennis court, and a fishing boat**.

The non-profit structure is meaningful: **the club is run for archery, not for profit**, meaning **prices stay accessible, scheduling caters to members and dedicated archers rather than tourist-volume optimization, and the equipment / coaching are oriented around skill development rather than throughput**. The combination of **on-site accommodation + multiple sport amenities** makes this **one of Pattaya's few "stay and train" archery destinations** for serious students traveling from elsewhere in Thailand or internationally.

For Pattaya residents and visitors curious about archery, the **welcoming non-profit atmosphere with patient beginner coaching** makes the Pattaya Archery Club an accessible entry into a sport that's globally underserved in tourist destinations.

## What Archery in Thailand Looks Like

### Olympic / Recurve Archery

The international standard:

- **Recurve bow** — limbs curved away from archer at ends
- **Sight + clicker + stabilizer** for competitive shooting
- **Distances:** typically 18m indoor, 70m outdoor (Olympic)
- **Target:** 10-ring face with concentric scoring
- **Olympic sport** since 1972

### Compound Archery

Modern engineered:

- **Compound bow** — pulleys / cams at limb tips
- **Let-off** — easier to hold at full draw
- **Higher arrow speed**
- **Shorter distances** — 18m indoor, 50m outdoor
- **World Archery Compound Championships** since 1995

### Traditional Archery

- **Longbow / horsebow** — no sights or accessories
- **Instinctive shooting** — skill-based aiming
- **Cultural / historical** focus
- **Asian horsebow tradition** — Korean, Mongolian, Thai variants

### Field & 3D Archery

- **Outdoor courses** through woodland
- **3D animal targets** — Thai-relevant — boar, deer
- **Skill-and-judgment** more than pure target accuracy
- **Hunting practice** for traditional archers

Pattaya Archery Club likely offers **mostly target / Olympic-style archery** with potential introduction to other styles.

## The Non-Profit Structure

### What "Non-Profit" Means Here

Pattaya Archery Club is **a non-profit making organization** — registered as such with Thai authorities. This affects:

- **Pricing** — kept accessible vs. commercial archery ranges
- **Scheduling** — based on member needs, not tourist volume
- **Equipment quality** — invested in rather than rented for profit
- **Coaching** — patient, member-development focused
- **Atmosphere** — community-driven not commercial

For comparison: commercial archery ranges in tourist destinations typically charge **฿1,000-2,500 per shooting session** with **rented equipment and short instruction**. Non-profit clubs deliver **dramatically more value** at **฿300-500 per session** (or annual membership rates) with **better coaching and community**.
