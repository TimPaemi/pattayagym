Manta Kids Pattaya (IG Center)

Overview

Manta Kids Pattaya is the most institutionally credentialed children's swim academy in the city — a Swim Australia / ASCTA-affiliated school inside the IG Center complex on Chaiyaphruek 2 Road in East Pattaya. The academy teaches babies from 4 months old up to children aged 12, with a structured three-program curriculum (Splash → Swirl → Swim) that progresses from in-water familiarization for infants all the way to formal competitive five-stroke technique for school-age kids.

The headline credential is rare in Pattaya: all instructors are certified by the ASCTA — Australia Swimming Coach & Teacher Association — one of the world's most respected swim-coaching governing bodies. The head coach is a former Thai national swimmer, combining international certification with national-team competitive pedigree. For families wanting the most credentialed swim instruction available in Pattaya, this is the answer.

Why Manta Kids Matters

Most "kids learn to swim" options in Pattaya are casual hotel-pool lessons or unaccredited individual coaches. Manta Kids is an institutional academy with international affiliation, a structured curriculum, defined certifications, salt-water indoor heated pools, a published syllabus, and a proper academic-style term structure (10 weeks + Safety Week). The combination puts it in a category by itself:

  • ASCTA certification — international standard
  • Former Thai national swimmer as head coach — competitive pedigree
  • Swim Australia methodology — globally recognized curriculum
  • Indoor heated salt-water pool — sanitary, comfortable for babies, no chlorine sting
  • Term-based curriculum — proper progression, not random drop-in classes
  • Small class sizes — max 8 pairs (Splash/Swirl) or 8 kids (Swim)
  • Parental involvement framework — parent-and-child for younger ages

The Three Programs

Manta Kids structures its curriculum into three age-banded programs, each building on the next:

Splash — Ages 4-18 months

The infant program. Begins with breath-holding (a learned reflex) and water familiarization.

  • Class duration: 30 minutes
  • Format: 1 parent per 1 child
  • Maximum: 8 parent-child pairs per class
  • Frequency: 1 class/week
  • Term: 10 weeks + 1 Safety Week (11 weeks total)

This is the earliest swim instruction available in Pattaya and is foundational for water safety. Babies develop comfort, breath control, and basic floating responses before they can walk.

Swirl — Ages 4 months to 3.5 years

The toddler program. Emphasis shifts to survival swimming and propulsion.

  • Class duration: 30 minutes
  • Format: 1 parent per 1 child
  • Maximum: 8 pairs per class
  • Curriculum focus: floating on back, kicking, breath holding under water, basic forward propulsion, getting to wall safely

By the end of the Swirl progression, kids should be able to enter water, surface, and reach safety on their own — the core "survival swimming" outcome.

Swim — Ages 3.5 to 12 years

The formal/competitive program. Teaches the five strokes of competitive swimming:

  1. Freestyle (Front Crawl)
  2. Backstroke
  3. Breaststroke
  4. Butterfly
  5. Survival Backstroke
  • Class duration: 50 minutes
  • Format: No parent needed (kids work directly with instructor)
  • Maximum: 8 kids per class
  • Curriculum focus: correct stroke mechanics, swimming form similar to professional swimming athletes, water safety, distance swimming, breath timing

This is proper technical swim instruction, not splash-around play. Kids who progress through the Swim program emerge with competition-grade stroke mechanics.

Safety Week — Built Into Every Term

Each 10-week term includes an 11th "Safety Week" focused entirely on water safety education for child and parent:

  • Pool safety rules
  • What to do if you fall in fully clothed
  • How to help a friend who's struggling (without entering the water yourself)
  • Recognising water emergencies
  • Parental supervision practices

This integration of safety education into the curriculum is what distinguishes a credentialed swim school from a generic swim teacher.

Instructor Pedigree

Head Coach: Former Thai National Swimmer

The named distinction. Having competed at the national level, the head coach brings competitive pedigree that informs the technical depth of instruction — how strokes are taught, what fine corrections matter, how kids are progressed through the technique sequence.

All Instructors: ASCTA-Certified

Australia Swimming Coach & Teacher Association certification is one of the most respected swim-coaching credentials internationally. ASCTA-certified coaches have completed structured curriculum on:

  • Pool safety
  • Water-rescue protocols
  • Age-appropriate swim teaching methodology
  • Stroke development sequences
  • Behaviour management for children
  • Safe class management

This certification standard is rare among Pattaya swim instructors. Most independent swim teachers in the area do not hold international credentials.

Pool & Facility

  • Indoor swimming pool (climate-controlled, weather-independent)
  • Salt-water system (gentler on skin and eyes than chlorine)
  • Warm water (heated for baby/toddler comfort)
  • International standard pool design
  • Clean changing rooms with parent areas
  • Reception and waiting area

The salt-water + heated combination is the technically correct setup for infant swim instruction and is rare in Pattaya pools.

Pricing

Pricing is transparent and tier-structured:

One-Time Application Fee

  • ฿2,000 — paid once at first enrollment
  • Includes welcome package per program

Quarterly Tuition

  • Splash & Swirl (30-minute classes): ฿7,500/quarter (10 sessions + 1 Safety Week)
  • Swim (50-minute classes): ฿9,900/quarter (10 sessions + 1 Safety Week)

Math It Out

For the Swim program at ฿9,900 / 11 sessions = approximately ฿900 per 50-minute lesson with an ASCTA-certified instructor in a heated salt-water pool. This is excellent value for credentialed instruction — comparable hotel/private swim lessons in Pattaya often cost ฿800-1,500 per 30-minute session with non-certified instructors.

Pattaya Branch Context

Manta Kids operates multiple branches across Thailand (the original Bangkok operation expanded to Pattaya). The Pattaya branch sits inside the IG Center complex — a kids-and-family-focused educational facility in East Pattaya — making it convenient for families already engaging in other educational programs there.

Reviews & Reputation

Held in strong regard among Pattaya families — both Thai families and international expat parents. Review highlights:

  • Patient, professional instructors — kids comfortable in the water quickly
  • Excellent pool conditions — clean, warm, well-maintained
  • Structured progression — parents can see their child's development
  • Safety-first culture — no shortcuts on the safety curriculum
  • Booking is easy — straightforward enrollment process

The combination of credentialed instruction, structured curriculum, and genuine kids-only specialty has made Manta Kids the default top-tier swim school recommendation in Pattaya for infants and young children.

Reputation Summary

The default top-tier kids swim academy in Pattaya. The combination of ASCTA credentials, Thai-national-team coaching pedigree, structured Swim Australia methodology, indoor heated salt-water pool, and properly-sized classes makes Manta Kids unique in the local market. Particularly recommended for:

  • Expat families wanting international-standard instruction for their kids
  • Thai families wanting competitive-track swim development
  • Parents of infants and toddlers seeking proper water-safety foundation
  • Families committed to multi-year swim development (the term structure supports this)

Best For

  • Infants and toddlers (Splash 4-18 months, Swirl 4mo-3.5yr)
  • Young children wanting proper technical swim instruction (Swim 3.5-12 yr)
  • Families committed to ongoing enrolment through quarterly terms
  • Parents seeking ASCTA-credentialed instruction
  • Kids on a competitive-track swimming pathway

Not Best For

  • Casual one-off swim lesson seekers (better at hotel pools)
  • Adult swim instruction (Manta Kids is exclusively kids 4mo-12yr; check First Serve Sports Club for adults)
  • Drop-in pool access without instruction (use Hard Rock pool day pass or public pools)
  • Children over 12 (program age cap)
