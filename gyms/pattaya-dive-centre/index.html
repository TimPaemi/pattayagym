★ About this venue

Pattaya Dive Centre

Overview

Pattaya Dive Centre is the longest-established PADI dive center on Pattaya's Beach Road — over 20 years at the same location between Soi 10 and Soi 11 — and the only Beach Road PADI 5-Star Dive Center. It is English-owned and operated by PADI Master Instructor David Wright, who brings more than 25 years of dive industry experience. The center holds the rare PADI CDC (Career Development Centre) status — meaning the highest tier of PADI affiliation, the elite for instructor training and career development. It is also a Tec (Technical Diving) center offering technical diving programs, decompression certifications, and trimix training for advanced divers.

For travellers wanting the longest-established, most-credentialed, English-language dive center on Pattaya Beach Road, Pattaya Dive Centre is the default top choice. Day-trip prices of ฿3,200 for certified divers and ฿4,500 for first-time Discover Scuba Diving are competitive with the broader Pattaya dive market while delivering CDC-tier training quality.

Heritage & Credentials

20+ Years on Beach Road

Pattaya Dive Centre has been on the same beachfront location for over two decades — an unusual longevity in the Pattaya tourism market where many dive operators rotate through over years. The location between Soi 10 and Soi 11 puts it directly in the central Pattaya tourist hub, walking distance from hundreds of hotels.

English Owned & Operated

The center is English-owned, with PADI Master Instructor David Wright at the helm. Wright brings more than 25 years in the dive industry, both as a working instructor and as a center operator.

PADI 5-Star CDC Status

The PADI tiering for dive centers progresses:

  1. Dive Resort (basic affiliation)
  2. 5-Star Dive Center (premium tourism focus)
  3. 5-Star IDC (Instructor Development Center) (instructor training capable)
  4. 5-Star CDC (Career Development Centre) — the top tier

Pattaya Dive Centre holds CDC status — placing it among the most credentialed dive centers in Thailand. CDC means the center can deliver the full progression from Open Water Diver up through Master Scuba Diver Trainer (MSDT) and beyond, with internships and career development programs.

Tec Diving

Beyond recreational PADI courses, the center is a Tec Dive centre — offering technical diving programs:

  • PADI Tec 40 / Tec 45 / Tec 50 (decompression diving)
  • Tec Trimix (deeper technical with helium gas mixes)
  • Tec rebreather (closed-circuit rebreather for extended bottom times)

This puts Pattaya Dive Centre in rare company in Thai diving — most dive centers are recreational-only. Tec divers passing through Pattaya will find this center able to support their continuing education.

Pricing

Day Trip Diving

  • Certified divers (2-dive day trip): ฿3,200
  • Discover Scuba Diving (DSD) for first-time divers: ฿4,500

Includes equipment rental, boat fees, and dive guide. Add-ons (camera rental, GoPro, photo packages) priced separately.

PADI Courses

  • Open Water Diver Course: Full beginner certification (recreational diving qualification). Typically 3-4 days, includes pool/confined water + 4 open water dives + theory
  • Advanced Open Water Diver Course: Builds on Open Water with 5 dives across specialties (typically Deep, Navigation + 3 electives like Wreck, Night, Peak Performance Buoyancy)
  • Rescue Diver: Safety-focused certification
  • Divemaster: Professional level training
  • Tec Diving courses: various technical certifications

For exact 2026 pricing on courses: divecentrepattaya.com/prices

Pattaya Dive Sites

Pattaya Dive Centre runs trips to the Near Islands and the Wreck Sites in the Gulf of Thailand offshore from Pattaya.

Near Islands (30-40 min boat ride)

The "Near Islands" are a cluster of small islands ~6 km offshore — Koh Larn, Koh Sak, Koh Khrok:

  • Koh Larn (Coral Island) — primary site, vibrant coral reefs, hawksbill sea turtles common, green turtle occasional sightings, suitable for all levels
  • Koh Sak — clear waters, shallow depths, muck diving specialty (seahorses, nudibranchs, shrimp), beginner-friendly
  • Koh Khrok — coral reef, varied marine life

Wreck Sites — Pattaya = Thailand's Wreck Diving Capital

Pattaya is uniquely the wreck diving capital of Thailand, with several purposefully-sunk Royal Thai Navy vessels creating world-class artificial reefs:

  • HTMS Khram — purposefully sunk
  • HTMS Kut — purposefully sunk
  • HTMS Mataphon — purposefully sunk
  • Hardeep Wreck — historic wreck
  • HTMS Sattakut — popular wreck dive

These wrecks are covered in soft corals, sponges, and sea fans, attracting:

  • Batfish (large schools)
  • Barracuda
  • Lionfish
  • Giant groupers
  • Various reef fish species

For wreck-diving enthusiasts, this is a major reason to dive Pattaya specifically — most other Thai dive destinations don't offer this density of intentional artificial reef wrecks.

Location & Access

  • Beach Road, between Soi 10 and Soi 11 — directly in central Pattaya tourist hub
  • Walking distance from most central Pattaya hotels
  • From Pattaya Beach: literally on the beach road
  • From Soi Buakhao / Pattaya Klang: 5-10 min walk
  • Daily walk-in possible for trip booking and course inquiry
  • Boat departures: typically morning, return mid-afternoon

  • English-owned and operated — David Wright with 25+ years experience
  • Tec Diving capable — rare in Pattaya, supports advanced divers

Cons

  • Tourism-focused location can be busy in peak season
  • Pricing is mid-tier — cheaper dive centers exist in Jomtien (less central)
  • Boat-based diving only — no shore diving from this center
  • Pattaya water visibility varies — generally 5-15m, not Andaman-coast quality
  • Wet season (June-October) can have rougher seas

Best For

  • First-time divers wanting Discover Scuba in central Pattaya
  • Certified recreational divers wanting daily trip access
  • Open Water through Divemaster students
  • Tec divers seeking technical certifications
  • Wreck-dive enthusiasts — Pattaya is Thailand's wreck capital
  • Travellers staying in central Pattaya wanting walking-distance dive shop
  • Long-stay students doing PADI internship through CDC pathway

Not Best For

  • Travellers based in Jomtien wanting walk-to-shop access (Jomtien Dive Center, Mermaid's Dive nearby instead)
  • Pure budget divers (slightly higher than non-Beach-Road operators)
  • Andaman-coast quality visibility seekers (Phuket, Krabi, Similan Islands offer better viz)
