Overview

Jetts Fitness is the leading 24/7 commercial fitness chain in Thailand, operating multiple locations in Pattaya alongside its broader Bangkok / Hua Hin / Phuket / Chiang Mai national network. Originally founded in Australia in 2007, Jetts has expanded globally to Thailand, the United Kingdom, the Netherlands, and New Zealand, becoming one of the most recognizable international fitness brands in Southeast Asia.

In Pattaya, Jetts now operates from a single location at Little Walk Mall on Sukhumvit Road, after the original Royal Garden Plaza branch on Pattaya Beach Road permanently closed on 31 May 2022. The two defining brand features are 24/7 access (the gym literally never closes) and no lock-in contracts (month-to-month memberships), making Jetts a natural fit for the traveller/expat demographic who can't commit to long-term Thai gym contracts.

For travellers, working remote nomads, condo-dwellers, and short-stay residents who want a familiar Western-style commercial gym experience with predictable equipment quality, Jetts is the most accessible, least friction option in the Pattaya fitness scene.

The Jetts Brand

Origins (Australia, 2007)

Jetts was founded in Brisbane, Australia in 2007 by Brendon Levenson and Cristian Mantellini with a clear positioning: 24/7 access, no lock-in contracts, low monthly fees, and a focus on real fitness over lifestyle marketing. The model proved transformative in the Australian fitness market and rapidly expanded:

  • 2010s expansion across Australia and New Zealand
  • 2014 entry to UK and Netherlands
  • 2010s Thailand entry with Bangkok flagships followed by regional rollouts
  • By 2020 — over 250 clubs globally

What Makes Jetts Different

  • 24/7 access — members enter via key card any time, day or night
  • No staffed reception during off-peak — fully automated late nights
  • No lock-in contracts — cancel anytime
  • Standardized equipment across locations — same brand, same machines
  • Group training (GroupX) classes — included in membership
  • Global reciprocal access — members get access to Jetts clubs worldwide while traveling

This last feature is uniquely valuable for international travellers — a Jetts member from Sydney can walk into Jetts Pattaya without paying day-pass fees.

Pattaya Locations

Jetts Little Walk Mall

  • Address: 8/114 Sukhumvit Rd, Bang Lamung District, Chonburi 20150 (Little Walk Mall)
  • Location: Inside the Little Walk shopping/lifestyle complex on Sukhumvit / Pattaya Klang
  • Convenience: Mall-attached parking; baht-bus access from Beach Road and 2nd Road; ~10-minute drive from Walking Street
  • Vibe: Modern commercial space, full GroupX studio, full equipment floor

Jetts Royal Garden Plaza (closed)

The Royal Garden Plaza branch on Pattaya Beach Road permanently closed on 31 May 2022. Members of the original two-club Pattaya footprint were consolidated into Little Walk, which remains the only Pattaya Jetts club. The Beach Road space was a mall-attached, family-context location with the same brand standard as Little Walk before closure.

Equipment & Floor Layout

A standard Jetts location includes:

Cardio Zone

  • Treadmills (typically 8-15 units, brand-name like Life Fitness or Matrix)
  • Stationary bikes — upright + recumbent
  • Ellipticals / cross-trainers
  • Stair climbers / step machines
  • Rowers (Concept2 or equivalent)
  • Some assault bikes / SkiErgs at newer locations

Strength Zone

  • Pin-loaded selectorized machines for full muscle group coverage
  • Plate-loaded machines (Hammer Strength or equivalent)
  • Smith machine
  • Cable crossover stations

Free Weights Zone

  • Dumbbells (typically up to 40-50kg pairs)
  • Olympic barbells + plates
  • Squat racks / power racks
  • Adjustable benches

Functional Zone

  • TRX suspension trainers
  • Kettlebells (range from 8kg to 32kg)
  • Battle ropes
  • Plyo boxes
  • Open floor space for stretching, mobility, bodyweight work

Class Studio

  • GroupX studio for scheduled classes
  • Wood floor or rubberized
  • Mirrors, sound system, bar/weights for classes

GroupX Classes

Jetts includes a range of group fitness classes in membership:

  • BODYPUMP — barbell-based strength endurance (Les Mills program)
  • BODYCOMBAT — kickboxing-inspired cardio (Les Mills)
  • BODYJAM — dance-based cardio (Les Mills)
  • Zumba — Latin-inspired dance fitness
  • Yoga — varied levels
  • TRX — suspension training class
  • Free Move (Hip-Hop) — dance fitness

The Les Mills program family is internationally licensed and consistent across Jetts locations globally.

Pricing

Day Pass

  • ~฿500 day pass for non-members and travellers
  • No commitment — pay and use single visit

Membership Tiers (indicative 2026)

  • Standard month-to-month: ~฿1,550-1,700/month
  • 3-month commitment: ~฿1,400-1,600/month (with 1 free PT session sometimes)
  • 6-month + annual packages with progressive discounts
  • Joining fee: typically ฿1,000-2,000 one-time
  • Personal training: premium add-on ~฿500-1,200/session

This pricing positions Jetts in the mid-tier of Pattaya gyms — meaningfully more than budget-tier hardcore gyms (Castra at ฿120/visit, ฿7,000/year), and meaningfully less than premium private clubs (FITZ Club).

Hours & Access

24/7 Access

  • Always open — 24 hours, 7 days a week, including holidays
  • Key card / app access during off-peak hours
  • Staffed reception during peak (typically 09:00-21:00)
  • Cleaning rotations scheduled around peak times
  • Security cameras + emergency access for off-peak safety

The 24/7 model is especially valued by:

  • Shift workers — chefs, hospitality staff, expat workers with non-9-to-5 schedules
  • Jet-lagged travellers — early-morning workouts post-arrival
  • Insomniac trainers — late-night sessions
  • Hot-season hours-shifters — early AM or late PM to avoid heat
