Ramayana Water Park Pattaya

Overview

Ramayana Water Park is the largest water park in Thailand AND in all of Southeast Asia — a 184,000 square meter aquatic megaplex located 20 kilometers south of Pattaya at Na Jomtien, Sattahip, themed around the Ramayana — the great Hindu epic that's foundational to Thai culture and the source of the Ramakien (the Thai national epic). The park covers an area roughly equivalent to 25 football pitches — making it dramatically larger than the nearby Cartoon Network Amazone (Columbia Pictures Aquaverse) facility and putting it in a different scale category than virtually any other Asian water park.

The park opened officially on May 5, 2016 after construction beginning in 2011 — a 5-year build that allowed the developers to deliver 26 water slides across 34 lanes, a 600-meter-long lazy river that wraps around a natural hill with integrated waves, bubbles, and waterfalls, and a double wave pool with a 150-meter-wide beach that lets visitors swim with tubes in one section or wave-bathe without tubes in the other. The Ramayana cultural theming is integrated throughout the park architecture, landscaping, character costumes, and naming — distinguishing the experience from generic-themed water parks globally.

The international recognition is meaningful: in 2019, just 3 years after opening, Ramayana ranked #1 in Asia and #10 in the World among water parks per TripAdvisor Travelers' Choice Awards — confirming that the park's scale, ambition, and execution made it a genuinely world-class destination.

  • Guests can spread across the park during peak times
  • Family separation possible — kids in shallow areas, adults at slides, grandparents at lazy river
  • Multiple food/dining options distributed across the park
  • Less crowded feel despite high attendance days

The Ramayana Theme

Ramayana / Ramakien Background

The Ramayana is one of the great epics of South and Southeast Asian literature:

  • Originated in ancient India — attributed to the sage Valmiki
  • Tells the story of Prince Rama, his wife Sita, his loyal brother Lakshmana, and the demon king Ravana
  • Adapted into Thai national epic as the Ramakien — Thailand's most important literary work
  • Kings of Siam took the name "Rama" — Thailand's monarchs are known as Rama I through Rama X (current king Rama X / Vajiralongkorn)
  • Foundational to Thai art, dance, literature, religion
  • Murals at Wat Phra Kaew (Temple of the Emerald Buddha) depict the entire Ramakien

How Ramayana Themes the Park

  • Architectural elements — Hindu/Thai-temple inspired structures
  • Landscaping — natural rock formations, jungle aesthetic
  • Character integration — hanuman (monkey general), demons, gods
  • Naming conventions — slides named after Ramayana characters and concepts
  • Live shows and entertainment — Thai dance and Ramayana-derived performances
  • Cultural significance — connects family entertainment with Thai cultural heritage

This deeper cultural theming is meaningfully different from Cartoon Network Amazone's commercial-IP approach.

The 26 Slides / 34 Lanes

Slide Breakdown

The 26 slides span 34 lanes (some slides are multi-lane):

Family-Friendly Slides

  • Tube rides for groups of 2-4
  • Body slides for teens and adults
  • Mat slides for racing-style competition
  • Splash zones for younger kids

Thrill Slides

  • High-speed drop slides for adrenaline seekers
  • Loop slides for the bold
  • Multi-lane racing slides — ride alongside friends and family

Family Tube Slides

  • Multi-rider tubes for shared experience
  • Variable-speed sections
  • Photo opportunities during descent

Slide Hardware

Like Cartoon Network Amazone, Ramayana likely uses premium international slide manufacturers (Polin Waterparks, ProSlide, WhiteWater West) for engineering-grade safety and ride quality.

The 600-Meter Lazy River

Why It's Distinctive

Most lazy rivers are simple circular channels around the perimeter of a water park. Ramayana's lazy river:

  • Runs 600 meters — substantially longer than typical
  • Wraps around a natural hill — provides elevation variation and scenic views
  • Integrated waves — periodic waves through the river course
  • Bubbles — bubbling sections for varied sensation
  • Waterfalls — ride through cascading water features
  • Natural-rock setting — feels like floating through a natural canyon rather than a constructed channel

This is one of the most ambitious lazy river installations globally — typically 200-400m is considered impressive, 600m is exceptional.

The Double Wave Pool with 150m Beach

Wave Pool Design

The wave pool is double-functioned:

  • Side 1: Tube section for floating wave-riding
  • Side 2: Open swim section without tubes for direct wave-bathing
  • Beach: 150 meters wide (490 feet) — among the largest artificial beaches in any water park

Wave Generation

  • Periodic wave generation — typically every 10-15 minutes for waves, between cycles for calm swimming
  • Wave heights: Range from gentle for children to substantial for adults
  • Family-friendly programming during off-peak intensity windows

This double design accommodates both casual swimmers and active wave-riders simultaneously.

Pricing & Logistics

Day Pass Pricing

Standard Pattaya water park pricing tiers:

  • Adult day pass: ~฿1,200-1,800 typical
  • Child day pass: ~฿800-1,200
  • Discount tickets through Klook, KKday, GetYourGuide, Trazy
  • Annual pass options
  • Combination tickets with hotel stays / transport packages

Hours

  • Daily 10:00-18:00 (verify at park website)
  • Last entry typically 17:00
  • 8-hour park day allows multiple slide rotations

Getting There

  • 20 km south of central Pattaya
  • 30-40 minute drive
  • Private taxi: ~฿500-800 each way
  • Park shuttle services from many Pattaya hotels
  • Combined ticket+transport packages common

Facilities

  • Locker rentals
  • Towel rentals
  • Multiple food courts with international and Thai cuisine
  • Souvenir shops
  • First aid + lifeguards throughout
  • Wheelchair accessibility at most attractions
  • Cabana rentals premium tier
  • Family changing rooms

TripAdvisor World Top 10 Ranking

The 2019 TripAdvisor Travelers' Choice Awards ranking is meaningful:

  • #1 Water Park in Asia (2019)
  • #10 Water Park in the World (2019)
  • 3 years after opening — extraordinary trajectory for new park
  • Voted by visitors globally — not industry insiders

This puts Ramayana in company with global water-park flagships: Aquaventure (UAE), Tropical Islands (Germany), Cowabunga Bay (USA), Beach Park (Brazil), Wet 'n' Wild (multiple countries).
