CrossFit Pattaya @ The Jungle Gym

Overview

Jungle Gym Pattaya is the first and only legitimate CrossFit affiliate in Pattaya — a multi-discipline functional fitness facility owned and operated by Coach Murray, located on 165/4 Moo 6, Nong Prue, in central-east Pattaya. The brand pairing is deliberate: as Coach Murray himself describes it, "CrossFit Pattaya is our premier program, but the Jungle Gym is our identity". The facility runs serious CrossFit programming on the official PADI-equivalent CrossFit affiliate model — branded WODs, Olympic lifting, certified coach instruction — alongside a wildly varied multi-discipline programming layer that includes MMA, dodgeball, slacklining, target archery, archery tag, yoga, nutrition coaching, and even DNA testing for fitness profiles.

The gym carries partnership status with Under Armour and Decathlon, putting brand-name equipment and gear support behind the operation. For travellers and Pattaya residents wanting a real CrossFit affiliate experience plus genuinely fun multi-activity programming, Jungle Gym is the only answer in the city — and the variety alone makes it a standout.

Position in the Pattaya Market

Pattaya doesn't have multiple CrossFit boxes. Bangkok has Rock Domain, RQ Club, CrossFit Arena, and several other affiliates; Phuket has CrossFit Phuket and CrossFit Siam Phuket; Koh Tao has CrossFit Koh Tao. Pattaya has Jungle Gym — and that's it for legitimate CrossFit affiliation in the city.

This makes Jungle Gym the default destination for any visiting CrossFitter passing through Pattaya and the home box for the local CrossFit community of expats, nomads, and Thai residents.

Coach Murray — Owner & Head Coach

The gym's identity is anchored in Coach Murray, the owner and head coach. Reviews consistently highlight Murray as:

  • Very attentive during sessions — actively watches form, corrects technique
  • Knowledgeable and professional — proper CrossFit certification and coaching pedagogy
  • Makes every class fun and different — programming variety, not repetitive WODs
  • Tailors classes to help each individual — scales for ability levels

The named-coach-who-cares positioning is meaningful in CrossFit, where coach quality directly determines class quality. Murray's continuous presence and hands-on involvement is the gym's main selling point alongside the multi-discipline offerings.

Brand Partnerships

The gym holds active partnerships with two of the largest sports brands globally:

  • Under Armour — combat sports apparel and training wear
  • Decathlon — multi-sport equipment and accessibility-focused brand

These partnerships translate into:

  • Brand-name equipment throughout the gym
  • Pro-shop access to Under Armour and Decathlon products
  • Sponsored events / clinics occasionally hosted at the facility

Disciplines & Programs (The Wild Variety)

What sets Jungle Gym apart from any other Pattaya gym is the breadth of programming. This is genuinely an "active adult playground" rather than a pure CrossFit box:

CrossFit (The Premier Program)

  • Certified affiliate WOD programming following the CrossFit methodology
  • Olympic lifting, conditioning, gymnastics, varied movements at high intensity
  • Group classes with structured warm-up, strength/skill component, WOD, cool-down
  • Open Gym option for self-directed members

Personal Training & Coaching

  • 1-on-1 PT for strength, conditioning, fat loss, sport-specific
  • Small Group Training (2-4 people) — semi-private with PT-quality attention
  • Nutrition Programming — diet plans tailored to goals
  • DNA Testing — genetic profile-based training/nutrition recommendations (uncommon in Pattaya)

Combat & Self-defence

  • MMA — striking + grappling cross-training
  • Self-defence — practical defense classes

Unusual Activities (The Jungle Gym Side)

  • Dodgeball — group sessions, social play
  • Slacklining — balance training, both functional and fun
  • Target Archery — traditional archery
  • Archery Tag — group game / event activity (popular with corporate / family bookings)

Recovery / Wellness

  • Yoga — supplementary classes
  • Stretching / mobility — built into programming

Open Gym

  • Self-directed access between scheduled classes
  • Use the equipment without coach involvement
  • Standard inclusion in monthly memberships

Schedule

CrossFit Class Times

  • Mon-Fri: 09:00-10:00 (morning) and 19:00-20:00 (evening)
  • Saturday: 09:00-10:00
  • Sundays: Open Gym (typically) or rest

Other Disciplines

Run on rotating weekly schedules — yoga, MMA, archery, dodgeball, etc. each have their own slots. Specific weekly schedule on the Jungle Gym schedule page.

Pricing

Jungle Gym uses standard tiered membership models common in Western CrossFit boxes. Indicative ranges based on Pattaya CrossFit market:

  • Drop-in CrossFit class: ~฿400-600
  • Monthly unlimited: ~฿3,500-4,500
  • 3-month / 6-month / annual packages discount further
  • Personal training: premium rate
  • Multi-discipline class packages: custom rates
  • DNA testing + program: specialised one-time fee
  • Family / event bookings (archery tag, dodgeball): group event pricing

For exact 2026 rates: junglegympattaya.com or contact via Facebook / Instagram DM.

Facilities

The "Jungle Gym" branding hints at the eclectic facility:

  • Full CrossFit-equipped training floor (Olympic platforms, racks, barbells, plates, kettlebells, ropes, rings, GHD, plyo boxes)
  • Cardio machines (rowers, bikes, treadmills)
  • Dedicated MMA / mat space
  • Archery / target range — uncommon amenity
  • Slackline setup (outdoor functional balance)
  • Dodgeball court / open space
  • Showers, lockers, changing rooms
  • Pro shop (Under Armour / Decathlon partnerships)

Pros

  • The only legitimate CrossFit affiliate in Pattaya — uncontested market position
  • Coach Murray — knowledgeable, professional, attentive, beloved by members
  • Genuinely unique multi-discipline mix (archery, dodgeball, slacklining, MMA alongside CrossFit)
  • Under Armour + Decathlon brand partnerships
  • English-language coaching
  • Flexible schedule — morning and evening CrossFit slots
  • Open Gym option for self-directed work
  • Family/event possibilities (archery tag, dodgeball)
  • Holistic add-ons (nutrition coaching, DNA testing)
  • Small Group Training option for semi-private affordability
  • CrossFit class community — expat fitness scene's social anchor

Cons

  • Schedule density lighter than dedicated big-city CrossFit boxes — only morning and evening classes per day
  • Smaller community size than Bangkok CrossFit affiliates
  • Multi-discipline focus might dilute pure-CrossFit identity for purists
  • Less polished aesthetic than premium Bangkok boxes
  • Pricing not always published transparently — quote-based for some services

Best For

  • Visiting CrossFitters needing a Pattaya affiliate
  • Multi-discipline functional fitness fans
  • Travellers wanting flexible drop-in pricing
  • Families/groups wanting unusual activities (archery tag, dodgeball)
  • Coach Murray fans — the head coach is the gym
  • Expat fitness community in Pattaya
  • Open Gym users wanting self-directed CrossFit-grade equipment access

Not Best For

  • Hardcore CrossFitters needing multiple class options per day
  • Pure-CrossFit-box purists without other activities
  • Pure budget seekers
  • Travellers wanting on-site accommodation
