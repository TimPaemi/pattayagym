Running & cycling clubs.

By Tim Paemi · 9 min read · Updated 2026-05-31 · How we rank →

Pattaya social sport means Monday hash runs, weekend cycling meetups, free sunrise beach aerobics, and expat cricket and rugby teams — twenty-one club venues in our directory. This guide maps how to plug in without a gym membership.

Pattaya · 158 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

Pattaya community sport is not all gyms and rings — it runs on hash clubs, weekend cycling groups, free beach aerobics, public running routes, and expat teams for cricket, rugby, and lawn bowls. Twenty-one venues sit in our clubs category. This guide maps how to join social sport without a monthly gym contract.

Hub: Running / cycling clubs · Digital nomad fitness · Seniors low-impact · compare · plan my trip.

Running — routes, hash, and races

Pattaya public running routes covers free paths — Pattaya Beach 5.8 km, Jomtien, Mabprachan 4 km loop, Pratumnak Park, Nongprue. Best at dawn before heat.

  • Pattaya Hash House Harriers — mixed running club since 1984, Mondays 16:00/17:00, rotating start points. World's largest weekly hash tradition locally.
  • Pattaya Monkey Hash — longer monthly runs, odd-numbered months, 3rd Saturday.
  • Amazing Thailand Pattaya Marathon — annual July road race since 1992, 10K/half/full, World Athletics standard, Boston qualifying.
  • Pattaya Triathlon — Thailand Tri-League series, swim/bike/run with iconic Pattaya Hill finish.

Cardio landmarks: Big Buddha Hill stair climb (Pratamnak) · Khao Chi Chan walking site south at Sattahip.

Cycling — road, MTB, and weekend clubs

Pattaya cycling clubs umbrella covers Cuore Masters Team, Monkey Xtreme, Jomtien Cycling, and Pattaya MTB — weekend morning meet points across the city, mostly free to low annual fee (฿0–2,000).

Pair long rides with Darkside recovery: East Pattaya gyms · Quiet countryside alternative: Pattaya Bike and Boat Tours (Naklua guided routes — listed under adventure).

Free daily fitness — beach aerobics

Pattaya Beach public aerobics runs sunrise 06:00–08:00 and sunset 17:00–19:30 on Beach Road and Jomtien boardwalks — Zumba, dance fitness, yoga, tai chi. No fee, no signup. Pattaya's most underrated daily wellness routine for long-stay trainers between pad rounds.

Related: yoga retreat guide · cheapest gyms when you want weights after aerobics.

Team and social clubs — cricket, rugby, bowls, petanque

  • Pattaya Cricket Club — only BCL club in Pattaya, Nov–June, Horseshoe Point ground, three teams.
  • Pattaya Panthers RFC — contact and touch rugby, annual Pattaya Tens in May.
  • Pattaya lawn bowls — three facilities, 14+ covered rinks, Banchang ฿250/day roll-up Mon/Wed/Fri.
  • Pattaya Sai 3 Petanque — central Soi 3, French boules community, drop-in friendly.
  • Pattaya Sports Club — multi-sport non-profit, ~4,700 members, tennis and social events on 3rd Road.

Beach pickup sport

Jomtien beach volleyball — afternoon/evening pickup on multiple courts, mixed Thai-international, free play. No league signup required.

Quick pick by traveller type

  • New expat wanting friends? → Monday hash or weekend cycling club ride.
  • Training holiday runner? → Beach route dawn laps + evening MT pads.
  • Digital nomad? → Sunrise beach aerobics + afternoon cowork block — nomad fitness guide.
  • 50+ low impact? → Lawn bowls roll-up or petanque — seniors guide.
  • Event trip? → Book Pattaya Marathon (July) or Tri-League race dates early.

Sample weekly rhythm (run + Muay Thai)

Mon: hash run · Tue/Thu: morning pads · Wed: Mabprachan loop run · Sat: cycling club or beach volleyball · Sun: Big Buddha stairs + rest swim — pools guide.
