Common questions about yoga / pilates in Pattaya

Where can I do yoga in Pattaya?

Yoga Pattaya Studio, Yoga Haus, Ashtanga Yoga Pattaya, Nok Yoga, One-D, and Balance Yoga Studio are the established yoga venues, mostly in Jomtien and Central Pattaya.

How much do yoga classes cost in Pattaya?

Drop-in classes ฿300–฿700. Class packs ฿2,500–฿5,000 for 10 sessions. Unlimited monthly memberships ฿3,500–฿6,000. Privates run ฿1,500–฿2,500/session.

Are Pattaya yoga classes in English?

Most studios run classes in English. Yoga Pattaya, Yoga Haus, Ashtanga Yoga Pattaya, and One-D all teach predominantly in English with international clientele.

What styles of yoga are taught in Pattaya?

All major styles — Hatha, Vinyasa Flow, Ashtanga (Mysore + led), Yin, Hot, and Restorative. Some studios also teach Pilates, Thai yoga stretching, and meditation.
