Swimming pools.

By Tim Paemi · 6 min read · Updated 2026-06-01 · How we rank →

Pattaya swimming means municipal pools from ฿20 visits, five-star day-passes, beach mornings, and Ramayana-scale water parks. This guide maps every swim venue in our directory with access rules you should confirm before you travel.

Pattaya swimming is not one facility — it is four overlapping systems: municipal public pools (฿20–100 visits), hotel and resort pools (day-pass or guest), beach and open water, and water parks for family full-days. This guide maps every swim-related venue in our 157-entry directory with honest access rules — always confirm day-pass policy by phone before you travel.

Public pools — the cheapest lap swim in Pattaya

Pattaya operates two municipal public swimming pools — exceptional value for residents and long-stayers.

Naklua Public Swimming Pool

Naklua Public Swimming Pool serves north Pattaya and Wongamat corridor residents. Standard Thai municipal model: chlorinated lanes, kids' pool, locker rooms, lifeguards. Pricing in venue research: typically ฿20–100 per visit depending on resident status and age — confirm at gate.

Jomtien Public Swimming Pool

Jomtien Public Swimming Pool sits between South Pattaya and Jomtien Beach — the south-side equivalent for Baan Suan Lalana, View Talay, and Jomtien condo belts. Same municipal pricing band; best budget formal swim access south of central.

Who should prioritise public pools: retirees, long-stay nomads, Thai residents, fight campers on rest days who refuse hotel day-pass markups.

Hotel and resort pools — day-pass culture

Five-star strips sell pool + gym access to non-guests when occupancy allows — policies change every season. Always call ahead with date, party size, and whether you need gym + pool or pool only.

Water parks — family full days (not lap lanes)

  • Ramayana Water Park — Na Jomtien/Sattahip; venue file cites largest in Thailand/SEA, 184,000 sqm, 26 slides, 600m lazy river, double wave pool. Hours on file: daily 10:00–18:00. Full-day trip — not a 45-minute lap session.
  • Columbia Pictures Aquaverse (Cartoon Network Amazone) — themed slides, Na Jomtien corridor.
  • Centara Grand Mirage — resort water park + family pools; childcare & pools guide.
  • Underwater World — aquarium and touch pools; education day, not lap training.

Beach swimming and open water

Jomtien and Wong Amat suit calm morning laps before jet-ski traffic peaks. Rules: swim parallel to shore, avoid marked craft lanes, respect red flags during monsoon (roughly May–October). Open-water prep links to diving & watersports, dive operators, and KBA Kiteboarding.

FAQ

What is the cheapest swimming in Pattaya?

Naklua and Jomtien public pools — municipal entry typically under ฿100. Confirm resident vs tourist rate at the gate.

Can non-guests use hotel pools?

Often yes via day-pass — Hilton, Hard Rock, Andaz, Marriott, and others list day-pass potential in our files. Policies change; call the spa or fitness desk the same morning.

Is Ramayana Water Park good for lap swimming?

No — it is a slide and wave-pool theme park. Use public pools or hotel lap lanes for training metres. Ramayana is a family leisure day.

Where should Jomtien residents swim laps?

Jomtien public pool first for budget; Andaz or Marriott for luxury laps and gym combo; beach mornings before 08:00 for open water. Guide: best gym Jomtien.

Are public pools safe during monsoon?

Outdoor public pools may close during storms; beaches post red flags. Check weather and pool Facebook pages before you go. Indoor hotel pools are the rainy-season backup.

How do hotel pool day-passes compare to gym day-passes?

Hotel passes often cost more but include towel, locker, and spa access. Gym-only tourists: gym day pass guide. Luxury comparison: luxury sports clubs.

Seasonal notes — dry vs wet months

November–February brings calmer seas and comfortable outdoor hotel laps. March–May heat peaks — swim before 09:00 or after 17:00 at public and beach venues. May–October monsoon raises jellyfish risk on some beaches and closes flags more often — shift to public indoor-capable hotels or mall-linked fitness with pool. Water parks run year-round but crowds spike Thai holidays — book Ramayana tickets online when possible. Ask your condo management about pool access before signing a long-stay lease.

Pool hygiene and gear — practical packing

Public pools expect swim cap rules on some lanes — buy locally for ฿50–150. Hotel pools usually provide towels with day-pass; public pools may not. Goggles with UV tint help outdoor hotel lanes at midday. Shower before entering municipal pools (Thai pool culture). Do not assume diving boards at public facilities — lap lanes only. Recovery: Pattaya Medical for ear or skin issues after long open-water weeks.
