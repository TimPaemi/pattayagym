Equestrian & polo.

By Tim Paemi · 12 min read · Updated 2026-06-01 · How we rank →

Pattaya equestrian means two world-scale venues — Horseshoe Point's International Riding School since 1999 and Thai Polo Club's tournament fields north of Mabprachan. This guide maps both from venue research: who each suits, what to book ahead, and how a horse day fits a wider sport holiday.

Pattaya · 158 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

Pattaya has two serious equestrian venues — Horseshoe Point Resort at Mabprachan and Thai Polo & Equestrian Club north of the city. Both run riding schools, polo, and multi-sport days on large estates. This guide covers who each suits, booking expectations, and how to pair a horse day with Muay Thai, golf, or family sport nearby — sourced from venue markdown, not brochure copy.

Equestrian here is not a beach pony photo-op — both venues operate at national or Asia-scale scale with formal riding schools, competition infrastructure, and advance booking. Hub: Equestrian category · Luxury sports clubs · East Pattaya guide · compare · plan my trip.

Horseshoe vs Thai Polo — market position

Most tourist regions offer small trail-ride stables. Pattaya's two listed venues are different: Horseshoe Point combines TEF-certified riding school pedigree with a 1,500-acre adventure estate; Thai Polo Club targets polo players and international equestrian competition. Neither is walkable from Beach Road — plan transport.

1. Horseshoe Point Resort — Mabprachan mega-facility

Horseshoe Point Resort & Country Club (100 Moo 9, Pong, Bang Lamung — near Mabprachan Reservoir) is a 1,500-acre estate. The International Riding School was established in 1999 and holds:

  • Thailand Equestrian Federation (TEF) certification
  • Affiliation with Centro Equestre da Leziria Grande (Portugal) — classical riding lineage via Maestro Luis Valença Rodrigues' school

Equestrian infrastructure in venue files: five indoor/outdoor arenas, 100+ horses across Lusitano, Appaloosa, Lipizzaner, Arab, Warmblood, Falabella, and Thai pony breeds. Programmes span dressage, show jumping, classical riding, polo, trail rides, and kids' pony sessions.

Same campus runs paintball, zipline, mountain biking, archery, water balls, and a multipurpose sports field — useful when your group splits interests. On-site accommodation supports stay-and-ride weekends. Phone +66 38 253 599.

Distances (venue file): ~14.5 km to Pattaya Beach (~25–30 min by car), ~14.7 km to Jomtien; ~10 minutes to Siam Country Club golf; adjacent to Mabprachan recreation popular with cyclists — running & clubs guide.

Best for: families wanting a full-day estate visit; beginner trail rides; classical/dressage students; FAST PRO Football Academy uses Horseshoe grounds — kids sport guide. Pair Darkside training at Kombat Group or Jungle Gym CrossFit same week.

2. Thai Polo & Equestrian Club — Asia-scale polo

Thai Polo & Equestrian Club (Banglamung / Mabprachan Lake area, ~25 min north of central Pattaya in venue research) claims largest multi-functional polo and equestrian facility in Asia — 250 hectares, three polo fields plus two practice pitches, 250+ stables, endurance and Derby programmes, Argentinian vet on staff.

Best for: serious polo players and international equestrian visitors; premium tier (฿฿฿฿). Book ahead — walk-ins rare. Website: thai-polo-club.com.

Cricket crossover: Pattaya Cricket Club plays at Horseshoe Point ground Nov–June — not Thai Polo, but same east belt for sport planning.

Quick pick — first visit

  • First trail ride / family day? → Horseshoe Point — riding school + adventure activities on one ticket.
  • Polo or competitive equestrian? → Thai Polo & Equestrian Club — tournament-scale fields.
  • Stay overnight? → Horseshoe accommodation on-site; Thai Polo by appointment.
  • Budget day? → Neither is cheap — compare resort tier: luxury sports clubs.
  • Riding + golf weekend? → Horseshoe near Siam Country Club — best golf courses.

Booking, safety, and what to verify

Both venues require advance booking for riding slots, helmet fitting, and weight/age policies for children. Confirm in writing:

  • Session length and whether guide accompanies trail rides
  • Helmet and boot hire included or extra
  • Weight limits and minimum age for mounts
  • Insurance and waiver language (especially for kids)
  • Photography rules around horses and polo fields

Transport: estates sit east/north of central Pattaya — use Pattaya Vehicle Rentals or hotel taxi desk; not walkable from Walking Street. Allow buffer time — Banglamung traffic spikes weekends.

Injury backup: Pattaya Medical. Gentle alternatives same week: seniors low-impact sport · swimming pools.

FAQ

Which venue is better for kids' first horse ride?

Horseshoe Point — pony programmes, adventure activities, and family accommodation on one estate. Confirm age and height rules when booking.

Can I visit Thai Polo without playing polo?

Contact the club directly — visitor policies vary. Serious polo and equestrian training dominate; Horseshoe is the easier general visitor entry.

How far is Horseshoe from Pattaya Beach?

About 14.5 km (~25–30 minutes by car) per venue file. Plan round-trip taxi or rental — not a baht-bus trip.

Is horse riding safe in Pattaya heat?

Book morning sessions; hydrate; wear long trousers and closed shoes. Estates run structured programmes — avoid unlicensed beach operators not in our directory.

Can I combine horses with Muay Thai same trip?

Yes — east-side camps (Kombat Group, Fairtex) sit drivable from Mabprachan. Rest days matter — do not stack hard riding and hard sparring without recovery.

What should I book first?

Riding slot and transport. Secondary: accommodation at Horseshoe if stay-and-ride; polo chukker dates at Thai Polo for competition visitors.
