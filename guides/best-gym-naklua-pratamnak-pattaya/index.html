Naklua & Pratamnak.

By Tim Paemi · 7 min read · Updated 2026-06-02 · How we rank →

North Pattaya camps and hilltop iron — Fairtex, Sityodtong, Muscle Factory, padel, Wong Amat, and luxury resort gyms mapped for where you sleep.

Pattaya · 157 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

Naklua and Pratamnak are the quieter north and hilltop south of Pattaya — not the Beach Road chaos, not the Jomtien long-stay strip. Naklua holds Fairtex, Sityodtong, Wong Amat beach, and the Sanctuary of Truth. Pratamnak is Muscle Factory territory, Royal Cliff / Fitz Club luxury, padel courts, and the Big Buddha stair climb. This guide only lists venues you can reach from these neighborhoods without pretending a central-Pattaya gym is "Naklua" because the Google pin says so.

Start with Naklua area hub, Pratamnak area hub, and category slices: Muay Thai in Naklua, Pratamnak fitness, Pratamnak racquet sports. Shortlist on compare and map hotels with plan my trip.

Naklua vs Pratamnak — which hill are you on?

Naklua / Wong Amat runs north from Terminal 21 toward the Sanctuary of Truth — family beaches, resort clusters, legendary MT camps on North Pattaya Road. Pratamnak Hill sits between central Pattaya and Jomtien — steep sois, sea views, bodybuilding gyms, yacht club, pickleball and padel. They are 10–15 minutes apart by car but not walkable as one neighborhood. Pick your gym first, then your condo — not the reverse.

Rush-hour Sukhumvit and Pratamnak Hill Road can double taxi times. Monthly fighters should live within 15 minutes of morning session, especially for Fairtex's 07:30 slot.

Why trainers choose Naklua / Pratamnak over central Pattaya

  • Serious Muay Thai density — Fairtex resort camp and Sityodtong lineage within minutes.
  • Quieter sleep — Wong Amat and hilltop condos beat Walking Street noise for recovery.
  • Luxury + budget mix — Muscle Factory hardcore iron beside Fitz Club and InterContinental tier.
  • Family sport — Centara Mirage water park, public Naklua pool, AF Academy football.
  • Medical anchor — Bangkok Hospital Pattaya sports rehab on Sukhumvit Naklua side.

Best Muay Thai in Naklua

Fairtex Training Center — resort camp standard

Fairtex Training Center Pattaya on North Pattaya Road is the city's most famous stay-and-train address: nine rings, on-site hotel, 25m pool, English-friendly coaches. Mon–Sat two-a-days. ฿฿฿. Not a walk-in tourist pad session — plan a week minimum to justify orientation.

Pick this if: you want one campus for training, sleep, and pool recovery. Skip if: you need budget drop-ins only — see cheapest gyms.

Sityodtong Pattaya — pilgrimage camp

Sityodtong Pattaya in Nong Prue (Naklua side) is the branch of the 1960 Kru Yodtong dynasty — morning and afternoon sessions, authentic Thai gym culture, ฿฿ pricing. Less resort polish than Fairtex, more lineage credibility.

Pick this if: you care about history and traditional camp rhythm. Pair with beginners guide for first-week expectations.

Best gyms on Pratamnak Hill

Muscle Factory Pattaya — largest hardcore gym

Muscle Factory Pattaya on Pratumnak Soi 5 is a three-floor bodybuilding institution — Bangkok sister gym, industrial fans, serious free weights. Mon–Fri until midnight. ฿฿. The default answer when someone says "where do bodybuilders train in Pattaya?"

Pick this if: hypertrophy and powerlifting matter more than pad work. Skip if: you need Muay Thai on the same membership — add a camp separately.

SUN Fitness — Naklua + Pratamnak branches

SUN Fitness Pattaya runs a chain membership across Buakhao, Naklua, and Pratamnak branches — budget ฿, late hours, commercial equipment. Good for lifters who also train MT elsewhere.

Cross Pattaya Pratamnak — hotel gym + kids

Cross Pattaya Pratamnak bundles Cloud Club gym, PLAYROOM kids zone, and pool — day passes for non-guests near Royal Cliff. ฿฿฿. Useful for families where parents lift and kids play.

Racquet sports — padel, pickleball, tennis

Pratamnak is Pattaya's racquet hotspot:

  • Play Padel Pattaya — Kasetsin Soi 3; Thailand's first indoor padel courts, hourly booking, clinics.
  • Pickleball Pattaya — Pratumnak Soi 6; dedicated courts, English coach, social leagues.
  • Fitz Club — Royal Cliff; seven tennis courts, squash, swimming, luxury tier ฿฿฿฿.
  • Pattaya Tennis & Badminton Inter Club — multi-racquet including pickleball.

Lakeside padel at Pattaya Padel Club on Mabprachan is east Pattaya — 20+ min from Pratamnak, still reachable for weekend sessions.

Yoga, swim, and active rest

  • One-D Yoga Studio — Naklua 16/2; Hatha, Vinyasa, Yin; retreats.
  • Naklua Public Swimming Pool — city laps on a budget.
  • Wong Amat Beach — Pattaya's calmest family swim beach; free.
  • Big Buddha Hill — 100+ steps; genuine cardio with panoramic views.
  • Royal Varuna Yacht Club — RYA sailing; prestige Pratamnak waterfront.

Resort fitness (Naklua / Pratamnak)

  • Centara Grand Mirage — Wong Amat; water park + spa + climbing wall.
  • Cape Dara Resort — Naklua cliff; infinity pool + gym/spa.
  • InterContinental Pattaya — Pratamnak; 25m lap pool, tennis, 24h fitness.

Day-pass policies change — call ahead. For non-resort monthly deals see gym day pass and Naklua fitness listings.

Who should live in Naklua / Pratamnak

Live Naklua if you train Fairtex or Sityodtong, have kids near international schools north, or want Wong Amat mornings. Live Pratamnak if you lift at Muscle Factory, play padel/pickleball, or want hill views without Jomtien commute. Commute from central only for occasional sessions — daily taxis erode training volume over a month.

Compare with Jomtien gyms if you are still choosing a beach zone. Training-holiday logistics: Muay Thai training holiday and 1 week vs 1 month.

Sample weekly rhythm (Naklua Fairtex + Pratamnak lift)

  • Mon/Wed/Fri AM: Fairtex morning block
  • Tue/Thu PM: Muscle Factory or SUN Fitness strength
  • Sat: Wong Amat swim or Big Buddha steps
  • Sun: rest — Pattaya Restaurant Guide recovery meal

FAQ

What is the best gym in Naklua for Muay Thai?

Fairtex for resort stay-and-train; Sityodtong for lineage and authentic camp culture. Full rankings: best Muay Thai Pattaya.

Is Muscle Factory on Pratamnak worth the trip from Naklua?

Yes — 10–15 minutes by Grab unless rush hour. Many Fairtex students lift at Muscle Factory on off days. No Muay Thai there — keep camp membership separate.

Where can I play padel or pickleball near Pratamnak?

Play Padel and Pickleball Pattaya are both on Pratumnak sois — book courts online where listed.

Are Naklua beaches better for families than Pattaya Beach?

Wong Amat is generally calmer. Always check current swim flags. Family context: family-friendly Pattaya.

How far is Naklua from Jomtien gyms?

25–40 minutes by car. If you split time between zones, read best gym Jomtien before signing two monthly contracts.

Do I need Thai language in Naklua camps?

Fairtex and Sityodtong both train foreigners daily; basic Thai still helps. Phrase list: Thai gym terms.

Pratamnak Hill lifestyle for long-stay athletes

Hilltop condos trade stair climbs for sea views and quieter nights than Beach Road. Muscle Factory and Fitz Club anchor serious iron; Fairtex and Sityodtong sit Naklua-side for camp culture. Morning runs on Pratamnak Hill Road + outdoor calisthenics park are free conditioning add-ons.

Wong Amat vs Naklua market — sleep quality

Wong Amat beach strip suits families and retirees who want calmer water. Naklua market soi adds local food economics — better for 6-month budgets than tourist menus on Beach Road. Both are north of central chaos; taxi to Walking Street is ฿100–200 when you want nightlife, not when you need sleep.

Sample north-side training month

Week 1–2: Fairtex or Sityodtong twice daily · padel/pickleball recovery at Play Padel. Week 3: deload — yoga at Balance, pool at Centara if family visit. Week 4: test sparring progression · Bangkok stadium weekend via day-trip guide. Visa holders stack reporting trips with immigration rhythm.
