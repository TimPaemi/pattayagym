Common questions about crossfit / functional in Pattaya

Is there CrossFit in Pattaya?

CrossFit Pattaya at The Jungle Gym is the main affiliated CrossFit box. The official CrossFit affiliate ecosystem is smaller in Pattaya than Bangkok or Phuket — confirm current affiliation status before signing up long-term.

How much does CrossFit cost in Pattaya?

Drop-in ฿400–฿600. Monthly unlimited ฿3,500–฿5,500. Beginner fundamentals course typically ฿2,000–฿4,000 for 4–8 sessions.

Do I need experience to start CrossFit?

No — most boxes require new members complete a fundamentals course (1–2 weeks) covering Olympic lifts, gymnastics movements, and pacing before joining regular WODs.
