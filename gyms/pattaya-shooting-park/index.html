Pattaya Shooting Park

Overview

Pattaya Shooting Park is Pattaya's largest outdoor shooting range — a multi-discipline firearms facility on Soi Khao Mai Kaew in East Pattaya offering a diverse menu of pistols, revolvers, shotguns, and rifles to tourists and residents who want to experience supervised live-fire shooting in a controlled environment. The range is one of Pattaya's signature adrenaline tourism activities — alongside the Tower Jump at Pattaya Park, EasyKart go-karting, Thai Sky Adventures skydiving, and Bira Circuit motor racing — appealing to the demographic that wants to try something they can't easily do at home.

The range offers firearms ranging from .22 LR (lightest, ideal for beginners) through .38 Special, 9mm, .45 ACP, and shotguns — with expert instructors guiding every shooter through safety protocols, stance, grip, and target acquisition. Free Pattaya hotel pickup is included in most packages, making logistics simple for tourists who don't want to figure out transport to East Pattaya independently.

For travellers wanting a structured supervised firearms experience with multiple gun options, and especially for first-time shooters who appreciate the patient instruction this range is known for, Pattaya Shooting Park is the city's flagship outdoor shooting venue alongside the indoor competitor Dragon Shooting Club (which has the largest indoor range in Thailand with 20+ shooting lanes).

What "Outdoor Range" Means

Outdoor vs Indoor Differences

Outdoor ranges offer:

  • Variable distances — typically 10-50m for pistols, longer for rifles
  • Shotgun lanes — clay pigeon / skeet possible
  • Natural ambient noise reduction — unlike enclosed indoor ranges
  • Sun-and-weather exposure — tropical heat consideration
  • Larger total facility with multiple lanes / disciplines

Indoor ranges offer:

  • Climate-controlled environment (AC)
  • Standardized lighting
  • Fixed shorter distances typically 7-25m
  • Sound-isolated booths
  • Year-round consistency

For Pattaya tourists, outdoor at Pattaya Shooting Park is closer to the cinematic shooting experience most first-timers expect.

Firearms Available

Caliber Options

.22 LR (.22 Long Rifle)

  • Lightest recoil — ideal for first-time shooters
  • Cheap ammunition — most accessible package
  • Used in: training, plinking, competition
  • Recoil: very mild
  • Suitable for: complete beginners, children under supervision (16+)

9mm

  • Most common pistol caliber globally — military and police standard
  • Moderate recoil — manageable for new shooters with instruction
  • Examples: Glock 17/19, SIG P226, Beretta M9
  • Used in: military, law enforcement, sport shooting

.38 Special / .357 Magnum

  • Revolver classic caliber
  • .38 Special: moderate recoil, simpler operation
  • .357: more powerful, heavier recoil
  • Used in: police service, sport shooting, self-defence

.45 ACP

  • Heavier American military caliber
  • More felt recoil than 9mm
  • Examples: 1911-pattern pistols, Glock 21
  • Tourist favorite — "the .45" has cinematic appeal

Shotguns

  • 12-gauge typically
  • Slug or birdshot depending on range layout
  • Strong recoil — instructor demonstration first
  • Used in: clay pigeon, hunting, defense

Total Inventory

Pattaya Shooting Park typically maintains 20+ different firearms across these categories — meaning visitors can try a varied menu in a single session.

Pricing Packages

Indicative Pricing

Pattaya Shooting Park pricing is package-based:

  • Basic single-firearm package (10-25 rounds): ~฿1,190-1,800
  • Multi-firearm packages (4 guns, 60 total shots): ~฿5,000
  • Premium packages (multiple firearms + extras): $30-$150 USD equivalent
  • Group / corporate / bachelor party packages negotiable

What's Included

  • Range fee
  • Gun rental
  • Ammunition (specific count per package)
  • Targets
  • Eye and ear protection
  • Instructor guidance
  • Hotel pickup in most packages (free)

Safety & Instruction

Mandatory Safety Protocol

Every shooter goes through:

  • Safety briefing — eye/ear protection, range commands, finger discipline
  • Firearm-specific instruction — load, aim, shoot, decock
  • Stance and grip demonstration
  • Trigger finger discipline — finger off trigger except when ready to shoot
  • Range commands — Cease Fire, Range Hot, etc.
  • One-on-one supervision for first-time shooters

Minimum Age

  • 16 years old minimum with adult supervision
  • Adults must be of legal age (18+)
  • No alcohol allowed before shooting
  • Health restrictions — physical or psychological conditions disqualify

Experience Levels

Total Beginner Path

  • Start with .22 LR — minimal recoil to build confidence
  • Progress to 9mm if comfortable
  • Optional .38 Special / .45 ACP for the iconic shot
  • Skip shotgun if recoil-sensitive

Experienced Shooter Path

  • Multiple-firearm packages — try 4-6 guns in single session
  • Long-arm shooting if available (rifle, shotgun)
  • Larger ammunition counts for marksmanship practice
  • Compete in informal scoring with friends

Hotel Pickup Logistics

Free hotel pickup typically includes:

  • Pickup from major Pattaya hotels within stated zone
  • Transfer to Soi Khao Mai Kaew (~25-30 min drive)
  • Wait at range during shooting session
  • Return transfer to hotel
  • Total experience: ~2-3 hours including transit

Pros

  • Largest outdoor shooting range in Pattaya
  • Multiple firearm calibers — .22, .38, .45, 9mm, shotguns, rifles
  • Free hotel pickup included in most packages
  • Expert instructors for safety and technique
  • Multilingual service — English, Thai, Russian, Chinese
  • Flexible package pricing — accommodates budget to premium
  • Outdoor environment — natural setting
  • Shotgun availability — rare at indoor competitors
  • Tourist-friendly logistics — hotel pickup eliminates transport hassle
  • Group / corporate booking flexibility

Cons

  • East Pattaya location — 25-30 min from Walking Street
  • Outdoor heat in dry season midday (March-May)
  • Weather-dependent — heavy rain can pause sessions
  • Premium per-shot pricing vs. domestic shooting in firearm-friendly countries
  • Limited online booking transparency — most bookings via tour operators
  • Strict safety eligibility — health and age requirements
  • Not suitable for nervous shooters — recoil and noise can intimidate

Best For

  • First-time shooters wanting safe supervised introduction
  • Cinema-fans wanting the iconic .45 / shotgun experience
  • Tourist groups — bachelor parties, corporate events
  • Experienced shooters wanting to try Thai range options
  • Couples seeking shared adventure activity
  • Russian / Chinese / English-speaking tourists with multilingual support
  • Visitors with hotel pickup preference
  • Adrenaline tourism stack alongside skydive, kart, tower jump

Not Best For

  • Strict-anti-firearm travellers
  • Children under 16
  • Pregnant or recovering travellers
  • Walking Street walkable preference — this requires transport
  • Pure budget seekers — single-shot tickets minimum ~฿1,200
  • Indoor / AC comfort seekers — try Dragon Shooting Club
