Common questions about muay thai in Pattaya

What is the best Muay Thai gym in Pattaya?

Sityodtong (1959) and Fairtex (1971) are the most decorated heritage camps. Venum Training Camp is the modern flagship. Best choice depends on goal — fight prep, fitness, or beginner intro.

How much does Muay Thai cost in Pattaya?

Drop-in classes ฿300–฿500. Monthly unlimited training ฿4,000–฿15,000. All-inclusive resort camps with accommodation ฿20,000–฿60,000/month.

Do I need experience to train Muay Thai in Pattaya?

No. Most Pattaya camps explicitly welcome beginners and provide all gear. Pad-holders are patient with first-timers; the first 2-3 weeks teach fundamentals.

What should I bring to Muay Thai training?

Hand wraps, mouth guard, water bottle, training clothes. Gloves and pads are usually provided. Most camps sell wraps and mouth guards on site.
