# Jomtien Public Swimming Pool

## Overview

The Jomtien Public Swimming Pool is **the second of Pattaya's two municipal public swimming pools** — located **between South Pattaya and Jomtien Beach**, complementing the **Naklua Public Swimming Pool in north Pattaya**. Together, these two pools provide **the entire municipal public swimming infrastructure** for Pattaya — a city of 300,000+ residents plus millions of annual tourists. The Jomtien location specifically serves **South Pattaya residents, Jomtien Beach community members, and budget-conscious south-Pattaya visitors** who want **affordable swimming without the premium pricing of hotel pools or club memberships**.

Like the Naklua public pool, the Jomtien public pool operates on **standard Thai municipal pool model**: **basic infrastructure, low entry fees, lifeguard supervision, locker rooms with showers, and chlorinated water maintained to public health standards**. The pricing is the same exceptional value as Naklua's pool — typically **฿20-100 per visit** depending on resident status and age — making it **the most affordable formal swimming access in south Pattaya**.

For Jomtien Beach residents (especially those at Baan Suan Lalana, Jomtien condos, View Talay developments), South Pattaya residents, and budget-conscious travelers staying in Jomtien hotels, the public pool serves daily lap swimming, family recreation, and casual cooling-off needs at Thai community pricing.

## Geographic Context — Between South Pattaya and Jomtien

### Location

The pool sits **between South Pattaya proper and Jomtien Beach** — in the **transitional area** that connects the two beach districts:

- **South Pattaya** — the south end of central Pattaya (Walking Street area extending south)
- **Pratamnak Hill** — the elevated ridge between South Pattaya and Jomtien
- **Jomtien Beach** — the long sandy beach south of Pattaya
- **The transitional zone** — where the public pool sits

### Area Characteristics

- **Mix of Thai working-class residential and expat condo developments**
- **Less tourist density** than central Pattaya / Walking Street
- **Quieter pace** than Beach Road
- **Songthaew (baht bus) accessible**
- **Walking distance** from many South Pattaya / Pratamnak / Jomtien developments

### Access from Major Areas

- **From Walking Street:** ~3-5 km south, songthaew or Grab
- **From Jomtien Beach Road:** ~1-2 km north, walking distance from many condos
- **From Pratamnak Hill:** ~1-3 km east, songthaew accessible
- **From central Pattaya hotels:** 10-20 minute songthaew ride

This makes the pool **walking distance from many south-Pattaya residences** but **less convenient for central Pattaya tourist hotels**.

## Pricing

Standard Thai municipal pool pricing:

- **Adult Thai resident:** ~฿20-50 per visit
- **Adult non-resident:** ~฿50-150 per visit
- **Child:** ~฿10-30 per visit
- **Senior discount** typical
- **Locker rental** ~฿10-20

This is **dramatically cheaper than any private pool option** in Pattaya. A daily swim costs less than a single coffee.
