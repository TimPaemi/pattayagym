Kids & youth sport.

Pattaya kids sport means football academies from age 3, trampoline parks stacked at Harbor Mall, and Muay Thai programmes at major camps — nine dedicated venues in our directory plus cross-category options. This guide maps each path for relocating and holiday families without inventing prices or schedules.

Football academies — how Pattaya compares by age

Pattaya youth football is competitive for a resort city — multiple academies, FAT licensing on some programmes, and Russian/English/Thai coaching paths. Pick by entry age, language, and competitive vs developmental intent.

1. AF Academy Football School — youngest starters

AF Academy Football School accepts children from age 3 — youngest in Pattaya football per venue file. HQ 215/18 Moo 6, Na Kluea (Naklua); training also listed in Jomtien, Pratamnak, and Central Pattaya.

Programme emphasis for ages 3–6: ball control, physical development, first team concepts. South Pattaya sessions use outdoor fields; Central Pattaya uses indoor fields — weather flexibility matters in rainy season. Languages: English, Russian, Thai. Phone +66 96 078 8308 · afacademy.pro.

Best for: families with toddlers entering structured football; Russian-speaking households; parents who need a location near their condo belt without cross-city drives every session.

2. FAST PRO Football Academy — premium technical

FAST PRO Football Academy runs UEFA-A licensed European coaching, ages 4–15, at Horseshoe Point and IP Soccer Club locations. Premium technical programme — pair estate day with equestrian guide if siblings split activities.

3. Planet Football (FC Planet) — competitive pathway

Planet Football spans beginner squads through competitive academy and senior team — ages 5+ to adults. Best when your child may outgrow purely recreational training and needs a club structure with match rhythm.

4. Rusich Club Pattaya — Russian-language pathway

Rusich Club Pattaya runs Russian-language HSIF Thailand programme, ages 5–16, after-school rhythm. Fits Pattaya's large Russian-speaking family base — cross-link Russian-speaking sport guide for parent training options.

5. Pattaya City Football Academy — FAT licence

Pattaya City Football Academy holds Bronze-level Academy License (Football Association of Thailand), ages 6–14. Credible pick when you want domestic federation structure rather than expat-only coaching.

Quick pick recap: youngest starters → AF Academy · Russian-speaking family → Rusich · premium European coaching → FAST PRO · competitive pathway → Planet Football · FAT structure → Pattaya City FA.

Harbor Mall stack — trampoline, climb, mall logistics

Harbor Pattaya Mall (Central Pattaya Road) stacks kids activities on upper floors — same building as Deep Climbing Gym (8F).

BOUNCE Pattaya

BOUNCE Pattaya — international franchise, 6F. Free Jump arena, slam dunk lane, dodgeball, performance airbag, Mini BOUNCE for toddlers, BOUNCE FIT adult cardio. Best when you want branded safety standards and mixed-age family sessions.

JumpZ Trampoline Park

JumpZ Trampoline Park — 6F Harbor Mall, AC family sessions listed daily 10:30–19:30 in venue file. Compare pricing and grip socks policy vs BOUNCE on arrival — we do not publish live rates.

Half-day family stack: morning trampoline → lunch in mall → afternoon climb at Deep (older kids) → evening parent Muay Thai while kids rest. Pool add-on: swimming & pools guide · climbing guide.

Muay Thai for kids (cross-category)

Many Muay Thai camps run kids programmes — Fairtex, Venum, Kombat Group routinely train children. Verify age policies on each camp page under Muay Thai category. Safety context: Is Muay Thai safe? · beginner framing: MT beginners.

Not listed under kids-youth but relevant: Tarzan Adventure kids zipline course (Sattahip), EasyKart kids tier on Thepprasit — rest-day adrenaline, not daily training.

International school sport — enrolled students only

Regents International School Pattaya runs basketball, football, swimming, athletics, rugby, and cricket — for enrolled students only, not public drop-in. Relocating families: Pattaya School Guide before you assume school facilities replace academies.

Parent training windows — same city, parallel schedules

Parents training Muay Thai or gym while kids are occupied:

  • Football academy drop-off (90–120 min) → morning pads at nearest camp belt
  • Harbor Mall trampoline block → parent session at central chain gym — day pass guide
  • Weekend Horseshoe football → parent golf or riding sibling activity — equestrian guide

Full playbook: childcare & family pools. Long-stay visas: training & visa guide. Housing: Pattaya Villa.

What to verify before enrolling

  • Trial session cost and refund policy
  • Which field location applies to your condo area (AF multi-site)
  • Coach languages and ratio of kids per coach
  • Insurance, medical form, and concussion policy for contact sports
  • Uniform and equipment list — avoid surprise purchases week one
  • School holiday camp dates vs your visa or rental lease

School holidays and rainy-season planning

International school breaks (Oct, Dec–Jan, Apr) flood football academies — book trials two weeks ahead. Rainy season pushes AF Academy toward Central indoor fields; confirm which location runs when roads flood east. Harbor Mall indoor stack is the reliable wet-weekend default: trampoline → climb → mall cinema for parents.

Split siblings: older child at FAST PRO Horseshoe morning, younger at AF Academy afternoon nearer condo — reduces one-car family stress. Teens wanting MT: trial one kids class at Venum before committing month pass; mouthguard and shin guards are non-negotiable purchases.

Documentation for academies: passport copy, allergy list, emergency contact — standard in Thailand youth sport. Rusich and AF both serve Russian-speaking parents; bring Thai translator only if you need FAT paperwork explained in Thai at Pattaya City FA.

FAQ

What is the youngest age for football in Pattaya?

AF Academy lists from age 3. FAST PRO starts at 4; most others from 5–6.

Best academy for Russian-speaking kids?

Rusich Club for Russian-language pathway; AF Academy also lists Russian among English and Thai.

Can kids climb the same day as trampoline?

Yes at Harbor Mall — climbing guide for Deep on 8F; pace younger kids to avoid overtired sessions before car rides home.

Is kids Muay Thai safe?

Major camps run structured kids classes with pads and supervision — still verify age rules per venue. Read safety guide and observe a class before enrolling.

Where do competitive players train?

Planet Football and Pattaya City FA for pathway/club structure; FAST PRO for elite technical coaching emphasis.

What should parents do during sessions?

Most academies expect drop-off for older kids; toddlers may need parent presence — confirm with AF Academy for ages 3–4.
