# CrossFit Pattaya @ The Jungle Gym

## Overview

CrossFit Pattaya operates inside **The Jungle Gym**, an unusual hybrid facility in Nong Prue that combines a proper CrossFit affiliate with a wildly varied mix of other fitness, sports, and outdoor-skills programming. It's not a pure CrossFit box — it's something closer to a **multi-discipline functional fitness club**: CrossFit + MMA + dodgeball + slacklining + target archery + archery tag + open gym + personal training + nutrition coaching + even DNA testing for fitness profiles. Partnered with **Under Armour** and **Decathlon** for equipment and gear, the facility leans into the "active adult playground" identity rather than a pure box-and-WOD aesthetic.

For travelers and expats wanting a CrossFit affiliate workout in Pattaya, this is the answer — and they'll likely find themselves enjoying an unexpected dodgeball or archery session along the way.

## Position in the Market

Pattaya doesn't have multiple CrossFit boxes the way Bangkok does. Jungle Gym/CrossFit Pattaya is **effectively the only legitimate CrossFit affiliate** in the city, which makes it the default choice for visiting CrossFitters and the local expat fitness community. The multi-discipline programming gives it appeal beyond just CrossFit — families and groups often book the unusual offerings (archery tag, dodgeball) for events.

## Disciplines & Programs

A genuinely eclectic mix:

- **CrossFit** — daily WODs, group classes following CrossFit methodology
- **Personal Training** — 1-on-1 strength, conditioning, fat loss, sport-specific
- **Small Group Training** — semi-private 2-4 person sessions
- **Nutrition Programming** — diet plans tailored to goals
- **DNA Testing** — genetic profile-based training/nutrition recommendations
- **Mixed Martial Arts** — striking + grappling cross-training
- **Self Defense** — practical defense classes
- **Dodgeball** — scheduled group sessions
- **Slacklining** — balance training, fun + functional
- **Target Archery** — traditional archery
- **Archery Tag** — group game / event activity
- **Yoga** — supplementary yoga classes
- **Open Gym** — drop-in self-directed work

This breadth is rare in Pattaya outside the resort camps.

## Schedule

Core scheduled classes (confirmed):

- **Mon-Fri:** 09:00-10:00 (morning) and 19:00-20:00 (evening)
- **Saturday:** 09:00-10:00
- **Open Gym:** extended hours both before and between classes

Specific class day-by-day rotation (CrossFit / yoga / MMA / archery / etc.) is published on the site/Facebook and varies week to week. The class structure is flexible — students typically commit to a small number of weekly classes plus open gym usage.

## Trainers

The lead coach reputation is anchored by **Coach Murray**, repeatedly described in reviews as **knowledgeable, professional, and able to make every class fun and individualized**. The multi-discipline coaching team includes specialists in each area (CrossFit-certified for WODs, MMA coaches for combat, archery instructors, etc.).

English is the primary language of instruction — this is a foreigner-friendly facility.

Specific rates depend on package and discipline mix; the gym uses tiered membership models common in CrossFit (drop-in, weekly, monthly, multi-month). Indicative ranges based on Pattaya CrossFit market:

- **Drop-in CrossFit class:** ~฿400-600

## Facilities

The "Jungle Gym" branding hints at the eclectic nature:

- Full CrossFit-equipped training floor (Olympic platforms, racks, barbells, plates, kettlebells, ropes, rings, GHD, plyo boxes)
- Cardio machines (rowers, bikes, treadmills)
- Dedicated **MMA / mat space**
- **Archery / target range** — unusual amenity
- **Slackline setup** — outdoor functional balance
- **Dodgeball court / open space**
- Showers, lockers, changing rooms
- Pro shop (Under Armour / Decathlon partnerships)

## Accommodation

No on-site accommodation. Located in Nong Prue, a residential area with abundant short-stay condos and apartments at affordable monthly rates (~฿8,000-15,000/month for monthly rentals).

## Location & Getting There

- **Area:** Nong Prue, central-east Pattaya

- 10-15 min by taxi/Bolt from central Pattaya beach areas

- **The only proper CrossFit affiliate in Pattaya** — default destination for traveling CrossFitters
- **Genuinely unique multi-discipline mix** (archery, dodgeball, slacklining alongside CrossFit/MMA)
- Coach Murray strongly reviewed
- Under Armour / Decathlon equipment partnerships
- English-language coaching
- Flexible schedule

- Schedule density is lighter than dedicated big-city CrossFit boxes

- Less polished aesthetic than premium Bangkok CrossFit boxes

## Reputation Summary

Strongly liked by **expats and travelers** who appreciate the multi-discipline approach and Coach Murray's coaching quality. The CrossFit programming is legitimate, the WOD culture is real, and the "extras" (archery, dodgeball, slacklining) make it a genuinely fun facility rather than a grimly serious training environment. **Works best for athletes who don't need a 6-a-day CrossFit class density.**
