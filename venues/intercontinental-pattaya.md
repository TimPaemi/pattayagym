InterContinental Pattaya Resort is the **IHG luxury flagship** on Pratamnak Hill — perched between Pattaya Beach and Jomtien Beach with sweeping bay views, three outdoor pools, the **Amburaya Spa**, a 24-hour fitness centre, tennis courts, and a heritage that traces back to 1971 when the original Royal Cliff complex first put Pratamnak on Pattaya's luxury hospitality map.

For travellers wanting a 5-star fitness + spa + pool day in a quieter, elevated, sea-view setting — and not the louder Beach Road Hilton or the resort-water-park Centara Mirage — InterContinental delivers a more refined, more residential luxury experience. Tennis players in particular have a strong reason to choose IC over its peers (more on this below).

## Position in Pattaya market

The Pattaya 5-star hospitality stack now includes:

- **Hilton Pattaya** — Beach Road central, 16th-floor infinity pool
- **InterContinental Pattaya Resort** — Pratamnak Hill, hilltop, three pools + tennis (this venue)
- **Andaz Pattaya Jomtien Beach (Hyatt)** — Na Jomtien boutique
- **Centara Grand Mirage** — Wong Amat / family Lost-World theme + water park
- **Cape Dara Resort** — Naklua cliffside
- **Mövenpick Siam Hotel Na Jomtien** — Swiss-Accor flagship
- **Royal Cliff (Fitz Club)** — Pratamnak racquet/swim club
- **Dusit Thani Pattaya** — heritage Thai-flagship
- **Holiday Inn Pattaya** — Beach Road mid-luxury

InterContinental's distinctives:

- **Hilltop / sea-view position** with fewer ambient-noise compromises than Beach Road
- **Three outdoor pools** including a serious 25m lap pool — rare in Pattaya
- **Tennis courts** for guests + day visitors
- **Amburaya Spa** — IHG signature spa programme
- **Adjacent to Royal Cliff complex / Fitz Club** — Pratamnak is the densest luxury cluster in Pattaya

## Fitness centre

The 24-hour fitness centre serves hotel guests round the clock with modern cardio, full strength stack, and free weights. Equipment is the typical IHG-spec Life Fitness / Technogym mix, kept in good condition. Personal training bookings available through the spa concierge.

## The pools

Three outdoor pools — the structural distinctive of InterContinental over most of its Pattaya 5-star peers:

1. **25-metre lap pool** — for serious swim training. Most Pattaya hotel pools are sized for lounging; IC's lap pool actually lets you swim laps without flip turns every 8 metres.
2. **Family / leisure pool** — shallower, with day-bed seating around the edge.
3. **Adults-only pool** — quieter, often with cocktail service.

Day-pass access is typically available — call the resort to confirm current rates and availability. Pool butler service is part of the experience.

## Amburaya Spa

Amburaya is IHG's wellness sub-brand, and the Pattaya outpost is one of its larger Asia operations. Treatments span:

- Traditional Thai massage (60 / 90 / 120 min)
- Aromatherapy oil massage
- Body scrubs and wraps using Thai herbs
- Couples treatment rooms
- Multi-step packages (half-day / full-day)
- Hydrotherapy and sauna access

The spa setting overlooks the gardens — quieter than the central pool zone.

## Tennis & racquet sports

This is one of InterContinental's underrated draws. **Tennis courts** are available to hotel guests and day visitors, sitting in the resort's expansive grounds with appropriate buffer from the pool zones. Racquet rental and ball provision available. Pratamnak Hill more broadly is the racquet-sport hub of Pattaya thanks to the adjacent Royal Cliff Fitz Club + Pickleball Pattaya — IC adds tennis to that cluster.

## Other facilities

- **The Cliff** — flagship restaurant with sea views; not a sport amenity, but sets the dining bar
- **Kids' club** + family programs
- **Beach access** — short walk down to Royal Cliff Bay or shuttle to Pattaya Beach
- **Concierge** — multilingual, assists with off-site sport bookings (golf, dive, Muay Thai)

## Pros

- **24-hour fitness** for guests — no after-hours lockout
- **Three pools including a real 25m lap pool** — unusual at Pattaya 5-star tier
- **Tennis courts on-site** — rare among Pattaya hotels
- **Amburaya Spa** — IHG-grade wellness programme
- **Pratamnak Hill setting** — quieter and more residential than Beach Road
- **Sea views** from most public areas
- **Day-pass culture** for non-resident visitors
- **Adjacent to the Royal Cliff / Fitz Club / Pickleball Pattaya cluster** — multi-venue Pratamnak day possible

## Cons

- **Hilltop access** means short Bolt/Grab ride from central Pattaya / Walking Street (5–8 min)
- **Pricing is full 5-star ฿฿฿฿** — day-pass + spa adds up
- **Fitness centre is hotel-spec, not sport-spec** — no power racks, mid-range bumpers
- **Tennis court count is limited** — book ahead, especially in peak season
- **No water park** — families wanting that should choose Centara Grand Mirage instead
- **Limited group classes** vs dedicated boutique studios

## Best for

- Tennis players wanting a luxury-resort court session on Pratamnak Hill
- Lap swimmers — the 25m pool is purpose-built for actual swim training
- Couples on a quiet wellness escape (spa + sea views + Cliff dinner)
- Bangkok weekenders driving down for a 1–2 night reset
- Guests preferring elevated, residential luxury over Beach Road central
- Day-pass visitors stacking IC + Fitz Club + Pickleball Pattaya in one Pratamnak day

## Not best for

- Powerlifters / serious heavy-iron trainees
- Families wanting a water park (Centara Grand Mirage is the right choice)
- Walking Street nightlife-adjacent travellers (IC is residential-quiet, intentionally)
- Budget travellers — pricing is full 5-star

## How to visit

For overnight stays, book through ihg.com or major OTAs. For day-pass: call the resort and ask for the spa/fitness desk; quote "day-pass + spa" and ask what's currently available. For tennis: enquire about court hire and booking lead time separately. For Amburaya Spa: book directly through the spa, ideally 24–48 hours ahead on weekends.

For travellers planning a Pratamnak Hill multi-venue day, pair InterContinental with **Royal Cliff Fitz Club** (members-only racquet/swim/fitness — but day-pass possible), **Pickleball Pattaya** (5 min away), and **Muscle Factory** (the heavy-iron counterpoint). The hill clusters luxury + serious training within 10 minutes of each other.
