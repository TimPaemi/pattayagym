Boxing & kickboxing.

By Tim Paemi · 4 min read · Updated 2026-06-08 · How we rank →

Pattaya search results skew Muay Thai — this guide maps boxing, kickboxing, and hybrid fight gyms where you can train western striking without the clinch.

Pattaya · 157 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

Search "boxing gym Pattaya" and Google mostly returns Muay Thai camps — because in Thailand, striking gyms are Muay Thai by default. Western-style boxing and kickboxing (Dutch rules, K1) exist but are fewer, often inside MMA hybrids or dedicated fight gyms.

This guide separates Muay Thai (elbows, knees, clinch) from boxing / kickboxing (hands-focused or low-kick kickboxing) and lists every Pattaya venue where you can train non-MT striking without guessing from the name.

Muay Thai vs boxing vs kickboxing in Pattaya

  • Muay Thai — 8-point striking, clinch, Thai style. 40+ camps. Best Muay Thai guide · all camps.
  • Western boxing — hands only, ring rounds, head movement. Fewer pure boxing gyms; some MT camps add a boxing coach.
  • Kickboxing / K1 — punches + kicks (often low kick), no clinch. Found at MMA and fight academies.
  • MMA / hybrid — striking + grappling under one roof. BJJ & MMA guide.

Pure and hybrid boxing / kickboxing venues

Pattaya Boxing World — Central Pattaya

Name says boxing — verify current programme mix (boxing-focused pad work vs MT) when you visit. Central location suits short-stay tourists near Beach Road. Pair with Central area guide.

Rage Fight Academy

Fight academy positioning — MMA, striking, and conditioning under fight-team culture. Good if you want kickboxing rounds plus cage work in the same membership block.

Venum Training Camp

Brand-name fight camp — Muay Thai and MMA crossover, international visitor flow. Ask explicitly for boxing-only sessions if you do not want clinch work.

Kombat Group Thailand — East Pattaya

All-inclusive fight resort — Muay Thai, boxing, BJJ, MMA on one campus. Popular for training holidays where you rotate disciplines weekly. Stay-and-train guide.

Battle Conquer Gym — Central

Air-conditioned MT gym with strong foreigner base — pad rounds can be pitched boxing-heavy if you ask the trainer. Not a western boxing gym, but practical for hand-focused tourists.

Fight Evo360

Contemporary combat sports model — performance testing, periodization, analytics-forward coaching. Striking programme mix varies by coach roster; confirm boxing vs MT split before signing a month.

When to pick Muay Thai instead

If you want kickboxing-style training in Pattaya, many visitors are happier at a reputable Muay Thai camp doing pad rounds without clinch — cheaper, more schedule options, easier drop-in. Start at WKO (budget) or Fairtex (resort) and tell the kru "boxing only, no clinch."

Beginners: Muay Thai for beginners · English: English-speaking camps.

Gear and first session

  • 16oz gloves for sparring — camps lend for pad work; buy if you stay 4+ weeks
  • Mouthguard if you do light sparring — non-negotiable
  • Hand wraps — learn to wrap or buy quick wraps at gym shop
  • Running shoes for roadwork — many camps expect morning run

Sample weekly plan (boxing-focused tourist)

Mon/Wed/Fri: morning pad rounds (boxing emphasis) at Central camp · Tue/Thu: strength at Tony's Gym or day-pass chain · Sat: optional light sparring — only when coach approves · Sun: rest or pool recovery.

FAQ — boxing & kickboxing in Pattaya

  • Is there a pure boxing gym in Pattaya? Pure western boxing gyms are rare; most "boxing" signage means Muay Thai or hybrid fight camp. Pattaya Boxing World and fight academies (Rage, Venum) are your first calls — confirm programme on arrival.
  • Can I learn kickboxing as a beginner in Pattaya? Yes — MMA and fight camps run beginner striking classes, and Muay Thai camps will tailor pad work to kickboxing rules if you ask. Best for beginners.
  • How much does boxing training cost in Pattaya? Similar to Muay Thai drop-in economics — single sessions, weekly, and monthly packages. Budget camps are far cheaper than Western cities; resort camps cost more. Check price tier on each venue page; confirm rates directly.
  • Is sparring mandatory? No at reputable camps — pad work and conditioning only is normal for tourists. Sparring requires coach invitation and usually separate liability awareness.
