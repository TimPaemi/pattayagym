# Jomtien Dive Center

## Overview

Jomtien Dive Center is **a PADI 5-Star IDC dive center in Jomtien Beach** holding the **5-Star TripAdvisor Certificate** alongside its PADI affiliation — making it one of **Pattaya's most-credentialed dive operations on the Jomtien side**. With phone **+66 33 144 007** and team leader **Gary** at the helm, the center markets itself as **"the widest selection of dive courses in Pattaya"** — a meaningful claim given its IDC status and ability to certify divers from absolute beginner all the way through professional instructor levels.

For travellers staying in Jomtien (vs. central Pattaya / Beach Road), this is **the closest premium-tier dive center**, eliminating the typical Pattaya-central-vs-Jomtien commute that other operators require.

## Credentials

### PADI 5-Star IDC

The **5-Star IDC (Instructor Development Centre)** designation places Jomtien Dive Center in the **top tier of PADI affiliations** below CDC. IDC certification means:

- Authorized to deliver **PADI Instructor Development Courses** (training new PADI instructors)
- Full **professional-track progression**: Divemaster → Assistant Instructor → Instructor → MSDT
- **Higher service-quality standards** than basic PADI affiliates
- **Equipment standards** maintained to IDC requirements

### 5-Star TripAdvisor Certificate

Independent of PADI's tiering, Jomtien Dive Center holds the **5-Star TripAdvisor Certificate** — reflecting consistent positive customer reviews. The combination of **PADI institutional credentialing + customer-facing TripAdvisor recognition** is the gold standard for dive center quality assessment.

## Courses

As a PADI 5-Star IDC, Jomtien Dive Center offers the **full PADI course progression**:

### Beginner

- **PADI Discover Scuba Diving (DSD)** — non-certification first-time experience
- **PADI Scuba Diver** — 2-day starter (limited depth)
- **PADI Open Water Diver** — full beginner certification

### Intermediate

- **PADI Advanced Open Water** — 5 dives across specialties
- **PADI Specialty Courses** — Nitrox, Deep, Wreck, Buoyancy, Night, etc.
- **EFR (Emergency First Response)** — required prerequisite for Rescue
- **PADI Rescue Diver** — safety-focused certification

### Professional

- **PADI Divemaster** — first professional level
- **Assistant Instructor (AI)**
- **Open Water Scuba Instructor (OWSI)** — full instructor certification (via IDC)

### Tec Diving

Available for technical divers seeking decompression certification.
