Overview

No Limit Divers is the highest-tier dive center in Pattaya for professional and instructor-level training — a PADI 5-Star Instructor Development Center (IDC) led by Gary Phillips, one of the few PADI Platinum Course Directors in Asia. Where most Pattaya dive shops cap out at delivering recreational courses (Open Water through Rescue Diver), No Limit Divers is fully licensed to take students from zero diving experience all the way to certified PADI Open Water Scuba Instructor in-house — a vertical-stack capability that only a handful of dive operations in Thailand match.

The operation runs two fully equipped dive centers in Pattaya/Jomtien with VIP-class boats servicing the offshore reef and wreck sites of the Gulf of Thailand, and offers dedicated multi-week internship programs including a Zero-to-Hero pathway for complete beginners and a fast-track 10-week Divemaster-to-Instructor Pro Plus program for serious career divers.

For travelers who want the most credentialed pro-track diving education in the Pattaya region, who plan to work in the dive industry, or who simply want to be taught by Asia's senior PADI brass, No Limit Divers is the unambiguous top choice.

The 5-Star IDC tier is meaningfully different from a 5-Star Dive Resort or even 5-Star CDC. IDC affiliation means PADI has audited and certified the dive center to train new instructors — a multi-week curriculum culminating in an Instructor Examination (IE) that creates new PADI dive professionals.

To run an IDC, the dive center must have:

  • A Course Director on staff (the only PADI rank empowered to train instructors)
  • Fully equipped multi-classroom training facilities
  • Sufficient boats and dive site access for daily IE-readiness diving
  • Long-standing professional reputation under PADI auditing

Gary Phillips' Platinum Course Director status is one tier above standard Course Director — held by the most experienced and successful instructors globally.

What "Platinum Course Director" Means

PADI awards the Platinum designation annually to Course Directors based on:

  • Number of new PADI Instructors successfully trained (typically 30+ per year minimum)
  • Pass rate of those instructors at PADI Instructor Examinations
  • Length of tenure as Course Director
  • Quality reviews from PADI examiners

Globally, only a small percentage of Course Directors hold Platinum status; in Asia, the count is in the dozens. Gary's Platinum tier means that any student instructor at No Limit Divers is being trained by someone PADI itself recognizes as among the strongest educators in the region.

Dive Site Access

No Limit Divers' fleet of VIP-class boats serves Pattaya's offshore dive sites:

Near Islands (~30-45 min boat ride)

  • Koh Sak, Koh Krok, Koh Lin — beginner-friendly reefs at 8-15m depth
  • Coral gardens with reef fish, anemones, occasional small sharks/rays

Far Islands (~1-1.5 hour ride)

  • Koh Pai (Bamboo Island), Koh Rin — better visibility, larger reef structures
  • Wreck dives including HTMS Khram (former WWII warship sunk as artificial reef in 2003)
  • Suitable for Advanced Open Water and beyond

HTMS Khram

The HTMS Khram wreck is one of the regional standout dive sites — a 74-meter-long Royal Thai Navy decommissioned LSM landing ship sunk near Koh Khram in 2003 as an artificial reef. It sits in 30m of water with the deck at 18-22m, making it accessible to AOW-certified divers.

The wreck has developed extensive coral colonization since 2003 and harbors:

  • Schools of barracuda and snapper
  • Resident grouper, lionfish, scorpionfish
  • Occasional whale sharks (rare but documented)
  • Penetration possibilities for tech-trained divers

Courses & Programs

Recreational Courses

  • PADI Discover Scuba Diving (DSD) — half-day, no certification, just a taste
  • PADI Open Water Diver — 3-4 days, full certification to 18m
  • PADI Advanced Open Water — 2 days, 5 specialty dives, certification to 30m
  • PADI Rescue Diver — 3 days, emergency response training
  • PADI Specialty courses — Wreck, Deep, Nitrox, Drift, Underwater Photography, etc.

Professional Pathways

This is where No Limit Divers separates from competitors:

PADI Divemaster Internship

A multi-week residential program that takes a Rescue-certified diver through:

  • Unlimited diving during the internship
  • Real-world dive leadership training
  • Workshops + skills development
  • Mentorship under Platinum Course Director Gary Phillips
  • Final certification as PADI Divemaster — entry-level dive professional

PADI Zero-to-Hero Instructor Internship

A complete-novice-to-instructor pathway designed for those starting with little or no dive experience:

  • Begins with PADI Open Water Diver course
  • Progresses through Advanced Open Water
  • Continues through Rescue Diver
  • Includes Divemaster certification
  • Concludes with the Instructor Development Course (IDC) and Instructor Examination (IE)
  • Total program: typically 8-12 weeks

PADI Pro Plus Program (10-Week DM-to-Instructor)

A fast-track professional pathway for divers who already have Rescue certification:

  • 10 weeks total
  • Combines DM internship + IDC + IE prep
  • Graduates as PADI Open Water Scuba Instructor (OWSI)
  • Industry-ready credential

Continuing Education

  • PADI Master Scuba Diver — recreational topcap
  • Specialty Instructor courses — for instructors expanding teaching range
  • Course Director preparation — for instructors aspiring to next level
