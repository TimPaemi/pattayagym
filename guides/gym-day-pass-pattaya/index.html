Gym day-pass.

By TimPaemi · 6 min read · Updated 2026-06-02 · How we rank →

You do not need a monthly membership to train in Pattaya. Most serious gyms accept day and weekly passes — here is where to walk in and what to expect.

Pattaya · 157 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

Day-pass gyms are the saver for short Pattaya trips. If you're here for 3–14 days, joining a gym on a monthly plan is wasteful — most reputable gyms accept walk-in day passes for ฿200–500, and weekly passes for ฿800–2,500.

This guide covers fitness venues in Pattaya with a transparent day-pass option. Pricing tiers are listed below. All hand-checked from our 157-venue Pattaya.Gym directory — confirm on the venue page before you travel.

Hub: 24-hour gyms · cheapest gyms · compare · plan my trip.

1. Anytime Fitness Pattaya — 24/7 day pass

Anytime Fitness Pattaya has the global Anytime Fitness day-pass policy: roughly ฿500/day or ฿2,000/week. Open 24/7 (key fob entry after hours). Two branches: Bukis Point (South Pattaya) and Sukhumvit-Pattaya. Cardio, weights, group classes. The reliable choice if you're already a global Anytime member — your home-club key fob may work.

2. Jetts Fitness Pattaya — drop-in day pass

Jetts Fitness Pattaya at Mike Shopping Mall is a 24-hour gym from the Jetts Australian chain. Day passes around ฿400. Weekly ~฿1,500. Full equipment, mostly cardio + free weights + plate-loaded machines. No pool. Central Pattaya — walking distance from Beach Road.

3. Fitness 7 — long-running Pattaya local

Fitness 7 is one of Pattaya's older locally-owned gyms. Day passes ฿200–300 (cheaper than the chains). Full free-weight room, plate-loaded machines, basic cardio. Less polished than Jetts/Anytime but better value if you're cost-conscious.

4. Muscle Factory Pattaya — bodybuilding-focused day pass

Muscle Factory is the hardcore bodybuilding gym in Pattaya. Day pass ~฿300. Mon–Fri 07:00–24:00, Sat–Sun 07:00–23:00. Heavy iron, plate-loaded everything. Not for cardio — for serious lifting. Foreigner-friendly but the regulars are competitive bodybuilders. Area context: Naklua & Pratamnak.

5. Castra Gym — modern day-pass option

Castra Gym is a newer Pattaya fitness venue with a transparent drop-in option. Mixed equipment — strength + cardio + functional. Good for travellers who want a non-chain experience.

6. Universe Gym — old-school day-pass friendly

Universe Gym is another long-running Pattaya local. ฿200–300 day passes. Strong free-weight selection, less cardio focus. Mostly Thai bodybuilders + foreign regulars. Soi Buakhao belt — Central guide.

7. Tony's Gym — central, basic, very cheap

Tony's Gym in Central Pattaya is the cheapest day-pass option in the city (~฿100–150). No frills, no AC, basic equipment, but the price is unbeatable if you just need somewhere to lift for a session.

8. Elite Gym & Fitness Pattaya — mid-tier day pass

Elite Gym & Fitness accepts walk-ins around ฿300–400/day. Well-equipped, AC, mixed Thai/foreign crowd. Reliable middle-of-road choice.

9. Platinum Fitness — Russian-friendly day pass

Platinum Fitness accepts drop-ins; Russian-speaking front desk is common. Good for visitors from Russian-speaking countries who want a gym without language friction. See Russian-speaking sport.

10. Manhattan Pattaya Fitness — Pattaya local with day-pass

Manhattan Pattaya Fitness has day-pass options at standard local-gym pricing. Solid equipment, popular with long-stay expats.

11. Fitz Club — premium day-pass (hotel guest pricing)

Fitz Club at Royal Cliff Hotels Group on Pratamnak Hill offers day passes for non-hotel guests at premium pricing (฿1,000+/day). What you get: tennis courts, squash, swimming pool, gym, table tennis. Worth it for a one-day premium-club experience. Full tier: luxury sports clubs.

12. Hilton Pattaya — hotel guest day-pass

Hilton Pattaya day passes are typically reserved for hotel guests but can be arranged for outside visitors at premium pricing. 5-star equipment, spa access, pool, sauna. Worth it for a luxury one-off; not the everyday choice.

How to actually pick a day-pass gym

  • For 1–3 days: Tony's Gym (cheapest) or Jetts (most convenient location)
  • For 1–2 weeks: Anytime Fitness weekly pass or Fitness 7
  • Hardcore lifting: Muscle Factory
  • Multiple sports in one day: Fitz Club
  • 24-hour access: Anytime or Jetts
  • Muay Thai + weights same trip: morning pads at ranked camp, afternoon iron day pass

First visit checklist

  1. Carry cash — many front desks prefer baht notes for day passes.
  2. Passport copy for chain registration (Anytime, Jetts) on first visit only.
  3. Towel + water bottle — budget gyms may not supply.
  4. Ask if bags can stay at desk — locker policies vary.
  5. Photograph the price board — disputes are rare but proof helps.

Phrase help at the desk: Thai gym terms — rai wan (daily), ra kha tao rai (how much?).

FAQ

How much does a gym day pass cost in Pattaya?

Range is ฿100–1,000+ depending on tier. Budget local gyms (Tony's, Fitness 7, Universe): ฿100–300. Mid-tier (Anytime, Jetts, Elite): ฿300–500. Premium club tier (Fitz Club, Hilton): ฿1,000+. Most day passes are paid at the front desk on arrival.

Do I need to bring anything besides money?

Training clothes, athletic shoes, towel, water bottle. Gloves/wraps if you plan bag work. Small padlock useful. Passport may be requested at chain gyms for first-time registration.

Do Pattaya gyms have steam room / sauna?

Premium clubs (Fitz, Hilton, Andaz) yes. Mid-tier chains (Anytime, Jetts) usually not. Local gyms generally no. Battle Conquer (Muay Thai) has sauna and ice plunge.

What time do gyms open?

24-hour: Anytime Fitness, Jetts. Daily 06:00–23:00: most local gyms. Hotel gyms: typically 06:00–22:00 for non-guests. Confirm on each venue page.

Can I buy weekly or monthly passes without a contract?

Yes at most gyms. Weekly: ฿800–2,500 depending on tier. Monthly without contract: ฿1,500–4,500 budget tier, ฿3,500–8,000 chain tier.

Are there women-only sessions or female trainers?

Pattaya gyms are not gender-segregated by default. Most chain gyms have female PT on request. See female-friendly gyms for ranked picks.

Muay Thai day sessions vs fitness day pass

Many tourists buy a fitness gym day pass and a separate pad round package at a Muay Thai gym same day — totally normal. Lifting heavy before pads is a mistake; sequence: MT first, iron after, or split AM/PM. Battle Conquer bundles sauna recovery useful between.

Tourist district traps

Beach Road touts may promise "gym + pool" packages that are spa scams — walk into listed venues on Pattaya.Gym only. Price boards should be visible; if not, ask ra kha tao rai before changing clothes.

Weekly pass math for tourists

Seven day passes at ฿400 = ฿2,800; weekly pass at Jetts ~฿1,500 — buy weekly on day two if you liked day one. Anytime global members: confirm home-club reciprocity before assuming free entry. Lost key fob replacement fees apply — report same day.

Photograph the locker room before you train — some budget gyms have no CCTV; use a small padlock on day-pass visits even if staff say optional.

Hotel guests: ask concierge for gym partnership discounts — some Beach Road towers negotiate Jetts or Fitness 7 day rates below walk-in board price.
