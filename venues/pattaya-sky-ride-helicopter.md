# Pattaya Sky Ride — Helicopter Tours

## Overview

Pattaya Sky Ride is **the city's primary helicopter tour and charter operator** — offering **aerial sightseeing tours over Pattaya beaches, city landmarks, and temples** alongside **fast Bangkok-to-Pattaya charter transfers** for travellers who want to skip the 2-3 hour ground transit. The fleet includes **Airbus EC-135 (up to 5 passengers) and Airbus EC-130 (up to 6 passengers)** aircraft — premium twin-engine helicopters with **spacious leather seats, air conditioning, and headphones with microphones for pilot communication during flight**.

Helicopter tourism is **the apex aerial-experience tier** in Pattaya — above Pattaya Park's 56th-floor Tower Jump zip line, above Tarzan Adventure's 25-platform jungle zipline, and above Flight of the Gibbon's 3 km canopy course. While **Thai Sky Adventures' tandem skydive (13,000 ft from a fixed-wing aircraft)** delivers **the more visceral aerial thrill**, helicopter tours deliver **the more sustained sightseeing experience** — looking down on Pattaya Bay, the Sanctuary of Truth, the offshore islands, and the skyline at leisure rather than during a 60-second freefall.

The **Bangkok-to-Pattaya charter** is genuinely useful for premium travellers — at **129,200 THB for the 45-minute flight (landing at Royal Cliff Beach Pattaya)**, it's expensive but converts the typical 2.5-3 hour Bangkok-Pattaya ground transit into a sub-hour aerial experience with photographic memories of central Thailand from the air.

## The Aircraft

### Airbus EC-135

- **Twin-engine** light utility helicopter
- **Up to 5 passengers** plus pilot
- **Cruise speed:** ~250 km/h
- **Range:** ~600 km
- **Used globally for** EMS, police, executive transport, sightseeing
- **Premium safety record**

### Airbus EC-130

- **Single-engine** light utility helicopter
- **Up to 6 passengers** plus pilot
- **Cruise speed:** ~240 km/h
- **Range:** ~600 km
- **Used globally for** sightseeing, executive transport
- **Spacious cabin** for the size class

Both aircraft are **premium-tier sightseeing helicopters** — meaningfully different from older / smaller helicopters used by budget operators globally.

### Cabin Amenities

- **Leather seats** (spacious vs. military / utility helicopter seating)
- **Air conditioning** (mandatory for tropical Thailand)
- **Headphones + microphone** for pilot / passenger communication
- **Large windows** for photography
- **Multi-language pilot commentary** typically

## Tour Types

### Pattaya Aerial Sightseeing

Standard tours typically include:

#### Pattaya Coastline

- **Pattaya Beach** from above
- **Walking Street** aerial perspective (less recognizable from air than ground)
- **Beach Road / 2nd Road / Sukhumvit** street grid
- **Sea-side hotels** including Hilton, Royal Cliff, Andaz
- **Bali Hai Pier** with dive boats and ferries

#### Offshore Islands

- **Koh Larn (Coral Island)** — visible from short flight
- **Koh Sak / Koh Krok** — smaller near islands
- **Far island silhouettes** if extending tour

#### Sanctuary of Truth

- **The famous wooden temple** at Naklua
- **Aerial photography opportunity** — different perspective from ground tours
- **Architectural detail** visible

#### Buddha Mountain (Khao Chi Chan)

- **The 109m gold-leaf cliff Buddha**
- **Aerial perspective** showing scale
- **Surrounding Sattahip landscape**

#### Other Landmarks

- **Pattaya Park Tower** (the 56-floor building with the Tower Jump zip line)
- **Royal Cliff Hotels Group** complex
- **Mabprachan Reservoir**
- **Khao Phra Tamnak (Buddha Hill / Pratamnak)**

### Bangkok-to-Pattaya Charter

The premium transfer service:

- **Bangkok departure** (typically Don Mueang or central Bangkok helipad)
- **45-minute flight time** to Pattaya
- **Landing at Royal Cliff Beach Pattaya** (premium hotel partner)
- **Door-to-door** transport with hotel coordination
- **Avoid Bangkok-Pattaya highway traffic**
- **Cost:** 129,200 THB per charter (entire helicopter, up to 5-6 passengers)

For groups of 5-6 sharing the cost, this is **~21,500-26,000 THB per person** — premium but possibly justifiable for time-pressed corporate travellers vs. dealing with traffic.

### Custom Charters

- **Custom routing** — fly anywhere within helicopter range
- **Multi-stop tours** combining Pattaya + Hua Hin + offshore islands
- **Aerial photography** for events (weddings, corporate)

## Safety

### Helicopter Tour Safety

Modern commercial helicopter operations have **excellent safety records**:

- **Twin-engine EC-135** — engine-out backup capability
- **Pre-flight inspections** routine
- **Pilot certification** to ICAO standards
- **Weather grounding** if conditions unsafe
- **Maintenance** to manufacturer schedules
- **Insurance** included in charter pricing

### Restrictions

- **Pregnant passengers** typically excluded
- **Severe heart conditions** — consult physician
- **Weight limits** applied per passenger and aircraft total
- **Closed-toe shoes** typically required
- **No smoking** in or near aircraft
- **Camera / phone use** OK during flight
