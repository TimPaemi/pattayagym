Common questions

What is the cheapest gym in Pattaya?

Free public running routes (Pattaya Beach, Mabprachan Lake) cost nothing. The cheapest membership gyms run ฿1,500-฿2,500/month — Castra Gym, Universe Gym, and Tony's Gym are typical.

Are there free workout spots in Pattaya?

Yes — Pattaya Beach 5.8km path, Jomtien Beach, Mabprachan Lake 4km loop, Pratumnak Park, and Chaiyapruek 400m track are all free public spaces with running and outdoor calisthenics.

Can I train Muay Thai cheaply in Pattaya?

Yes. WKO/ISS Gym at ~฿4,000/month is among the cheapest verified Muay Thai monthly rates with serious coaching. Drop-in classes at most camps run ฿300-฿500.
