★ About this venue

Dragon Shooting Club Pattaya

Overview

Dragon Shooting Club is the largest indoor shooting range in Thailand — a world-class facility in Pattaya with 20 standard shooting lanes plus 3 private VIP lanes for large groups, offering an extensive arsenal of the latest firearms ranging from .22 LR through 9mm, .45 ACP, .357 Magnum, and shotguns. The fully air-conditioned environment gives Dragon Shooting Club a meaningful comfort advantage over outdoor competitors (Pattaya Shooting Park) in Thailand's tropical heat, especially during the March-May dry season when outdoor ranges become uncomfortable midday.

The 20-lane scale is genuinely substantial — most Thai indoor ranges have 4-10 lanes, putting Dragon in the regional top tier alongside major Bangkok facilities. The 3 dedicated VIP lanes allow bachelor parties, corporate events, and family groups to shoot together in a private environment without competing with general-public schedule conflicts.

For Pattaya residents and visitors who want a controlled climate, modern firearms inventory, and group-event capacity for shooting experiences, Dragon Shooting Club is the apex indoor option in the city.

The 20-Lane Scale

Why 20+ Lanes Matters

Most indoor shooting ranges globally:

  • 2-4 lanes at small ranges
  • 6-10 lanes at mid-tier ranges
  • 15-25 lanes at major commercial ranges
  • 30+ lanes at military / police training facilities
Dragon's 20 standard + 3 private = 23 total lanes puts it in the major commercial range tier — a meaningful infrastructure investment.

What 20 Lanes Enables

  • High concurrent capacity — multiple groups simultaneously
  • Less waiting during peak tourist times
  • League / tournament hosting capability
  • Different stations for different calibers — pistol vs. shotgun zones
  • Training programs alongside walk-in tourist shooting

The 3 Private VIP Lanes

What VIP Lane Access Provides

  • Soundproofed dedicated space
  • No mixing with general public
  • Group privacy for bachelor parties, corporate events
  • Exclusive instructor during the booking window
  • Custom package menus — specific firearms, target setups
  • Photo / video privileges (typically restricted in public lanes)

Why It's Distinctive

Most Pattaya shooting venues offer only public-lane shooting — meaning groups share floor with strangers. Dragon's VIP option allows a private experience for celebrations, team events, or family groups at a premium tier.

Firearm Inventory

Standard Calibers Available

  • .22 LR — beginner-friendly, low recoil
  • 9mm — police/military standard
  • .45 ACP — heavier American caliber
  • .357 Magnum — revolver classic, strong recoil
  • Shotguns — typically 12-gauge

What "Latest Firearms" Means

Dragon markets the latest models — meaning recently-manufactured firearms rather than vintage stock:

  • Modern Glock pistols (Gen 4/5)
  • SIG Sauer P226/P320
  • Beretta M9 / 92FS
  • 1911-style pistols for .45 ACP
  • Smith & Wesson revolvers for .357
  • Modern semi-auto shotguns
This contrasts with some Pattaya ranges using older inventory.

Pricing

Indicative ranges:

  • Single-caliber package (10-25 rounds): ~฿1,200-1,800
  • Multi-firearm packages (4 guns): ~฿4,000-6,000
  • VIP private lane bookings: premium tier — typically ฿5,000-15,000+ per group
  • Group rate negotiable

Safety & Instruction

Standard shooting range protocols:

  • Mandatory safety briefing before shooting
  • Eye and ear protection provided
  • Instructor 1-on-1 for first-time shooters
  • Range officer monitoring all lanes
  • Strict firearm handling rules
  • No alcohol before shooting
  • Minimum age 16 with adult supervision
