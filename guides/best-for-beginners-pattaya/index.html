Common questions

What sport is easiest to start in Pattaya?

Yoga, swimming, and running require no prior skill. For combat sports, most Muay Thai camps welcome total beginners. For watersports, scuba (open-water course in 3-4 days) and SUP are popular entry points.

Do I need equipment to start a sport in Pattaya?

Most venues provide all equipment for beginners — Muay Thai gloves and pads, dive gear, paddles for pickleball, racquets at hotel courts. Bring workout clothes and water.

Are Pattaya gyms beginner-friendly to women?

Many — Anytime Fitness, hotel gyms (Hilton, Andaz, Mövenpick), boutique yoga studios, and Fitz Club all rank well on female-friendly safety and comfort. The big bro-coded iron gyms are the exception, not the rule.
