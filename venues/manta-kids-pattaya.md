# Manta Kids Pattaya (IG Center)

## Overview

Manta Kids Pattaya is **the most institutionally credentialed children's swim academy in the city** — a Swim Australia / ASCTA-affiliated school inside the **IG Center** complex on Chaiyaphruek 2 Road in East Pattaya. The academy teaches **babies from 4 months old up to children aged 12**, with a structured three-program curriculum (Splash → Swirl → Swim) that progresses from in-water familiarization for infants all the way to formal competitive five-stroke technique for school-age kids.

The headline credential is rare in Pattaya: **all instructors are certified by the ASCTA — Australia Swimming Coach & Teacher Association** — one of the world's most respected swim-coaching governing bodies. The **head coach is a former Thai national swimmer**, combining international certification with national-team competitive pedigree. For families wanting **the most credentialed swim instruction available in Pattaya**, this is the answer.

## The Three Programs

Manta Kids structures its curriculum into **three age-banded programs**, each building on the next:

### Splash — Ages 4-18 months

The infant program. Begins with **breath-holding** (a learned reflex) and **water familiarization**.

- Class duration: **30 minutes**
- Format: **1 parent per 1 child**
- Maximum: **8 parent-child pairs per class**
- Frequency: 1 class/week
- Term: 10 weeks + 1 Safety Week (11 weeks total)

This is the **earliest swim instruction available in Pattaya** and is foundational for water safety. Babies develop comfort, breath control, and basic floating responses before they can walk.

### Swirl — Ages 4 months to 3.5 years

The toddler program. Emphasis shifts to **survival swimming and propulsion**.

- Class duration: **30 minutes**
- Format: **1 parent per 1 child**
- Maximum: **8 pairs per class**
- Curriculum focus: floating on back, kicking, breath holding under water, basic forward propulsion, getting to wall safely

By the end of the Swirl progression, kids should be able to **enter water, surface, and reach safety on their own** — the core "survival swimming" outcome.

### Swim — Ages 3.5 to 12 years

The formal/competitive program. Teaches the **five strokes** of competitive swimming:

1. **Freestyle (Front Crawl)**
2. **Backstroke**
3. **Breaststroke**
4. **Butterfly**
5. **Survival Backstroke**

- Class duration: **50 minutes**
- Format: **No parent needed** (kids work directly with instructor)
- Maximum: **8 kids per class**
- Curriculum focus: correct stroke mechanics, swimming form similar to professional swimming athletes, water safety, distance swimming, breath timing

This is **proper technical swim instruction**, not splash-around play. Kids who progress through the Swim program emerge with **competition-grade stroke mechanics**.

### Safety Week — Built Into Every Term

Each 10-week term includes an **11th "Safety Week"** focused entirely on **water safety education for child and parent**:

- Pool safety rules
- What to do if you fall in fully clothed
- How to help a friend who's struggling (without entering the water yourself)
- Recognising water emergencies
- Parental supervision practices

This integration of safety education into the curriculum is what distinguishes a credentialed swim school from a generic swim teacher.
