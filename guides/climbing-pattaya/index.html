Climbing in Pattaya.

By Tim Paemi · 7 min read · Updated 2026-05-31 · How we rank →

Pattaya climbing means two real indoor gyms — Deep at Harbor Mall north and Bean Cow on the Darkside — plus hotel adventure walls that are not the same sport. This guide maps both gyms, who each suits, and family-friendly options.

Pattaya · 158 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

Pattaya has two dedicated indoor climbing gyms — Deep Climbing Gym at Harbor Mall (North Pattaya) and Bean Cow in Huay Yai (East Pattaya). Hotel rock walls at Centara Grand Mirage and resort adventure parks are not the same thing. This guide covers real climbing walls, who each gym suits, and how to pair bouldering with Muay Thai or beach recovery.

Hub: Climbing category · Naklua guide · East Pattaya guide · compare · plan my trip.

Deep Climbing Gym — Harbor Pattaya (North Pattaya)

Deep Climbing Gym on the 8th floor of Harbor Pattaya Mall is Pattaya's first purpose-built indoor wall — air-conditioned, 10m height, ocean-themed setting. Top rope, auto-belay, lead, and bouldering routes. Mon–Fri 10:30–19:00, Sat–Sun 10:30–19:30. Phone +66 89 332 1000.

Best for: tourists staying Naklua or North Pattaya who want a mall-adjacent session between pad rounds; beginners on intro top-rope; climbers escaping midday heat. Pair with Fairtex or Sityodtong for a north-side training block.

Bean Cow Climbing Gym — Huay Yai (East Pattaya)

Bean Cow Climbing Gym & Community Center (61/23 Moo 1, Huay Yai) runs an 8m wall with top rope and lead, CWA-certified teachers, belayer services, and top-rope safety classes. Family-friendly positioning — useful if kids climb while parents train nearby at Kombat Group or Jungle Gym CrossFit.

Best for: long-stay families on the Darkside; climbers who want structured instruction; East Pattaya residents avoiding the Harbor Mall taxi. Verify current hours on beancowclimbing.com before you drive.

Deep vs Bean Cow — quick pick

  • Staying Beach Road / Naklua? → Deep at Harbor Mall — easier reach, longer wall, AC mall parking.
  • Staying Darkside / Huay Yai? → Bean Cow — less traffic, CWA instruction, community vibe.
  • First time climbing? → Either gym runs intro sessions; Deep for mall convenience, Bean Cow for family classes.
  • Lead climber? → Both list lead routes — confirm current setting and certification requirements on-site.

Not real climbing walls (but useful)

  • Centara Grand Mirage — hotel rock wall + water park; resort guest experience, not a climbing gym.
  • Tarzan Adventure — jungle zipline and adventure park south at Sattahip; outdoor thrills, not route climbing. Sattahip guide.
  • CrossFit Pattaya @ Jungle Gym — slacklining on the same campus; grip and balance cross-training.

Sample weekly rhythm (climb + Muay Thai)

Mon/Wed/Fri: morning pads at north or east camp · Tue/Thu: Deep or Bean Cow 90-min session · Sat: open climb or yoga for finger recovery · Sun: rest or beach walk Jomtien.

Family days: morning climb Harbor Mall → afternoon hotel pool day pass. Injury backup: Pattaya Medical.
