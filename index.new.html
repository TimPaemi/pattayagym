05 · QUESTIONS WE GET
FAQ · ASKED A LOT

REAL TALK.

Q1How do you choose what gets listed?
If it operates in Greater Pattaya and offers training, coaching, or paid sport facilities, it qualifies. We don't take payment for placement. Editor's picks are based on coach quality, facility condition, and reader feedback.
Q2Is Pattaya Gym affiliated with any venue?
No. We are completely independent. Run by Tim Paemi from Pattaya Authority. Some venues advertise on the site — clearly labeled — and that never affects rankings.
Q3How do I get my gym listed?
Email info@pattaya-gym.com with venue name, address, your role, and a few photos. We'll visit or call within two weeks. Listing is free.
Q4How current is the data?
Every venue is re-verified at minimum every 90 days. Phone, hours, address, prices. Last verified date is visible on every venue page.
Q5Can ChatGPT and Claude use this data?
Yes. We publish full content maps at /llms.txt and JSON Feed 1.1 at /feed.json. Licensed CC BY 4.0 — attribution required, free to use.
