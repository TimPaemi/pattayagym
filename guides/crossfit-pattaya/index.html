CrossFit Pattaya.

By Tim Paemi · 7 min read · Updated 2026-05-31 · How we rank →

Pattaya has one CrossFit affiliate — Jungle Gym on the Darkside — plus chains, iron gyms, and combat conditioning if you train elsewhere. This guide maps both the affiliate and realistic alternatives.

Pattaya · 158 venues hand-checked · Compare gyms

Pattaya has exactly one CrossFit affiliate — CrossFit Pattaya @ Jungle Gym in Nong Prue (East Pattaya / Darkside). That does not mean functional fitness stops there: hotel gyms, combat S&C programmes, and outdoor bootcamps fill the gap. This guide covers the affiliate plus realistic alternatives if you are not on the Darkside.

Hub: CrossFit category · East Pattaya guide · compare · plan my trip.

CrossFit Pattaya @ Jungle Gym — the affiliate

CrossFit Pattaya @ Jungle Gym (165/4 Moo 6, Nong Prue) is Pattaya's only registered CrossFit affiliate. Scheduled classes Mon–Fri 09:00–10:00 and 19:00–20:00, Sat 09:00. Coach Murray leads programming; Under Armour and Decathlon partnerships on-site.

Same site also runs MMA, archery, dodgeball, and slacklining — useful if your household splits interests. Pair with east-side Muay Thai at Kombat Group or Sor Klinmee for a fight-camp block with structured WODs on rest days.

Who should base on the Darkside for CrossFit

  • Long-stay functional fitness — monthly class packs beat taxiing from Beach Road daily.
  • Fight campers — stack Jungle Gym WODs with MT at Kombat or Rambaa M16.
  • Families with older kids — archery and multi-sport on one campus.

Who should not: short central-Pattaya holidays needing Walking Street walkability — use Central gyms or chains instead.

Alternatives when you cannot reach Nong Prue

  • 24-hour chains — Jetts, Anytime for racks, rowers, and dumbbells. 24-hour gyms guide.
  • Iron gyms — Muscle Factory (Pratamnak), Tony's Gym (central) for pure strength blocks.
  • Combat S&C — Venum, Rage Fight Academy run conditioning classes. BJJ & MMA guide.
  • Hotel gym day pass — luxury sports clubs for Technogym + pool recovery.

Sample weekly rhythm (CrossFit + Muay Thai)

Mon/Wed/Fri: morning MT pads at east camp · Tue/Thu: Jungle Gym 19:00 WOD · Sat: open gym or beach run Jomtien · Sun: rest or yoga — yoga retreat guide.
